## Notes

[[Stunning Blow\|Stunning Blow]] (Deadly Strike) is the [[Monk\|Monk]]'s ability to land a perfectly-placed weaponless attack that stuns the target — and may even kill outright through mystic precision. It triggers when the unmodified attack roll is 5 higher than the required to-hit number.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Trigger:** Weaponless attack roll is 5 higher than the required to-hit number (use UNMODIFIED die roll — no bonuses count toward this threshold)
- **Stun Effect:** Target is stunned for 1d6 rounds
- **Death Effect:** 5% chance to kill outright, ONLY if the target's hit dice are no more than 1 higher than the monk's
- **Mechanics:** Built into the [[Open Hand Damage\|weaponless attack]] — no separate roll
