## Notes

Spells have one, two, or three **components**, listed in the spell description as **V**, **S**, and/or **M**.

## Key Facts

**V — Verbal:**
- Must speak a mystical incantation aloud
- Cannot be whispered
- Cannot cast a verbal-component spell if affected by [[Silence, 15ft Radius\|Silence, 15ft Radius]] or gagged

**S — Somatic:**
- Magical gestures
- Requires free use of at least one hand
- Some non-humanoid spellcasting creatures may use other appendages (tentacles, etc.); humans and demi-humans must use hands

**M — Material:**
- Requires a physical object
- GM decides which objects are appropriate (the spell description tells you when there's a gp cost)
- Casting may destroy the material component
- Common minor components don't usually need tracking unless a gp cost is listed
