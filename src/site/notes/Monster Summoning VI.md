## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** 80ft
- **Duration:** 7 rounds + 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Summoned creatures
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By casting this spell, the caster conjures up 1d2 monsters to serve them as allies in combat or to perform other services.
