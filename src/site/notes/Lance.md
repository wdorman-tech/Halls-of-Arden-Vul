## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee (Reach)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d6+1 vs S/M, 2d8 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 8
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 10ft
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Weight:** 10 lb
- **Cost:** 7 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A long mounted weapon. Inflicts **double damage** when used by a character riding a charging heavy warhorse or similar steed. If the attacker is on a normal riding or cavalry horse, the GM may reduce or remove this charge bonus.

When used dismounted, the lance is a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]] and inflicts **double damage** when [[Set Weapon Against Charge\|set against a charge]].

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (piercing).
