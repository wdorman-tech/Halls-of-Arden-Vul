## Stats

- **Cost:** 1 gp per 50 ft
- **Weight:** 10 lb per 50 ft
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1 per coil

## Notes

A 50-foot coil of hemp rope. Standard adventuring rope, useful for climbing, lowering pack, securing prisoners, or — paired with a [[Grappling Hook\|Grappling Hook]] — assaulting walls.

[[Rope, Silk\|Silk rope]] is lighter but ten times the price.
