## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Divination\|Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** Path 20ft wide, and 20ft per druid level in length
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows you to detect the presence of a specific type of animal you choose to locate.

## Details

You must concentrate for a full round in a particular direction in order to gain the insight granted by the spell.

The divination is not blocked by any intervening substance, of any thickness, so it can "see" through walls, thick growths of trees, and into burrows.
