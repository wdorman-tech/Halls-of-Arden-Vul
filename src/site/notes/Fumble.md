## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (Lessened effect)

## Notes

This enchantment causes intense clumsiness in the affected creature. Attempting to run will result in an immediate fall to a prone position, and anything held in hand (or claw, etc.) will be dropped.

## Details

A successful [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] allows the affected creature to act as if under the influence of a [[Slow\|Slow]] spell in order to avoid the spell's full effects.
