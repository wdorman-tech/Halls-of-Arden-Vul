## Notes

**Abjuration** spells protect a creature, object, or place. They negate harmful effects, banish creatures from other planes, and create magical barriers and impediments.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Abjuration
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Abjuration
- **Examples:** [[Protection From Evil\|Protection from Evil]], [[Protection from Evil 10ft Radius\|Protection from Evil 10ft Radius]], [[Dispel Evil\|Dispel Evil]], [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]], [[Anti-Magic Shell\|Anti-Magic Shell]], [[Globe of Invulnerability\|Globe of Invulnerability]], [[Banishment\|Banishment]], [[Sanctuary\|Sanctuary]], [[Resist Cold\|Resist Cold]], [[Resist Fire\|Resist Fire]]
