## Stats

- **Value:** 1/100 of a [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]]
- **Weight:** 0.1 lb (10 coins = 1 lb)
- **Encumbrance Slots:** negligible per coin; large quantities are handled by total weight

## Exchange

- 100 cp = 1 [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]]
- 10 cp = 1 [[Silver Piece\|Silver Piece]]

## Notes

The lowest-denomination coin in OSRIC. Used for cheap goods and tipping. Even a [[Torch\|Torch]] costs only 1 cp; a [[Candle, Beeswax\|candle]] is 3 cp.
