## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 5ft per MU level
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** 20ft x 20ft square per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

When the spell is completed, a wall of stone grows from existing stone formations. In order for the spell to succeed, there must be existing rock large enough to serve as the anchor on each side of the wall. The wall itself is one quarter of an inch thick for each level of the magic user and has a vertical area of up to 20ft x 20ft per caster level.

## Details

No rock from the foundation is consumed; the wall's stone is created from nothing, but needs existing rock from which to emerge and with which to meld.

The wall need not be vertical, but must be anchored -- a bridge is a good example of a way to create and use a horizontal wall of stone.

The wall may be dispelled, but otherwise it has all the characteristics of natural stone.
