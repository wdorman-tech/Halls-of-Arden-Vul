## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** included with armour purchase
- **Movement Cap:** none
- **Weight:** -- (worn)
- **Cost:** 10 gp (if purchased separately)
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 0 worn / 1 carried

## Notes

All purchased armour comes with a helmet included in the price. A helmet purchased separately costs 10 gp.

Without a helmet, characters take additional risk against head shots and certain critical attacks (per GM ruling).
