## Notes

A **Task** is a defined exploration goal that, when completed, awards a percentage of party TXP to all players. There are three tiers: minor, major, and extraordinary.

## Tiers

- **Minor (2% of TXP):** Smart thinking and planning. Examples: finding and disabling a trap without injuries, discovering a well-hidden stash of treasure.
- **Major (5% of TXP):** Rarer / harder challenges, generally only encountered once or twice per "season." Examples: solving a difficult puzzle, completing a faction quest you are trying to ally with.
- **Extraordinary (10% of TXP):** Very difficult tasks, multi-session goals, and/or significant status-quo shifts. Examples: taking down a faction, finally locating an artifact you've been searching for.

## Mechanics

- TXP and the % distribution are detailed in [[Exploration Awards\|Exploration Awards]].
- XP is awarded **immediately upon completion** by default.
- Henchmen get half-share, hirelings get quarter-share. They do not affect TXP.
