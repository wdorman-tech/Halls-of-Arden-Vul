## Notes

The Thorcin are a rural people native to this continent, predating Archontean expansion. They are farmers, herders, hunters, woodland-folk, and small-village dwellers — the population the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]] is now governing (or attempting to). They tend to favor [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]] over [[The Ten\|The Ten]], with nature deities like [[Aranyania\|Aranyania]] and household gods like [[Bisor\|Bisor]] especially common.
