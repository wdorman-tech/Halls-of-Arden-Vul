## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Conjuration\|Conjuration/Summoning]]
- **Range:** 80ft
- **Duration:** 1 turn per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** Elemental creature(s)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 rounds
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell summons forth and controls an elemental being from the plane of fire.

## Details

Roll 1d100 to determine what appears:

| Roll | Creature |
|---|---|
| 01-86 | 16HD Fire elemental |
| 87-94 | 1d4 Salamanders |
| 95-98 | Efreet |
| 99-00 | 24 HD Fire elemental |

The elemental will act as your ally, remaining for the spell's duration to assist you however possible, including engaging in combat with your enemies. Druids need no protective circle to summon fire elementals and need not concentrate upon the elemental in any way.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Dismiss Fire Elemental\|Dismiss Fire Elemental]].
