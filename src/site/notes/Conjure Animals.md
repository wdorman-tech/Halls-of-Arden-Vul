## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 6th (Cleric) / 6th (Illusionist)
- **School:** [[Divine Conjuration\|Divine Conjuration]] / [[Phantasmal Conjuration\|Phantasmal Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 2 rounds per cleric level (Cleric) / 1 round per illusionist level (Illusionist)
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 1 creature for each caster level
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 9 segments (Cleric) / 6 segments (Illusionist)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell "conjures" animal allies from thin air. The caster must be able to see the target point.

## Details

When casting the spell, the caster decides the relative strength of the creatures to call (Weak, Medium, Strong, or Mighty). The stronger the creature type, the fewer creatures appear. The GM then rolls or decides what kinds of creatures appear.

### Weak (1 creature for each caster level)

| d20 | Creature |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | Baboon |
| 4-9 | Dog |
| 10-16 | Giant rat |
| 17-20 | Jackal |

### Medium (1 creature per 2 levels, rounding down)

| d20 | Creature |
|---|---|
| 1-4 | Badger |
| 5-14 | Herd animal |
| 15-20 | Horse |

### Strong (1 creature per 3 levels, rounding down)

| d20 | Creature |
|---|---|
| 1-2 | Boar |
| 3-5 | Camel |
| 6-7 | Giant goat |
| 8-9 | Horse |
| 10-11 | Hyena |
| 12-13 | Leopard |
| 14-15 | Phorusrhacid (Axe Beak) |
| 16-17 | Stag |
| 18-20 | Wolf |

### Mighty (1 creature per 4 levels, rounding down)

| d20 | Creature |
|---|---|
| 1-3 | Ape |
| 4-5 | Bear |
| 6-7 | Boar |
| 8-9 | Bull |
| 10-12 | Dire wolf |
| 13-14 | Leopard |
| 15-16 | Lion |
| 17-18 | Sabretooth tiger |
| 19-20 | Tiger |

This spell will fail unless the caster is on the Prime Material Plane.

This spell makes the animals understand the caster's spoken word commands. The animals don't have to obey a command that's obviously self-destructive.

The conjured animals are dismissed, and the spell ends, when they're all killed; when the caster is killed; when the caster uses their action to dismiss them; when the caster leaves the plane on which they cast this spell; or when the conjured animals leave the plane.

Killing or defeating animals conjured by this spell doesn't award any xp.

**Note:** If spacing is an issue on a battlemat or VTT, the GM may determine the spacing of the conjured animals when they appear.
