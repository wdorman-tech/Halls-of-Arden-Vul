## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 6ft radius around target point
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

Clairaudience allows the caster to eavesdrop magically upon a particular location, hearing (but not seeing) what is happening there. There is no effective limit to the spell's range, but the location must either be familiar to the caster (they have been there before) or be obvious, such as the top floor inside a tower they can see, or just beyond a dungeon door.

## Details

The spell cannot penetrate metal; sheets of any type of metal between the caster and the target area will prevent them from being able to scry upon the area.

The caster can hear sounds in a 6ft radius of the spell's focal point.

The spell cannot be used to scry across planes of existence; it is limited to locations in the same plane in which it is cast.
