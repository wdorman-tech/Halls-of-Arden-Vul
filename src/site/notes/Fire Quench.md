## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Evocation\|Evocation]]
- **Range:** 150ft
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Area of Effect:** Two 20ft x 20ft x 20ft areas per druid level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 9 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell extinguishes all normal fire in the area of effect (which is twice that of a [[Fire Storm\|Fire Storm]]). Magical fire has a 5% chance per druid level of the caster to be extinguished by the spell.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Fire Storm\|Fire Storm]].
