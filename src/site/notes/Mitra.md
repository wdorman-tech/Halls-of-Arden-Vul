## Notes

Mitra is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the lawful good god of justice, contracts, omniscience, defenders, oaths, and humanitarian impulses. He is perhaps the most iconic Lawful Good god in the pantheon — a patron of paladins, lawful judges, and oath-bound knights. His symbols are scales of justice, lions, and flames; his weapon is the long sword.
