## Notes

Speak with Plants is both a [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]] spell and a [[Monk\|Monk]] ability.

## Spell Stats (Cleric / Druid)

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 4th (Cleric) / 4th (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]] / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 30ft (Cleric) / Caster (Druid)
- **Duration:** 1 round per cleric level (Cleric) / 2 rounds per druid level (Druid)
- **Area of Effect:** One plant within range (Cleric) / 40ft radius around caster (Druid)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 10 rounds (Cleric) / 1 turn (Druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Spell Details

Only you can hear the magical translation.

When you speak to the plant, you speak in Common, and it magically understands you. It speaks in whatever way its species naturally communicates, and the spell translates its words into your mind. Some plants in OSRIC are mobile and potentially dangerous. You can try to persuade, deceive, or bargain with such a creature into performing a service for you.

Your GM will determine if a creature is a "plant" or not for the purposes of this spell.

The affected plant usually won't begin combat with you until the spell ends, but if you or your companions have attacked the plant in the last turn, then it doesn't have to stop fighting.

This spell doesn't compel the affected plant to tell the truth.

## Monk Ability

[[Monk\|Monk]]s gain Speak with Plants as a class ability. With Wisdom 15+ it is gained at 8th level; otherwise it is delayed to level 9 (catch-up ability).

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 8th (WIS 15+) or 9th (WIS <15)
- **Frequency:** Once per day
- **Effect:** Speak with plants
