## Notes

[[Druid Shape-Shift\|Druid Shape-Shift]] (Shapeshift) is the iconic 7th-level [[Druid\|Druid]] ability allowing the druid to assume the form of natural animals or birds up to three times per day. Each transformation also heals significant damage. Uses recharge at sunrise.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level Required:** 7th
- **Uses Per Day:** 3 (regained at sunrise)
- **Allowed Forms:** Any natural animal or bird; size from mouse-sized up to double the druid's normal weight
- **Mind/Body:** Composite creature — druid's mind, animal's body. Use creature's movement rate and combat statistics, but druid's hit points
- **Forbidden Forms:** Human, demi-human, or humanoid (other than the druid's own normal form)
- **Restrictions While Shape-Shifted:** Cannot cast spells; can only talk if the form has a voice (e.g. parrot)
- **Venom:** If the form is venomous, venom does NOT affect elementals, fiends, golems, jellies, oozes, slimes, or undead
- **Healing on Transform:** Heals injured druid based on level:
    - Level 7: 3d10 hp
    - Levels 8-9: 4d10 hp
    - Levels 10-11: 5d10 hp
    - Levels 12-13: 6d10 hp
    - Level 14: 7d10 hp
- **Reverting:** Choosing to turn back to normal form does NOT consume a use, but does NOT heal
- **Combat Timing:** Treated as casting a spell with casting time of 0 segments — cannot attack or cast a spell in the same round, but the change of form cannot be interrupted
