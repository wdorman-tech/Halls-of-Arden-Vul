## Notes

The [[Ranger\|Ranger]] is a wilderness warrior modelled on Aragorn and Robin Hood — a sub-class of [[Fighter\|Fighter]] who defends civilisation from evils that lurk just outside its borders. Rangers gain extra hit points at first level (2d8 starting), exceptional [[Tracking\|Tracking]], surprise advantages, and bonus damage against [[Giants\|Giants]] and evil [[Humanoids\|Humanoids]]. At higher levels they gain both [[Druid Spellcasting\|druidic]] and [[Magic-User Spellcasting\|arcane]] [[Ranger Spellcasting\|spellcasting]].

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 13, DEX 6, CON 14, INT 13, WIS 14, CHA 6
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Strength\|Strength]], [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]], and [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] (10% XP bonus if all three are 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d8 (max 11 at 10th level); rangers start with 2d8 at level 1
- **Alignment:** Any good
- **Armour:** Any
- **Weapons:** Any
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 3 (additional at level 5, 8, 11, 14, and 17)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -2 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** Available as optional rule (see [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]])
- **Initial Gold:** 5d4 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]], [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]] (per ancestry rules)
- **Multi-class options:** None (sub-class of Fighter)
- **Restrictions:** Must remain Good in alignment; must travel light and donate excess treasure to the poor; cannot work with more than two other rangers at a time; cannot employ hirelings or henchmen until level 8
- **Class Abilities:** [[Surprise (Ranger)\|Alert and Stealthy]], [[Tracking\|Cunning Tracker]], [[Giant Class Damage Bonus\|Fell Handed]] (+1 damage per ranger level vs evil humanoids/giants)
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 2 — Heroic Assault; Lvl 7 — May employ followers; Lvl 8 — Melee Attack Combination 3/2, [[Ranger Spellcasting\|Druidic Spell Casting]]; Lvl 9 — [[Ranger Spellcasting\|Arcane Spell Casting]] and stronghold/[[Ranger Followers\|Ranger Followers]]; Lvl 15 — Melee Attack Combination 2/1
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,250 XP; Level 8 at 150,000 XP (druidic spells); Level 9 at 225,000 XP (arcane spells, stronghold, followers); Level 18 at 2,925,000 XP (caps at 6th-level druid casting)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Optional stronghold at level 9; may attract a [[Ranger Followers\|Band of Merry Folk]] or Band of Magical Creatures at level 9
