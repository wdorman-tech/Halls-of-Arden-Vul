## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell transforms one creature into stone.

## Details

The [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against this spell is Petrifaction/Polymorph, not Spells.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Stone to Flesh\|Stone to Flesh]].
