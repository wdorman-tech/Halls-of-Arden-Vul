## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 2 turns per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell enables the caster to cloak themselves in the physical shape of another creature, and to continue shape-shifting at will for the length of the spell's duration. The transformation is of an entirely lesser order than that of [[Polymorph Other\|Polymorph Other]], incurring no risk of a system shock or of truly becoming the transformed creature.

## Details

Changing from one shape to another requires only 30 seconds (5 segments), and when the caster returns to their original form (ending the spell) they are healed of 1d12 points of any damage inflicted against the polymorphed forms they assumed.

The caster can polymorph into forms no smaller than a songbird and no heavier than 2,000 lbs.

Only the movement capabilities of the new form can be used, not its attacks, defences, or other abilities. Thus, the caster can transform themselves into a lion, and run as fast as a lion, but their claws don't inflict more damage than human fists. Similarly, if they transform into a dragon (maybe a small one, due to the weight limit), they will have the dragon's ability to fly but no breath weapon. As an orc, the caster would be able to use whatever weapons they can use in their original shape, but would lack [[Infravision\|Infravision]].

The caster retains their own [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] and whatever armour class they had when they transformed (including the effects of armour, if any).
