## Notes

The [[Fighter\|Fighter]] is the backbone of the adventuring party — the front-line warrior whose [[Strength\|Strength]] and hit points keep weaker companions alive. Fighters have the best to-hit progression of any class, may use any weapon, armour, or shield, and are one of the three classes (with [[Ranger\|Ranger]] and [[Paladin\|Paladin]]) eligible for [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]]. They establish strongholds and attract loyal mercenary followers at name level.

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 9, DEX 6, CON 7, INT 3, WIS 6, CHA 6
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Strength\|Strength]] (10% XP bonus if STR 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d10 (max 9)
- **Alignment:** Any
- **Armour:** Any
- **Weapons:** Any
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 4 (additional at level 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, 13, 15, 17, and 19)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -2 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** Available as optional rule (see [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]])
- **Initial Gold:** 5d4 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** All (with level limits per ancestry)
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Class Abilities:** [[Multiple Attacks\|Multiple Attacks]] / Melee Attack Combinations against weaker foes
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 2 — [[Multiple Attacks\|Heroic Assault]] (vs <8 max hp foes); Lvl 7 — Melee Attack Combination 3/2; Lvl 8 — Mighty Blow; Lvl 9 — [[Build Stronghold (Fighter)\|Stronghold and Title]]; Lvl 13 — Melee Attack Combination 2/1
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,000 XP; Level 7 at 70,000 XP (3/2 attacks); Level 9 at 250,000 XP (name level — stronghold); Level 13 at 1,250,000 XP
- **Stronghold/Followers:** [[Build Stronghold (Fighter)\|Castle and fiefdom]] at level 9; attracts a 1d4+3 NPC mercenary captain plus 4d20 0-level mercenaries — see [[Followers (Fighter)\|Followers (Fighter)]]
