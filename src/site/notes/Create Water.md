## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st (Cleric) / 2nd (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Conjuration\|Conjuration]] / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round (Cleric) / 1 turn (Druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

For each level you have in the cleric (or druid) class, you can conjure up to sixteen litres of water (about 1 cubic foot) from the [[Elemental Plane of Water\|Elemental Plane of Water]], from a targeted point. You can limit the amount of water created if you choose.

## Details

Unless the target point is inside a receptacle, the water immediately spills.

The target point can't be inside a creature.

Unlike the cleric version, the druidic version of this spell is not reversible.

## Reversed Form

The reverse (cleric only) is [[Destroy Water\|Destroy Water]].
