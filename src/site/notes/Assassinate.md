## Notes

[[Assassinate\|Assassinate]] is the [[Assassin\|Assassin]]'s signature ability — a one-shot kill attempt against a surprised enemy. On success, the target dies regardless of hit points; on failure, the target still takes normal weapon damage but the attempt doesn't count as a [[Backstab\|Backstab]].

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]]
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll
- **Conditions for Use:** Must surprise the target (see [[Surprise\|Surprise]] in Combat); target must have vital organs or vulnerable parts
- **Base Chance:** 50%
- **Level Modifier:** +5% per level of the assassin
- **Target Modifier:** -5% per 2 hit dice or 2 levels of the target (round down). If target has both, GM decides which to use.
- **Example:** 8th-level assassin vs 8 HD target = 50 + 40 - 20 = 70%
- **Success:** Target dies regardless of hp
- **Failure:** Target takes weapon damage as if hit normally; failed attempt does NOT count as a [[Backstab\|Backstab]]
- **Immune Targets:** Oozes, slimes, elementals, golems, zombies (no vital organs); avatars of gods, archdevils, demon lords, or other creatures of similar power
