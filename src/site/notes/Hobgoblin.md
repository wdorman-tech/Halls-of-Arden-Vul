## Notes

Hobgoblin is the disciplined, militaristic language of [[Hobgoblin\|Hobgoblin]] legions. Unlike the chaotic chatter of [[Goblin\|Goblin]], Hobgoblin has formal grammar, military terminology, and a literate tradition.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Hobgoblin\|Hobgoblin]] (native), often known by [[Goblin\|Goblin]]s in their service
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Negotiating with hobgoblin warbands, deciphering hobgoblin orders and maps
