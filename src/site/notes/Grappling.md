## Notes

**Grappling** is both an attack form (one of three [[Unarmed Combat\|Unarmed Combat]] types) and a *condition* reflecting that combatants are in physical close contact. After a grappling attempt gets past a fend-off and lands on the Unarmed To-Hit table, results are rolled on the Grappling Attack Results table (1d8) to determine the **hold** established (or shifted).

## Key Facts

**As a condition** (grappled creature):
- **Cannot fend off** unarmed attacks
- **Cannot cast spells**
- **Can only attack** with a dagger-length or smaller weapon (natural weapons exempt — claws/bite work normally)
- Ordinary [[Engaged in Melee Combat\|melee combat]] restrictions still apply

**Hold mechanics:**
- A grappling attack can only be made with **both hands free**
- A successful Unarmed To-Hit roll → roll 1d8 on Grappling Results
- The higher roll establishes the **Controlling hold**; the lower (or tied second) is the **Inferior hold**
- Both combatants can hold each other (typical outcome)

**Grappling Attack Results (1d8):**
| Roll | Result | Damage (real/temp) |
|---|---|---|
| 1 or lower | Awkward Scuffling | 0 / 1 |
| 2-4 | Arm Grab / Elbow Bash | 1 / 1-3 |
| 5 | Waist Lock / Leg Hold | 1 / 4 (5 total) |
| 6 | Rear Choke / Head Butt | 2 / 4 (6 total) |
| 7 | Arm Lock / Wicked Elbow Bash | 2 / 5 (7 total) |
| 8 | Head Lock / Gouge | 2 / 6 (8 total) |
| 9+ | Decisive Throw (break + re-establish, +2) | 3 / 6 (9 total) — defender loses next action |

**Modifiers:**
- Attacker: only one arm/appendage -1; multiple arms +1
- Size: -4 Tiny, -2 Small, +0 Medium, +2 Large, +6 Huge, +10 Gargantuan (mirrored for defender)

- **Helplessness:** combatants are not "helpless" until rendered unconscious by temporary damage
- **Damage tracking:** most damage is **temporary**, healing 1 hp/round at the start of each round
- **Pile-on:** additional attackers easily get holds (only fail on natural 1) — a fast way to bring someone unconscious
