## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Cleric, Magic-User) / 2nd (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine Necromancy\|Divine Necromancy]] / [[Druidic Necromancy\|Druidic Necromancy]] / [[Arcane Necromancy\|Arcane Necromancy]]
- **Range:** Touch (Cleric, Magic-User) / 10ft (Druid)
- **Duration:** 10 rounds + 1 round per cleric level (Cleric) / 4 rounds + 2 rounds per druid level (Druid) / 6 rounds + 1 round per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched / 1 creature
- **Components:** V, S, M (Cleric, Druid) / V, S (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** Fast (Cleric, Magic-User) / 3 segments (Druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None; willing recipient only

## Notes

The target becomes corpse-like, so they can play possum. While corpse-like, they're protected from certain kinds of harm.

## Details

This spell only affects willing creatures. The caster can end this spell at any time, and the affected creature can end this spell at any time.

- Affected creatures don't need to eat, drink, breathe, or sleep.
- Affected creatures can hear, smell, and think, but they're [[Blinded\|Blinded]] and lose their sense of touch. They don't feel pain and don't know if they're hurt. They're unaffected by [[Undead\|Undead]] creatures' touch attacks, can't be poisoned, and can't contract disease. If the creature is already poisoned when this spell is cast, then the poison's effects are suspended for the duration of the spell, but return afterwards.
- If an affected creature takes damage from any other source, then that damage is halved. Round any fractions down.

When the spell ends, it takes the affected creature one round to stand up and recover.

The druid and magic-user versions note that damage caused to the pseudo-corpse is reduced by one half, and the spell's recipient will not even know that the damage has been caused — potentially a dangerous situation. A person under the influence of this spell is not subject to level draining by undead and will not be affected by poisons until the spell wears off or is otherwise removed.

The caster can remove the catalepsy at will, and the spell is subject to being [[Dispel Magic\|dispelled]].
