## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee (Reach)
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 14+)
- **Damage:** 1d6+1 vs S/M, 3d4 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 6
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 5ft
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Cost:** 4 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A three-pronged spear. Long-hafted and pointed, the trident can be used as a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]] from the second rank and inflicts **double damage** when [[Set Weapon Against Charge\|set against a charge]].

One-handed use requires STR 14+.

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (piercing).
