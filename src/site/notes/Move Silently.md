## Notes

[[Move Silently\|Move Silently]] (Move Quietly) is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to move without being heard. While moving silently the thief moves at half speed. Combined with [[Hide in Shadows\|Hide in Shadows]] it sets up a [[Backstab\|Backstab]] in quiet conditions; in noisy combat, hiding alone is enough.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] same table; [[Monk\|Monk]] also has it as [[Move Silently (Monk)\|Move Silently (Monk)]])
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll
- **Speed:** Half speed while attempting silent movement
- **OSRIC Note:** You DO know whether the roll succeeded. On failure, you may try again next round.
- **Combination with Hide:** Use both Hide and Move Silently when sneaking up on someone in quiet conditions; in noisy combat, [[Hide in Shadows\|Hide in Shadows]] alone is enough.
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
