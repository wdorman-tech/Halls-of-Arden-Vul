## Notes

The Halls of Arden Vul is the megadungeon campaign setting we are playing in. It sits within the broader world of the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]], whose imperial reach has recently begun to expand across the continent. The dungeon itself is the primary site of adventure, but the surrounding world — its [[Pantheon\|Pantheon]], its three human [[Culture\|cultures]], its arcane [[Seven Collegia\|collegia]], and the ancient ruins beneath — all feed back into play.

The setting uses [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] 3.0 with a number of houserules. See the [[The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc\|The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc]] for the canonical houserule reference.

## Key Facts

- **System:** [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] 3.0
- **Style:** Megadungeon sandbox, OSR
- **Polity:** [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]] (recent imperial expansion onto this continent)
- **Native peoples:** [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] (rural natives), [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] (northern viking-like)
- **Pantheon:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]] (major Archontean) and [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]] (minor)
- **Magical institutions:** [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]] (only legal places of arcane study)
- **Ancient lore tongue:** [[Mithric\|Mithric]]
