## Notes

Archonteans are the dominant culture of the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]], whose imperial reach has recently begun to spread across this continent. They are the imperial colonists, soldiers, administrators, and merchants — both the carriers of imperial civilization and the face of imperial conquest, depending on whom you ask. Their state religion is centered on [[The Ten\|The Ten]].

## Key Facts

- **Language:** [[Archontean (language)\|Archontean (language)]]
- **Region:** Spreading across the continent from the imperial heartland
- **Polity:** [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]]
- **Religion:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]] are primary; [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]] are minor by their reckoning
- **Class associations:** Any; [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s in particular are bound to the [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]]
- **Setting role:** Imperial occupier / civiliser, depending on perspective
