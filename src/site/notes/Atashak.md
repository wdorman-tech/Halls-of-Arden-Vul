## Notes

Atashak is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the lawful evil god of fire, the sun, inevitable destruction, battle, and sacrifice. He is the cruel sun that scorches as it rises — the patron of conquerors who burn fields and demand offerings. His symbol is a burning torch; his weapon, the battle axe.
