## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 4
- **Space Required:** 3ft
- **Length:** 6ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt
- **Weight:** 4 lb
- **Cost:** 5 sp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A simple wooden staff, traditionally about 6 feet long. Permitted to all classes including [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]], and [[Druid\|Druid]]. A [[Monk\|Monk]]'s primary weapon in many builds (also covers Bo and Jo sticks).

A [[Druid\|Druid]]'s shillelagh staff, a [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]'s walking-staff, and a [[Cleric\|Cleric]]'s pilgrim staff are all this weapon.
