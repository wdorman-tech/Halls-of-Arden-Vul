## Notes

When a character is **resurrected** or **raised from the dead**, they must make a **Resurrection Success** roll. The character must roll **lower than or equal** to the number shown on a d100 (determined by [[Constitution\|Constitution]]). If the roll fails, the character does not survive and **cannot be raised or resurrected ever again**.

## Key Facts

- **Roll:** d100, must be ≤ Resurrection Success number from the [[Constitution\|Constitution]] table
- **Failure:** death is permanent; no further raises possible
- **Modifier source:** Constitution score
- **Spells affected:** [[Raise Dead\|Raise Dead]], [[Resurrection\|Resurrection]], [[Reincarnation\|Reincarnation]] (varies)
- **Excluded ancestries:** [[Elf\|Elf]] and [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]] — spirits not souls, cannot be raised at all
- **Distinct from:** [[System Shock\|System Shock]] (used for polymorph and similar bodily transformations)
