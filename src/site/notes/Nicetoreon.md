## Notes

Nicetoreon is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the True Neutral goddess of judgment, the afterlife, the passage of souls, and earning merit. She is the cosmic accountant of souls — neither rewarding nor punishing in advance, but weighing on the way out. Her symbols are a lead weight and a small figurine representing the soul; her weapon, the mace.
