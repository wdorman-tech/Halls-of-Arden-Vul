## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee / Missile (Hurled), Reach
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage (Melee):** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 8
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 5ft
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 20ft (Hurled)
- **Rate of Fire:** 1 per round (when thrown)
- **Weight:** 2 lb
- **Cost:** 5 sp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A light throwing spear, primarily used as a [[Thrown Weapon\|Thrown Weapon]] but usable in melee. Strength bonus applies to thrown damage.

As a long-hafted, pointed weapon, the javelin can be used as a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]] and inflicts **double damage** when [[Set Weapon Against Charge\|set against a charge]].

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged/piercing).
