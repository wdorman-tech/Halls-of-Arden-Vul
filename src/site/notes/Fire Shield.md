## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 2 rounds + 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

Pale flames envelope the caster, giving them certain protection against either fire- or cold-based attacks, depending upon whether they choose to wreath themselves in cold flame or hot flame. Anyone making a melee attack on the caster will inflict normal damage on the caster, but the attacker also takes damage: twice as much as inflicted. The spell has other effects depending in whether the caster chose to cast hot flame or cold flame for the shield.

## Details

### Cold Flame

- Cold flame grants the caster a +2 bonus on any [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs fire damage.
- If the fire attack does not allow a saving throw, the caster may make a saving throw for half damage.
- If the fire attack allows a saving throw for half damage and they succeed, they take no damage.
- If the caster fails a saving throw against a cold based attack while wreathed in cold flame, however, they suffer twice the normal damage.

### Hot Flame

- Hot flame grants the caster a +2 bonus on any saving throw vs cold damage.
- If the cold attack does not allow a saving throw, the caster may make a saving throw for half damage.
- If the cold attack allows a saving throw for half damage and they succeed, they take no damage.
- If the caster fails a saving throw against a fire based attack while wreathed in hot flame, however, they suffer twice the normal damage.
