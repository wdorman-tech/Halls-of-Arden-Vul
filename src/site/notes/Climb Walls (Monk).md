## Notes

[[Climb Walls (Monk)\|Climb Walls (Monk)]] is one of several thief skills granted to the [[Monk\|Monk]] class. Monks are exceptionally good climbers from level 1 — better than equivalent-level thieves at most levels.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll, succeed if rolled equal to or under the listed percentage
- **When to Roll:** Only for challenging climbs — trees, ropes, ladders need no roll
- **Mid-Climb Check:** Roll again at the midpoint; failure = fall
