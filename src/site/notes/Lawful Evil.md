## Notes

Because you are Lawful, you have a strong tendency to obey and support authority — as long as it is not Good or Chaotic in nature. If a law is inconvenient, you interpret it to the letter, not according to its spirit, when you can get away with it. You are the perfect minion for a villain. Because you are Evil, you act in your own interest with no limits on the harm you will do to others, though some limits may be imposed by following laws or a leader.

Lawful Evil is the alignment of tyrants, organised crime bosses, devil-pact warlocks, and rigid theocrats serving cruel gods.

- **Axis:** Lawful on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Evil on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]

- **Behavior:** rule-loving but cruel; the perfect minion or the patient villain
