## Notes

The College of Perception specialises in **illusion** and **mentalism**. Almost every [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] in the empire trained here, and so do most [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s who prefer to charm or sleep their enemies rather than fireball them. Its reputation skews "subtle and slippery."

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Illusion, mentalism, charm and sleep effects
- **Reputation:** Specialist house — the place to learn enchantment and illusion
- **Membership profile:** Almost all [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]s; subtler magic-users
- **Schools of magic favored:** Illusion, Enchantment/Charm
