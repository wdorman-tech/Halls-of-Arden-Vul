## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 20ft radius spherical cloud
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell creates a nauseating cloud of vapours to billow forth in a location chosen within range. All creatures caught within (or entering) the noxious cloud must make [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s. Any creature failing to save will be completely unable to act for 1d4+1 rounds, falling to the ground retching and gagging (treated as [[Stunned\|Stunned]] and staggered).

## Details

A creature that succeeds in making the saving throw may move from within the cloud and be free of the effects after only one round of gasping fresh air (being treated as stunned during that one round). Even creatures that succeed in making a saving throw cannot do anything within the cloud other than to leave as fast as possible.
