## Notes

**Enchantment / Charm** spells mostly affect a target creature's mind or senses. A few enchantment spells are different and instead let you imbue a creature, object, or place with magical properties.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Enchantment
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Enchantment
