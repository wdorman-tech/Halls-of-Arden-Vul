## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1d4 +1 rounds
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The creature touched (requiring a successful "to hit" roll) flies into an uncontrollable, twitching tarantella, dancing wildly.

## Details

The creature's armour class is penalised by 4 points, and it will fail any and all [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s during the spell's duration. It cannot act in any way, or gain benefit from a shield.
