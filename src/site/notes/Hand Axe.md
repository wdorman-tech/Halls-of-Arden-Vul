## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee / Missile (Hurled)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d4 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 4
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 1.5ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Range Increment:** 10ft (Hurled)
- **Rate of Fire:** 1 per round (when thrown)
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Cost:** 1 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A small hatchet usable as both a melee weapon and a [[Thrown Weapon\|Thrown Weapon]]. Strength bonus applies to hurled damage.

The [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
