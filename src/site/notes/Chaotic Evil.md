## Notes

Because you are Chaotic, you oppose laws and authority and do not consider them binding upon you. You probably keep your oaths only as a matter of personal honour, or to maintain credibility. Because you are Evil, you act in your own interest with no limitations on the harm you will do to others.

Chaotic Evil is the alignment of demons (who are inherently CE), wild raiders, mad cultists, and serial killers who answer to nothing and no one.

## Key Facts

- **Axis:** Chaotic on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Evil on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** Chaotic Evil tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]):** [[Qandasi\|Qandasi]], [[Treshub\|Treshub]]
- **Inherent alignment of:** demons (cannot shift)
- **Behavior:** unrestrained, self-interested, harms others without limit
