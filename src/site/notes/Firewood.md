## Stats

- **Cost:** 1 cp per day's worth
- **Weight:** 3 lb per day
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1 per day

## Notes

A bundle of firewood, sufficient for one day's cooking and warmth. Often gathered for free in wilderness, but useful pre-cut for travelling parties.
