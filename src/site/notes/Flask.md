## Stats

- **Capacity:** small liquid (typically 1 pint)
- **Weight:** -- (empty leather flask)
- **Cost:** 3 cp
- **Inventory Slots:** 0 worn / 1 if many carried

## Notes

A small leather (or sometimes ceramic/glass) liquid container. Common uses include carrying water, wine, lamp oil, or [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]].

A glass or ceramic flask is what holds [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]] (1 vial = .5 lb, 25 gp) and [[Flask of Oil\|lamp oil]] (1 pint = 1 lb, 1 sp). These can be thrown as grenade-like missiles — see [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]] and [[Flask of Oil\|Flask of Oil]].
