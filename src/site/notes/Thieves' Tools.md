## Stats

- **Cost:** 30 gp
- **Weight:** 1 lb
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A small kit of lockpicks, files, probes, and tension wrenches — required for the [[Thief\|Thief]] class abilities of [[Open Locks\|opening locks]] and [[Find Traps\|finding traps]] / [[Remove Traps\|removing traps]].

Without thieves' tools, a thief takes significant penalties to these skills (or cannot perform them at all, by GM ruling).
