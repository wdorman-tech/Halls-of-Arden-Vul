## Concept

Crossbows are mechanically-cocked launched [[Missile Weapon\|Missile Weapon]]s. They require **two hands** to use (no [[Shield, Small\|shield]] possible). Strength bonus does NOT apply to damage.

## OSRIC Crossbows

| Crossbow | Range Increment | RoF | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[Crossbow, Heavy\|Crossbow, Heavy]] | 80ft | 1/2 (one shot every 2 rounds) | 20 gp |
| [[Crossbow, Light\|Crossbow, Light]] | 60ft | 1 | 12 gp |

- [[Crossbow, Heavy\|Heavy crossbows]] **cannot be used from horseback** — only a footman can brace them correctly before firing.
- The slow rate of fire reflects the time required to crank/reload the weapon.

## Ammunition

Crossbows fire [[Bolt, Heavy Crossbow\|bolts]] (also called quarrels).
