## Notes

Pass Without Trace is both a [[Druid\|Druid]] spell and a 3rd-level [[Druid\|Druid]] class ability of the same name (Wilderness Movement).

## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]] / Charm
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Spell Details

This spell allows the targeted creature to move through any sort of terrain without leaving behind any sign of its passage. It leaves no footprints, no scent, and no other telltales of movement.

The path taken by the target creature radiates magic for 6d6 turns, so the only way to detect the path taken is with a [[Detect Magic\|Detect Magic]] spell.

## Druid Class Ability (Wilderness Movement)

This is a 3rd-level [[Druid\|Druid]] ability that allows the druid to move through natural growth or undergrowth at full speed, optionally leaving no tracks or trace of their passing.

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level Required:** 3rd
- **Mechanics:** Automatic — chosen at will
- **Effects:**
    - May move through natural growth or undergrowth at full movement speed
    - May choose to leave no tracks or trace of passing
