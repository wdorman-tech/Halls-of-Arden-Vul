## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Illusionist) / 4th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]] / [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** 20ft + 20ft per illusionist level / 20ft per MU level
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** 40ft x 40ft area + 10ft square per illusionist level / 10ft x 10ft square per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments (Illusionist) / 1 turn (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell masks the true appearance of an area, making it appear as something else. A road can be made to appear as a river, a forest can be made to appear as a ravine, etc.

## Details

The spell's effect remains until some intelligent being touches the area (or until it is dispelled). The spell is particularly useful for hiding pits, or making cliff edges seem to extend more than they actually do, but it has many other useful applications for the creative player.
