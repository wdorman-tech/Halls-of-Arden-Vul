## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Illusionist) / 4th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]] / [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** 5ft x 30ft x 60ft cone (Illusionist) / Cone, 60ft long, 5ft at origin, 30ft diameter at terminus (Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S (Illusionist) / V, S, M (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments (Illusionist) / 4 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell causes utter panic in the creatures exposed to the cone-shaped emanation of phantasmal terror the caster throws forth. If any creature, of any level or hit dice, fails its [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against the spell, it will move away from the caster as fast as possible, for as many rounds as the caster has levels in the illusionist or magic-user class.

## Details

There is a base 60% chance for a creature to drop whatever it is holding (immediately upon failing the saving throw), but this is reduced by 5% per HD (or level) of the panicked creature.
