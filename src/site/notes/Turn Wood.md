## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** 4 rounds per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** 120ft wide path, 20ft long per druid level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

At the completion of this spell, you hold your palm outstretched, and with the final word of the spell a tremendous burst of power rolls forth, forcing back all wooden objects.

## Details

Even very large wooden structures that are not affixed to the ground will be affected, although objects fixed to the ground will manage to withstand the spell's awesome power. A sapling-sized or smaller piece of wood will splinter and break even if it is firmly affixed.

You do not need to concentrate on maintaining the waves of force, and you cannot change the direction of the flow of eldritch power once the spell is cast.
