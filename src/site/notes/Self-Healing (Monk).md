## Notes

[[Self-Healing (Monk)\|Self-Healing (Monk)]] (Heal Self) is the [[Monk\|Monk]]'s ability to use focused mental and physical discipline to heal their own injuries — once per day, with the dice scaling by level.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Frequency:** Once per day
- **Mechanics:** Roll 1d4, plus a level-based bonus
