## Notes

A **melee attack** is an attack with a hand-held weapon such as a sword, halberd, or dagger. (Bows, crossbows, and hurled weapons are [[Missile Attack\|missile attacks]], not melee attacks.)

To make a melee attack, roll a **d20**. If the result (plus bonuses, minus penalties) is **equal to or greater than the target number to hit the enemy's [[Armour Class\|Armour Class]]**, you score a hit and roll [[Damage\|Damage]], which is subtracted from the target's [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]]. To-hit target numbers depend on character [[Class\|Class]] and [[Level\|Level]].

If using **Ascending Armour Class**, use the AAC formula instead.

## Key Facts

- **Roll:** d20 vs. target's AC
- **Bonuses:** [[Strength\|Strength]] to-hit and damage; magic weapon bonuses; [[Charge\|Charge]] +2; situational mods
- **Range:** 10 ft for any melee weapon (the "range" of even a hand-blow is 10 ft because of chaotic combat movement)
- **Cannot attack past an ally** unless using a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]]
- **Cannot attack past an enemy** to a different enemy
- **You may select your target** when figures are 1:1 with creatures; in mass melee, target is randomly determined
- **A natural 1** always misses (and may break a [[Weapon Proficiency\|Weapon Proficiency]] for non-proficient use)
