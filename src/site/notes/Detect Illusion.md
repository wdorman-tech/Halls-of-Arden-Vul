## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Divination\|Phantasmal Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 3 rounds + 2 rounds per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** Path 10ft wide, length 10ft per illusionist level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

When the caster casts this spell, they see any illusion for what it truly is. By touching an illusion, the caster can make its true form visible to any observer.

## Details

The caster is able to see the illusion as a faint overlay on top of the reality, so they know what others are seeing.
