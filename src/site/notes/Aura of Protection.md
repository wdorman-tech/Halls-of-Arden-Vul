## Notes

[[Aura of Protection\|Aura of Protection]] covers two related [[Paladin\|Paladin]] benefits: the better saving-throw table ("Shielded by Faith") and the permanent 10-foot radius Protection From Evil aura that radiates from the paladin at all times.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)

### Shielded by Faith
- The paladin uses a more favourable saving-throw table than other classes (see [[Paladin\|Paladin]] saving-throw table 1.3.8.4B)

### Protection From Evil 10ft Radius
- The paladin permanently radiates the first-level divine spell Protection From Evil with a 10ft radius (instead of normal radius)
- If the effect is suppressed or dispelled, it automatically returns at the beginning of the next combat round, even if the paladin is unconscious
- Effect ends if the paladin dies; returns if they are raised or resurrected
