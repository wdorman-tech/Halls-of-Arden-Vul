## Notes

When a [[Fighter\|Fighter]] establishes a stronghold at 9th level (see [[Build Stronghold (Fighter)\|Build Stronghold (Fighter)]]), a small army of broadly-aligned NPCs arrives to serve. The leader of these followers is an experienced mercenary captain, accompanied by a body of 0-level rank-and-file.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]]
- **Level Required:** 9th (concurrent with [[Build Stronghold (Fighter)\|Stronghold and Title]])
- **Mercenary Captain:** 1 NPC fighter of level 1d4+3
- **Rank and File:** 4d20 NPC 0-level mercenaries
- **Alignment Match:** All followers broadly match the PC's alignment
- **Triggering Conditions:** PC must offer fair taxes, decent wages, just judgment (for good/neutral fighters); or chances to oppress/extort (for evil fighters)
