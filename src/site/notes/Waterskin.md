## Stats

- **Capacity:** 3 pints
- **Cost:** typically a few sp
- **Weight:** 1 lb empty / 4 lb full
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A leather flask for carrying drinking water, sized to a 3-pint capacity. A character typically needs to drink at least one waterskin per day in mild weather, more in heat.
