## Notes

Ancestral Foes is the [[Gnome\|gnomish]] (and by extension [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]]) offensive trait against their hereditary enemies. When attacking [[Kobold\|Kobold]]s or [[Goblin\|Goblin]]s, the gnome may choose to add +1 to their [[To-Hit Roll\|To-Hit Roll]]. This is a narrower version of the [[Dwarf\|dwarven]] [[Grudge Bearer\|Grudge Bearer]] trait, which covers four humanoid types instead of two.

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** +1 to-hit
- **Applies vs:** [[Kobold\|Kobold]]s, [[Goblin\|Goblin]]s
- **Who has it:** [[Gnome\|Gnome]], [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]]
- **Stacks** with other to-hit bonuses
