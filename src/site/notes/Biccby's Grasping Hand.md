## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Magical hand
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates a powerful, magical hand similar to that of the magic-user spell [[Biccby's Forceful Hand\|Biccby's Forceful Hand]].

## Details

The hand can move a creature weighing 1,000 lbs or less at a rate of 10ft per round.

Alternatively, it can hold a creature up to 1,000 lbs in place, preventing the creature from taking any action.

It can reduce the speed of a creature between 1,001 and 4,000 lbs to no greater than 10ft per round.

It can slow the movement of creatures heavier than 4,000 lbs but smaller than 16,000 lbs to one-half.

Creatures weighing more than 16,000 pounds are not affected.

Biccby's grasping hand has as many [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] as the caster.
