## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th (Cleric) / 7th (Illusionist) / 9th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Transmutation\|Divine Transmutation]] / [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** Affected creatures, with objects and equipment worn or carried by those creatures (one to six creatures)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 3 turns (Cleric/Illusionist) / 9 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell transports avatars of the caster and up to five other creatures to the [[Astral Plane\|Astral Plane]], leaving the physical bodies behind.

## Details

This spell only transports willing creatures. Unwilling creatures are unaffected.

Most magic items are weakened or suppressed while on the [[Astral Plane\|Astral Plane]], and some spells work differently.

Affected creatures retain their physical bodies on the Prime Material Plane. The bodies are unconscious and incapacitated until the spell ends.

This spell creates astral avatars, which look exactly like the caster's physical bodies on the Prime Material. Each creature's avatar is connected to its physical body on the Prime Material by a silver cord. The silver cord is invisible and invulnerable to most kinds of damage, but can be severed by deities and creatures of similar power, by certain rare weather effects on the Astral Plane, and by a very limited number of special weapons and powers. If the silver cord is cut, the creature's astral avatar disappears and its physical body dies.

The astral avatar can enter other planes from the Astral, but it can't enter a plane where the caster has a physical body (other than to return into the original body).
