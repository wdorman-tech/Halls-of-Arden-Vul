## Notes

A character may **set a weapon against a charge** by bracing the weapon against the floor or some stationary object. A character setting a weapon cannot attack unless an opponent charges, but if such a charge occurs, **the set weapon attacks first** and **inflicts double damage** on a successful hit against the charging opponent.

If the action is declared, the weapon is set immediately in **segment 1** of the round.

A "[[Charge\|Charge]]" for this purpose includes any attack that lets the attacker move and attack — including some monsters' leaping attacks.

## Key Facts

- **Weapons that can be set:** spears, javelins, lances (when dismounted), pole arms, tridents
- **When set:** segment 1 of the round (immediately on declaration)
- **Effect:** attack before the charger; **double damage** on hit
- **Restriction:** cannot attack non-charging opponents this round
- **Counters:** [[Charge\|Charge]] action; some monster leaping attacks
