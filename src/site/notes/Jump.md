## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the subject to make one or more huge leaps. The recipient of the spell may bound forward 30ft, or may leap backward or straight up for a distance of 10ft. The leap is almost perfectly straight, with no arc.

## Details

The spell allows one leap, plus an additional one for every three magic-user levels the caster has above first level. Thus, at a caster level of 4, the spell permits a second leap, with additional leaps at level 7, 10, 13, etc.
