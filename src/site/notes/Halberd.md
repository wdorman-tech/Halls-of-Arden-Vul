## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee (Reach)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d10 vs S/M, 2d6 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 9
- **Space Required:** 5ft
- **Length:** 5ft
- **Damage Type:** Piercing or Slashing (wielder's choice)
- **Weight:** 15 lb
- **Cost:** 9 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A two-handed pole weapon combining axe-blade, spike, and hook. Can be used as a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]] from the second rank, and inflicts double damage when [[Set Weapon Against Charge\|set against charge]].

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
