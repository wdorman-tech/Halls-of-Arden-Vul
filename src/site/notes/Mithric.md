## Notes

Mithric is an ancient language primarily used by **magic researchers**. It is not a living mother tongue but a scholarly and arcane lingua franca for tomes, ritual texts, and old inscriptions found throughout the dungeon and its environs. A magic-user (especially one from the [[Collegium of Macrina\|Collegium of Macrina]]) gains real research advantage from learning Mithric.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** No native speakers; magical scholars and researchers
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left; available as a language swap at character creation
- **Setting role:** Reading ancient magical inscriptions, deciphering tomes, understanding ritual instructions in the Halls of Arden Vul
