## Notes

When a hit is scored, **damage** is rolled (per the weapon, spell, or attack form) and subtracted from the target's [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]]. OSRIC distinguishes several damage types — important when a creature is immune or resistant to certain kinds.

## Key Facts

**Normal Damage Types:**
- **Blunt:** weapons like staffs and clubs (not bladed/pointed)
- **Piercing:** stabbing weapons (daggers, spears, arrows)
- **Slashing:** cutting weapons (swords, axes)
- **Temporary:** damage usually from [[Unarmed Combat\|Unarmed Combat]]; non-living monsters are immune. Recovers at 1 hp/round.

**Other Damage Types** (these usually bypass "immune to non-magical weapons"):
- **Cold:** e.g. cone of cold
- **Corrosive:** acid
- **Electrical:** lightning bolts, etc.
- **Fire:** includes heat damage even without flame
- **Magical:** spells like spiritual weapon that bypass damage-type immunities
- **Poison:** some poisons inflict damage rather than instant death
