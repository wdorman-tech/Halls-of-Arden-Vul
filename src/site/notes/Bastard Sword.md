## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 15+)
- **Damage:** 2d4 vs S/M, 2d8 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 6
- **Space Required:** 4ft
- **Length:** 4.5ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Weight:** 10 lb
- **Cost:** 25 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item — Player Doc lists "claymore" as 2 slots)

## Notes

A "hand-and-a-half" sword, designed to be wielded with either one or two hands. One-handed use requires STR 15+ to avoid the [[Weapon Proficiency\|non-proficiency penalty]].

Also called a **claymore**.

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
