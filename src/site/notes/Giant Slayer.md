## Notes

Giant Slayer is the defensive trait shared by [[Dwarf\|dwarves]] and [[Gnome\|gnomes]] (and [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]]s) for fighting very large humanoids. When a giant tries to hit one of these characters in melee, that giant attacks at -4 (or equivalently, the dwarf/gnome's [[Armor Class\|Armor Class]] effectively improves by 4 against the attack). The GM decides which creatures count as "giants" — typically [[Giant\|Giant]]s, [[Titan\|Titan]]s, [[Troll\|Troll]]s, and similar very large humanoids.

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** giants attack at -4 to hit (or AC improves by 4 vs that attack)
- **Triggers vs:** giants, titans, trolls, and other very large humanoids at GM discretion
- **Who has it:** [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]], [[Gnome\|Gnome]], [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]]
- **Defensive only** — does not improve the dwarf/gnome's own attack rolls
