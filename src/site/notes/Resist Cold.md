## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Abjuration\|Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell protects you or one targeted creature from cold, which includes certain protections against magical cold in combat situations.

## Details

The warded creature is safe from non-magical cold, and is comfortable in lightweight clothing at low temperatures. If the temperature drops below arctic levels, the target creature begins to feel chilly.

The warded creature gains all of the following benefits:

1. All cold damage the warded creature takes is halved. If the damage is already halved (e.g. for a passed [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]]), it's halved again. Round fractions down.

2. The warded creature can choose to attempt a saving throw against any magical effect that deals cold damage, even if that effect doesn't normally allow a saving throw. If there's doubt about which kind of saving throw to use, it's against spells and other magical effects.

3. When the warded attempts a saving throw against cold damage, it can choose to add +3 to its saving throw.
