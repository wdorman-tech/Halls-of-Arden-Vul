## Notes

[[Tracking\|Tracking]] (Cunning Tracker) is the [[Ranger\|Ranger]]'s signature wilderness skill — following a creature's tracks. The base success chance is high in the wild and drops in cities or dungeons; the GM modifies it further based on conditions.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)
- **Mechanics:** Percentile roll
- **Base Chances:**
    - Rural or wilderness settings: 90%
    - Urban or dungeon settings: 65%
- **GM Modifiers:** Adjusted based on:
    - Age of the trail
    - Prevailing terrain
    - Weather
    - Light conditions
    - How many other creatures have passed since
