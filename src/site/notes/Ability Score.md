## Notes

Ability scores are the six numeric attributes that represent a character's personal qualities in [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]]: [[Strength\|Strength]], [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]], [[Constitution\|Constitution]], [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]], [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]], and [[Charisma\|Charisma]]. Each is rolled at character creation in a range of 3 to 18 (with the special case of 19 for fighters/paladins/rangers rolling exceptional [[Strength\|Strength]]). Higher numbers are better, but only certain scores grant mechanical bonuses — most rolls in the middle of the range are merely "average" and deliver no modifiers.

A character's class will care most about its **prime requisite(s)**, the score(s) that govern its core abilities. Ancestry, age, and magical effects can adjust scores beyond the initial range.

## Key Facts

- **Range:** 3-18 (19 only via exceptional Strength for [[Fighter\|Fighter]]s, [[Paladin\|Paladin]]s, [[Ranger\|Ranger]]s)
- **Generation:** See [[Modified Character Creation\|Modified Character Creation]] — this campaign uses 3d6 in order with a single swap allowed
- **Six scores:** [[Strength\|Strength]], [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]], [[Constitution\|Constitution]], [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]], [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]], [[Charisma\|Charisma]]
- **Prime requisites by class:**
  - [[Strength\|Strength]]: [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
  - [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]]: [[Thief\|Thief]], [[Assassin\|Assassin]]
  - [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]]: [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Druid\|Druid]]
  - [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]]: [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Mental attributes do NOT substitute for player skill** — puzzles are solved by player thinking, not Intelligence checks.
