## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Elemental creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By means of this spell, the caster calls an elemental being from the plane of air, fire, earth or water to do their bidding. The particular form of elemental to be summoned is a part of the spell's incantation, so the caster must specify the type of elemental when memorising the spell.

## Details

The caster exerts control over the elemental by concentrating upon it, and if their [[Concentration\|Concentration]] is broken, they lose control of the elemental. The elemental does not depart until the duration of the spell ends, and the caster cannot dismiss it before that time unless it is under their control.

The caster's ability to control the elemental is limited to 30ft. If the elemental leaves that radius it is not freed, but the caster can't give it orders.

There is a 1 in 20 chance per round (after the second round of the spell) that the elemental will break free of the caster's control even if they are concentrating upon maintaining it.

If the elemental breaks free of control or if the caster ceases to concentrate upon it, it will seek to attack the caster as soon as it can finish any combat in which it is involved. The caster can avail themselves of protections against an elemental such as the use of a magic circle or a [[Protection From Evil\|Protection From Evil]] spell.

The material component for this spell is a large quantity of the appropriate elemental substance.
