## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 6 turns per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** One person
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell increases a person's strength.

## Details

This spell only affects creatures that are bipedal (human, demi-human, or humanoid-like) and size Medium or smaller.

The spell recipient's [[Strength\|Strength]] increases by 1d6, and members of the various fighter-type classes (fighters, rangers, paladins) gain a +1 to this roll.

If the spell is cast upon a monster (such as an orc), the GM is free to rule for convenience that the effect of the spell is to grant +1 to damage, and if the d6 roll is a 5 or 6, a +1 "to hit" as well.
