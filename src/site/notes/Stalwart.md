## Notes

Stalwart is the [[Constitution\|Constitution]]-scaled [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] bonus shared by [[Dwarf\|dwarves]], [[Gnome\|gnomes]], and [[Halfling\|halflings]] (and by extension [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]]s). It applies against three save categories: aimed [[Magic Item\|Magic Item]]s, [[Poison\|Poison]], and [[Spell\|Spell]]s / magical effects. Higher Constitution gives a bigger bonus, capping at +5 for CON 18-19.

## Key Facts

- **Applies to saves vs:** (1) aimed [[Magic Item\|Magic Item]]s, (2) [[Poison\|Poison]], (3) [[Spell\|Spell]]s and magical effects
- **Bonus by [[Constitution\|Constitution]]:**
  - CON 4-6: +1
  - CON 7-10: +2
  - CON 11-13: +3
  - CON 14-17: +4
  - CON 18-19: +5
- **Who has it:** [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]], [[Gnome\|Gnome]], [[Halfling\|Halfling]] (and [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]] via Gnome statline)
- **Note:** the [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]] version of this resilience is called [[Half-Orc Resilience\|Half-Orc Resilience]] and uses different mechanics
