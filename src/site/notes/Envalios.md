## Notes

Envalios is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic neutral god of battle, mercenaries, and fire as pitiless destruction. Where [[Atashak\|Atashak]] is fire as imperial sacrifice and [[Rudiana\|Rudiana]] is battle as berserker rage, Envalios is the cold professional with a torch in one hand and a contract in the other. His symbol is a crow; his weapon, the battle axe.
