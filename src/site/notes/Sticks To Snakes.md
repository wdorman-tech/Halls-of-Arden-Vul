## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 4th (Cleric) / 5th (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]] / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 30ft (Cleric) / 40ft (Druid)
- **Duration:** 2 rounds for each level you have in the cleric class (Cleric) / 2 rounds per druid level (Druid)
- **Area of Effect:** A 10ft cube; maximum of 1 stick per cleric level (Cleric) / 5ft radius (Druid)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell transforms dead wood into serpents. The serpents don't understand speech, but if you mentally command them to attack a target, they obey.

For each snake you create, roll a d20. If the result is equal to or less than the number of levels you have in the cleric (or druid) class, then the snake is venomous. Otherwise, it is a constrictor.

## Details

Unless you are in a forest, it's a good idea to bring your own sticks. This spell only affects roughly cylindrical objects made of dead wood, with a maximum of your cleric (or druid) level. The target objects can't be magical.

**Venomous Snake:** AC 6, Mv 90ft, HD 2, #AT 1, dam 1 piercing, SA poison (save vs poison or die, apply CON modifier to save), Size S, AL N.

**Constrictor Snake:** AC 6, Mv 90ft, HD 2, #AT 1, dam 1 blunt, SA constrict (once hit, target is being constricted until snake releases or is killed; snake can only constrict one target at a time; snake can choose to inflict 1d4 blunt damage to a constricted target on its initiative segment instead of attacking), Size S, AL N.

Killing or defeating snakes called by this spell doesn't award any xp.
