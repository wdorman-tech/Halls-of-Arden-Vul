## Concept

A one-handed weapon can be wielded with a single hand, allowing the wielder to also carry a [[Shield, Small\|shield]] in the off-hand or to dual-wield in some cases.

## Common One-Handed Weapons

- [[Hand Axe\|Hand Axe]]
- [[Club\|Club]]
- [[Dagger\|Dagger]]
- [[Flail, Light\|Flail, Light]]
- [[Hammer\|Hammer]] (light)
- [[Javelin\|Javelin]]
- [[Lance\|Lance]]
- [[Mace, Light\|Mace, Light]]
- [[Pick, Light\|Pick, Light]]
- [[Long Sword\|Long Sword]]
- [[Scimitar\|Scimitar]]
- [[Short Sword\|Short Sword]]
- [[Warhammer, Light\|Warhammer, Light]]
- [[Spear\|Spear]] (1 or 2)

## Optional One-Handed (with Strength)

Some weapons are normally two-handed but can be wielded in one hand if the wielder meets a STR minimum. See [[Two-Handed Weapon\|Two-Handed Weapon]].
