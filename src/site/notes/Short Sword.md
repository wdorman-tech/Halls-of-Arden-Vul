## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d8 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 3
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 2ft
- **Damage Type:** Piercing or Slashing (wielder's choice)
- **Weight:** 3 lb
- **Cost:** 8 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A fast, short blade — the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s favoured sword. Permitted to most martial classes; **not** usable by [[Cleric\|Cleric]] or [[Druid\|Druid]] (edged).

The fast speed factor (3) gives short swords a major edge in tied initiative.
