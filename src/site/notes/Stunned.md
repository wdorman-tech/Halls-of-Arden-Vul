## Notes

**Stunning** usually refers to being **mentally stunned**, as per a [[Power Word, Stun\|Power Word, Stun]] spell, but some physical impacts, sights, or loud noises can cause mental stunning.

A stunned creature **cannot move or take any actions**. They cannot use shields, gain no [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] bonus to AC, and may be attacked at **+4 to hit**. However, they are not entirely helpless — they must be attacked normally (no auto-hit).

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** no actions; no shield; no DEX bonus to AC; attacked at +4
- **Not helpless:** must be attacked normally (no auto-hit like [[Paralyzed\|held]] or [[Unconscious\|Unconscious]])
- **Concentration:** broken if affected by stun while spellcasting
- **Duration:** spell-dependent (e.g. [[Power Word, Stun\|Power Word, Stun]] varies by HP)
- **Removal:** end of duration, [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]] for some sources
- **Stacks with:** other conditions
- **Common sources:** [[Power Word, Stun\|Power Word, Stun]], certain monster attacks, magical traps, environmental hazards (loud blast)
