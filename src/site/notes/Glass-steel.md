## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** One object
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell grants a glass object the strength of steel, while retaining its transparent properties. The weight of the object can be no more than 10 lbs per caster level.

## Details

In most cases, the object will make item [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s on the more favourable of the glass or metal tables.
