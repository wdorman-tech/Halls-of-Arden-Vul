## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** 50ft + 10ft per illusionist level
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (lessened effect)

## Notes

This spell employs the caster's ability to draw upon the shadow planes, adding an element of quasi-reality to an illusory spell. The "spell" may be one of a limited group: [[Cone of Cold\|Cone of Cold]], [[Fireball\|Fireball]], [[Lightning Bolt\|Lightning Bolt]], or [[Magic Missile\|Magic Missile]].

## Details

Anyone who would be damaged by the spell is entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against the illusion, but does not receive a second saving throw against the illusionary spell effect.

- Anyone who succeeds at the saving throw against the illusion (vs. spells) takes 1 hit point of psychic damage per caster level.
- Anyone who fails the saving throw takes the full damage that the illusionary spell would inflict, but the damage is psychic damage. Thus, a creature who is immune to fire would still be damaged by a shadow "fireball".
