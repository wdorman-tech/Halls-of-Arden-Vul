## Notes

Characters who fall into a pit, off a wall, or over a cliff take damage based on the height of the fall.

## Key Facts

| Distance | Damage |
|---|---|
| Less than 10 ft | none (in game terms) |
| 10 ft | 1d6 |
| 20 ft | 3d6 |
| 30 ft | 6d6 |
| 40 ft | 10d6 |
| 50 ft | 15d6 |
| Over 50 ft | 20d6 |

- **Optional:** the GM may allow a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against falling damage; success halves the damage
- **Item saves:** a separate [[Item Saving Throw\|Item Saving Throw]] is made for every 10 ft fallen unless the character lands on something soft (only one save total in that case)
