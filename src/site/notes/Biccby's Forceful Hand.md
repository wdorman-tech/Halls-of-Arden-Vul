## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates a powerful, magical hand similar to that of the magic-user spell [[Biccby's Interposing Hand\|Biccby's Interposing Hand]]. Unlike the interposing hand, the forceful hand can push opponents or objects directly away from the caster.

## Details

The hand can move a creature weighing 500 lbs or less at a rate of 10ft per round.

It reduces the speed of a creature between 500 and 2,000 lbs to no greater than 10ft per round.

It slows the movement of creatures heavier than 2,000 lbs but smaller than 8,000 lbs to one-half.

Creatures weighing more than 8,000 pounds are not affected.

As in the case of [[Biccby's Interposing Hand\|Biccby's Interposing Hand]], Biccby's forceful hand has as many [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] as the caster.
