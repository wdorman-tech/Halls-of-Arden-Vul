## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous (see below)
- **Area of Effect:** 1-4 creatures in a 20ft square area
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This extremely potent spell causes the victims to forget events of the immediate past. The spell obliterates memory of the last round plus one additional round per three caster levels.

## Details

The caster decides which creatures in the spell's area to affect, and these must make [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s or suffer the loss of memory.

The number of creatures targeted affects the saving throw modifier. The caster may designate 1-4 creatures as the targets of the spell. If one is targeted, the saving throw is made with a -2 penalty; if two are targeted, the penalty is -1; and if 3 or 4 creatures are targeted they suffer no penalty to the saving throw.

Lost memories can only be restored by [[Heal\|Heal]], [[Restoration\|Restoration]], or [[Wish\|Wish]]; the spell's effect is not subject to being merely dispelled.
