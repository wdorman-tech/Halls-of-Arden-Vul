## Notes

OSRIC uses dice with various numbers of sides, abbreviated with shorthand notation like "2d10," "3d6," or "1d10." The "d" means "die" or "dice," and the number after the "d" indicates the number of sides. The number before the "d" (if any) indicates how many of those dice to roll and add together.

## Key Facts

- **Standard dice needed:** at least one each of d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, and d20
- **d100 (or d%) notation:** roll 2d10, read first as tens and second as units; "00, 00" reads as 100
- **Saving throws:** rolled on a d20
- **Wandering monster checks:** typically 1 in 6 (d6)
- **Surprise checks:** d6
- **Initiative:** d6
- **Reaction rolls:** d100
