## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st (Illusionist) / 2nd (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Divination\|Phantasmal Divination]] / [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 5 rounds per illusionist level / 5 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast (Illusionist) / 2 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to see things that are invisible or hidden, and also to see into the astral and ethereal planes.

## Details

The spell's sight is limited to a range of 10ft per caster level. For Magic-User version, the spell's sight is limited to a path 10ft wide, with a length of 10ft per MU level.
