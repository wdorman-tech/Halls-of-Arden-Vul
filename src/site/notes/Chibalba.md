## Notes

Chibalba is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the True Neutral **Lord of Caves** — god of the underworld, slow death, and savage beauty. He is the patron of the deep places — natural caverns, lightless rivers — and of those who approach death not with cruelty but with reverence for its inexorable beauty. His symbol is a barred gate; his weapon, the scythe.
