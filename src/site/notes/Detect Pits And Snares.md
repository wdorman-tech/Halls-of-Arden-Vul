## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Divination\|Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 4 rounds per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** Path 10ft wide, 40ft long
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows you to magically perceive virtually any form of trap while outdoors. While indoors or underground, you can still detect the presence of pit traps, but not other types of traps.
