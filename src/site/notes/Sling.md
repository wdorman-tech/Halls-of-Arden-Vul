## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d4+1 vs S/M, 1d6+1 vs L (with bullet); 1d4 / 1d4 (with stone)
- **Damage Type:** Blunt
- **Range Increment:** 35ft
- **Rate of Fire:** 1 per round
- **Weight:** 0.5 lb
- **Cost:** 5 sp
- **Inventory Slots:** 0 or 1 (light item)

## Notes

A leather thong used to hurl stones or lead bullets. Uses one hand, so a [[Shield, Small\|shield]] is possible.

Permitted for [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (blunt). [[Sling Bullet\|Sling Bullet]]s do more damage than improvised [[Sling Stone\|Sling Stone]]s.

## Ammunition

- [[Sling Bullet\|Sling Bullet]] (lead) — 4/dozen weight, 1 gp/dozen
- [[Sling Stone\|Sling Stone]] — 2/dozen weight, found
