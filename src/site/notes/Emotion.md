## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Enchantment\|Phantasmal Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 10ft per illusionist level
- **Duration:** [[Concentration\|Concentration]]
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 40ft x 40ft
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

By casting this spell, the caster is able to instil others with one of four powerful emotions. The emotion is chosen when the spell is cast, and each has a different effect.

## Details

**Fear:** If the caster chooses to instil fear, the spell's effect is similar to that of the [[Fear\|Fear]] spell, but with a -2 penalty applied to [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s.

**Hate:** If the caster instils hate, the targets gain a +2 to saving throws, attacks, and damage.

**Hopelessness:** If the caster instils hopelessness, the target creatures who fail the save wander sadly away, or surrender in the face of a challenge such as a combat.

**Rage:** If the caster instils rage, the affected creatures attack at +1, gain a damage bonus of +3, and gain a bonus of 5 temporary hit points. Temporary hit points are lost first if the creature incurs damage. The affected creature will not willingly retreat from any combat or any opponent.
