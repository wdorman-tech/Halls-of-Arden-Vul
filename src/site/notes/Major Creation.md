## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** 6 turns per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** 1 cubic ft per illusionist level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

With a small piece of material, the caster may use this spell to create an object made of that same material. The base material cannot be alive, but unlike [[Minor Creation\|Minor Creation]] it does not need to be plant matter.

## Details

Within the level-based limit on the item's volume, the caster could create leather armour from a piece of leather, a door or club from a splinter of wood, a sword from a piece of steel, etc. The item exists only for the duration of the spell, at which time it turns back to the small piece of basic material.
