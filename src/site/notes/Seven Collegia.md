## Notes

Every [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] in the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]] must be a graduate of one of the Seven Collegia — any other place of arcane learning is outlawed. This makes a magic-user's collegium a defining piece of identity, almost like a class subtype: it indicates not just what spells they were taught but what philosophical and political circles they move in.

Player Doc rule: every magic-user PC chooses one of the Seven Collegia where they learned their magic.

## The Seven

| Collegium | Specialty | Reputation |
|---|---|---|
| [[The Order of Thoth\|The Order of Thoth]] | Generalist | Oldest, connected to nobility |
| [[The College of Perception\|The College of Perception]] | Illusion, mentalism, charm/sleep | Home of nearly all illusionists |
| [[The Imperial Academy\|The Imperial Academy]] | Generalist (small, elite) | Politically influential, upper nobility |
| [[Collegium of Macrina\|Collegium of Macrina]] | Research, theory, new spell creation | Large, academic |
| [[New School of Arcane Might\|New School of Arcane Might]] | Generalist or necromancy, might-makes-right | Disliked by the public |
| [[Order of the Fifth Circle\|Order of the Fifth Circle]] | Summoning, outer planes research | Specialist |
| [[Collegium of Cinders\|Collegium of Cinders]] | Elementalism, battle magic | Tied to the Archontean military |
