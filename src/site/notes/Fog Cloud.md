## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** 4 rounds + 1 round per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** 40ft x 20ft x 20ft cloud
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates a roiling mass of gas and vapour, similar in appearance to a [[Cloudkill\|Cloudkill]] spell. The fog cloud moves away from the caster in the direction indicated at a speed of 10ft per round.

## Details

Vision inside and into the fog is limited to 2ft.
