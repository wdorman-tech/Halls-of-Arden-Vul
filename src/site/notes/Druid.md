## Notes

The [[Druid\|Druid]] is a priest of nature — a sub-class of [[Cleric\|Cleric]] who serves nature gods, totem spirits, or kami rather than civilised pantheons. Druids draw on [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] and [[Charisma\|Charisma]] in equal measure, wielding power over fire, stone, weather, plants, and animals through [[Druid Spellcasting\|Druid Spellcasting]] cast with mistletoe, holly, oak leaves, or other [[Magical Plants\|sacred plants]] harvested by sickle. Advancement above level 11 is contested through the [[Hierophant\|druidic hierarchy]].

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 6, CON 6, INT 6, WIS 12, CHA 15
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] and [[Charisma\|Charisma]] (10% XP bonus if both are 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d8 (max 14)
- **Alignment:** Neutral only
- **Armour:** Leather armour and wooden shields only
- **Weapons:** Club, dagger, dart, hammer, scimitar, sling, spear, staff, torch (and flaming oil for some faiths)
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 2 (additional at level 4, 7, 10, and 13)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -4 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 3d6 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]], [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]] (per ancestry rules)
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Class Abilities:** [[Druid Spellcasting\|Druid Spellcasting]], [[Druidic\|Druidic]] (Druids' Cant), [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] +2 vs fire/lightning
- **Bonus Spell Slots:** [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] 13+ grants extra spell slots
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 3 — [[Identify Plants and Animals\|Druid's Knowledge]] and [[Pass Without Trace\|Wilderness Movement]]; Lvl 7 — Immune to Fey Charm, [[Druid Shape-Shift\|Shapeshift]], Druidic Craft; Lvl 12 — High Druid; Lvl 13 — Archdruid, Druidic Power; Lvl 14 — Supreme Druid
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,000 XP; Level 12 at 300,000 XP (High Druid); Level 13 at 750,000 XP (Archdruid); Level 14 at 1,500,000 XP (Supreme Druid)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Druids do not build strongholds. They may occupy a magical grove at any level. Followers are local plants, animals, and birds — no human followers.
