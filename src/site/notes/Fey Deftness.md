## Notes

Fey Deftness is the [[Elf\|elven]] mastery of their signature weapons. When an elf attacks with any [[Pulled Bow\|Pulled Bow]] (longbow, shortbow, composite bow — any non-mechanical bow drawn by hand) or with a [[Short Sword\|Short Sword]] or [[Long Sword\|Long Sword]], they add +1 to their [[To-Hit Roll\|To-Hit Roll]]. Note that this does not apply to [[Crossbow\|Crossbow]]s or to the [[Halfling\|Halfling]]'s preferred [[Sling\|Sling]].

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** +1 to-hit
- **Applies with:** any [[Pulled Bow\|Pulled Bow]] (longbow, shortbow, composite bow), [[Short Sword\|Short Sword]], [[Long Sword\|Long Sword]]
- **Does not apply to:** [[Crossbow\|Crossbow]]s, [[Sling\|Sling]]s, two-handed swords, or other weapons
- **Who has it:** [[Elf\|Elf]] only (not [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]])
- **Stacks** with other to-hit bonuses
