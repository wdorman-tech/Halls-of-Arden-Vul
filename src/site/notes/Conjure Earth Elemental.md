## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Conjuration\|Conjuration/Summoning]]
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** 1 turn per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell summons forth and controls an elemental being from the plane of earth.

## Details

The elemental is a 16 HD elemental from the plane of earth.

The elemental will act as your ally, remaining for the spell's duration to assist you however possible, including engaging in combat with your enemies. Druids need no protective circle to summon earth elementals and need not concentrate upon the elemental in any way.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Dismiss Earth Elemental\|Dismiss Earth Elemental]].
