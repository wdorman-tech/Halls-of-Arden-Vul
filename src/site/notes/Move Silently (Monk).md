## Notes

[[Move Silently (Monk)\|Move Silently (Monk)]] is one of several thief skills granted to the [[Monk\|Monk]] class. The monk's progression has its own table (Table 1.3.7.4B) — generally aggressive growth, surpassing thief percentages at high levels.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll, succeed if rolled equal to or under the listed percentage
- **Modifiers:** May be modified by [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] (Table 1.3.10.4C — Thief Skill Dexterity Adjustments)
