## Concept

Long-hafted, pointed weapons can be used as **reach weapons** — attacking from the second rank of battle "through" an ally while protected from attacks by that same ally.

## Reach Weapons

- [[Spear\|Spear]]
- [[Javelin\|Javelin]]
- [[Lance\|Lance]] (when used dismounted)
- [[Pole Arm\|Pole Arm]] (and variants — [[Halberd\|Halberd]], etc.)
- [[Trident\|Trident]]

## Set Against Charge

Long-hafted, pointed reach weapons inflict **double damage** when set to receive a charge if the foe actually charges.

## Lance Charge Bonus

A [[Lance\|Lance]] inflicts **double damage** when used by a character riding a charging heavy warhorse or similar steed. If the attacker is on a normal riding or cavalry horse, the GM may reduce or remove this bonus.
