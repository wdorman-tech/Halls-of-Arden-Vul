## Notes

Melkarton is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the neutral good god of the sea, travel, merchants, and fair dealing. He is the patron sailors offer their first catch to and the patron travelers pray to before a long road. His symbol is a manta ray; his weapon, the long sword.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]
- **Alignment:** [[Neutral Good\|Neutral Good]]
- **Gender:** M
- **Areas of Influence:** The sea, travel, merchants, fair dealing
- **Symbol(s):** Manta ray
- **Weapon(s):** Long sword
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Neutral Good\|Neutral Good]]
