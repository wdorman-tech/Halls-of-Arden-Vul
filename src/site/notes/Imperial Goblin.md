## Notes

In the [[Halls of Arden Vul\|Halls of Arden Vul]] setting, Imperial Goblins replace [[Gnome\|Gnome]]s entirely — gnomes do not exist here. Mechanically, an Imperial Goblin character uses every Gnome statistic (size, movement, traits, classes, level limits, lifespan), with **one change**: they learn an additional language instead of [[Gnomish\|Gnomish]]. See [[Gnome\|Gnome]] for the full stat block; this page exists to flag the houserule and the language swap.

## Key Facts

- **Stats: identical to [[Gnome\|Gnome]]** — refer to that page for size, movement, ability requirements, classes, multi-class options, level limits, age, and trait list
- **Language change:** drop [[Gnomish\|Gnomish]] from the gnome language list; add one additional language of the player's choice (subject to GM approval)
- **Ancestral traits:** [[Infravision\|Infravision]] (60ft), [[Stalwart\|Stalwart]], [[Stone-Kenning\|Stone-Kenning]] (gnomish percentages), [[Giant Slayer\|Giant Slayer]], [[Ancestral Foes\|Ancestral Foes]]
- **Available classes:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]], [[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Multi-class options:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Illusionist\|Illusionist]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Thief\|Thief]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]/[[Thief\|Thief]]
