## Stats

- **Cost:** 1 cp each
- **Weight:** 1 lb
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 (per several)

## Combat (when used as weapon)

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d4 vs S/M, 1d3 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 4
- **Space Required:** 2ft
- **Length:** 3ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt or Fire (wielder's choice)

## Notes

A pitch-coated stick that burns to provide [[Light Sources\|light]]. **Torches burn for 6 turns**. After 6 turns the torch is burnt out — and a burnt-out torch is not usable as a weapon.

A lit torch can also be used as an improvised melee weapon — useful when fighting [[Undead\|Undead]], [[Troll\|Troll]]s, or other creatures vulnerable to fire. Special: can ignite [[Flask of Oil\|Flask of Oil]] spilled on a target (AC 10 [10] for moving targets).
