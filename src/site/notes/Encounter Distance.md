## Notes

In general, the distance between two groups when combat begins is **1d4+4 x 10 ft (or yards, if outdoors)**, **unless** either side is [[Surprise\|surprised]]. If either side is surprised, the encounter takes place at the closer range of **1d3 x 10 ft (or yards)**.

The GM may modify this based on circumstances: ambushes, terrain, line of sight, light sources, etc.

## Key Facts

- **No surprise:** 1d4+4 x 10 ft/yards (range 50-80)
- **Surprise:** 1d3 x 10 ft/yards (range 10-30)
- **Outdoors:** measured in yards (3x the indoor distance in feet)
- **GM modifies:** based on terrain, ambushes, line of sight, etc.
- **Light:** approaching light gives intelligent monsters warning before visual contact
- **First step:** part of [[Order of Events (Combat)\|Order of Events (Combat)]] step 1
