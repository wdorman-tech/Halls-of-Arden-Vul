## Notes

The Archontean Empire is the political body whose recent expansion onto the continent forms the backdrop to the campaign. Its dominant culture is [[Archontean\|Archontean]], its primary language [[Archontean (language)\|Archontean (language)]], and its state religion centers on [[The Ten\|The Ten]] (with [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]] treated as minor or regional gods). The empire regulates arcane study tightly: only the [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]] are legal schools of magic, and any [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] who learned elsewhere is a criminal.

The empire is also the patron of the [[Imperial Academy\|Imperial Academy]] and (more directly militarily) the [[Collegium of Cinders\|Collegium of Cinders]]. Imperial power and politics are mirrored in the worship of [[Basileus\|Basileus]], the deified Emperor.

## Key Facts

- **Recent history:** Has begun spreading across this continent in the present generation
- **Native peoples encountered:** [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] (rural), [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] (northern)
- **State religion:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]]; [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]] are minor by Archontean reckoning
- **Magical authority:** Only the [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]] are legal arcane institutions
- **Patron deity of imperial authority:** [[Basileus\|Basileus]]
