## Notes

A dual-classed character has two classes and advances in one of them at a time, switching from the first to the second at some point in their career. Dual-classing is exclusive to [[Human\|Human]] characters and requires very high ability scores. Once switched, the character starts at level 1 in the new class and cannot use their old class's abilities without forfeiting all XP for that adventure — until they exceed their old level in the new class.

## Houserule

**Dual-classing is NOT used in this campaign.** Player characters in the Halls of Arden Vul campaign select a single class and advance only in that class — see the Player Doc for full campaign houserules.

## Key Facts

- **Eligibility:** [[Human\|Human]] only
- **Ability Score Requirement:** At least 15 in the Prime Requisite(s) of the old class AND at least 17 in the Prime Requisite(s) of the new class
- **Hit Points:** Keep your old hp; gain no new hp until you exceed your old class's level in the new class
- **XP:** All XP goes to the new class
- **Old Class Abilities:** Cannot be used without forfeiting all XP for that adventure, unless and until your new class's level exceeds your old class's level
- **Restrictions After Switch:** Magic-user/illusionist spells still can't be cast in armour; druid spells require druid-allowed armour; thief skills require thief-allowed armour (except [[Read Languages\|Read Languages]]); monk abilities require no armour (except monkish thief skills)
