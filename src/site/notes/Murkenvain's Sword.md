## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Summoned sword
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster evokes a magical sword into being, and can wield it by exerting their willpower (the caster does not need to wield the sword; it flies and attacks where they direct). While using the sword, the caster cannot cast spells, use magic items, or move, although the spell does not end if the caster's [[Concentration\|Concentration]] is broken; the sword simply does not attack during that round.

## Details

The sword attacks as a fighter one-half the caster's magic user level, always hits on a natural 19 or 20, and can hit creatures that can ordinarily be hit only with magic weapons (up to +3). It can attack creatures in the astral or ethereal planes of existence.

The sword inflicts 5d4 hit points of slashing or piercing [[Damage\|Damage]] (the caster chooses) against Tiny to Medium size creatures, and 5d6 hit points against creatures larger than Medium.

The material components for the spell cost 500 gp or more.
