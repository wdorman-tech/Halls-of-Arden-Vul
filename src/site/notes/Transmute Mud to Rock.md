## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Druidic Transmutation\|Druidic Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 160ft (Druid) / 10ft per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** 20ft cube per druid level / 20ft cube per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments (Druid) / 5 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell transforms mud into sandstone or a similar sedimentary rock.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Transmute Rock to Mud\|Transmute Rock To Mud]] (or [[Transmute Rock to Mud\|Transmute Rock to Mud]]).
