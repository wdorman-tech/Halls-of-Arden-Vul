## Notes

Ellion is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic neutral goddess of the night, the moon, uncertainty, and financial ventures. She is the patron of the gambler and the moonlight investor alike. Her symbols are blindfolds and stilts (perhaps for navigating uncertainty literally and figuratively); her weapons are the dagger and the crossbow.
