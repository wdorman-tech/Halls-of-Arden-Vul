## Concept

Missile weapons attack at range. They are classified as either:

- **Hurled (H):** thrown by the user. You can add your Strength bonus to damage. (See [[Thrown Weapon\|Thrown Weapon]].)
- **Launched (L):** propelled by a mechanism (bow, crossbow, sling). No Strength bonus to damage by default. (See [[Pulled Bow\|Pulled Bow]] and [[Crossbow\|Crossbow]].)

In some campaigns, specially-made bows might allow Strength bonuses to arrow damage. These bows are sold at special cost or only as treasure.

## Range Increment

Missile weapon accuracy decreases with range. You take a **-2 penalty** to hit for **each increment after the first**.

- Example: longbow has a 70ft range increment. At 60ft, no penalty. At 80ft, -2 penalty.
- If the target is more than **five full range increments** away, you can't hit at all.
- Long bows can't reach beyond 350ft. Short bows can't reach beyond 250ft.
