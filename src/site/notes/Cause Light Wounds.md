## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st (Cleric) / 2nd (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]] / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S (cleric) / V, S, M (druid)
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments (cleric) / 4 segments (druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The touched target creature takes 1d8 hit points of [[Magical Damage\|Magical Damage]], even if it is immune to mundane weapons. You must be able to see and touch the target creature.

## Details

If you miss the to-hit roll on the target, no damage is inflicted and the spell is lost.

In general, the spell only affects normally living creatures. It has no effect on [[Golems\|Golems]], [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], or the [[Undead\|Undead]].

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Cure Light Wounds\|Cure Light Wounds]].
