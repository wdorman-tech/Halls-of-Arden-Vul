## Notes

A **hireling** is an NPC paid to perform a specific service — torchbearer, porter, mercenary, retainer. Hirelings differ from [[Henchman\|henchmen]] in that they're transactional: paid on a schedule, not personally loyal, and not character-class adventurers (or only minor ones).

In combat and dangerous situations, hirelings check [[Morale\|Morale]] like other NPCs. Their morale is affected by circumstances, the leader's [[Charisma\|Charisma]], and quality of leadership (Section 1.7.4: Loyalty of Hirelings and Henchmen).

## Key Facts

- **Pay:** ongoing wages or per-job
- **Morale:** rolls like NPCs, see [[Morale\|Morale]]
- **Loyalty modifiers:** from [[Charisma\|Charisma]] of leader and circumstances
- **Examples:** men-at-arms, porters, torchbearers, ship's crew, guides
- **Distinct from:** [[Henchman\|henchmen]] (long-term, loyal, character-class)
