## Notes

**Charging into combat** allows the attacker to move **and** attack in the same [[Combat Round\|round]]. The attacker moves at twice their normal [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]] and must end the move within melee range (10 ft) of the target.

A successful charge grants **+2 to hit**. However, charging makes the attacker vulnerable: if the defender has a longer weapon, the defender attacks first, and the attacker gains no [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] bonus to AC during this attack (and characters without a DEX bonus take a -1 AC penalty). If the defender [[Set Weapon Against Charge\|sets a weapon against the charge]] and hits, they inflict **double damage**.

A **lance** wielded by an attacker on a **warhorse** or other combat-trained mount inflicts **double weapon damage** on the charge round. (Strength, magic, and specialisation bonuses are not doubled.)

## Key Facts

- **Speed:** 2x normal MR
- **Must end:** within 10 ft (melee range) of target
- **Bonus:** +2 to hit
- **Penalty if defender has longer weapon:** defender attacks first; charger has no DEX bonus to AC
- **Set against charge:** doubles the defender's damage on a hit
- **Lance + warhorse:** doubles weapon damage roll (strength/magic bonuses NOT doubled)
- **Frequency:** once per 10 rounds (i.e. once per turn)
- **Attack must be:** melee, hurled weapon, or unarmed
