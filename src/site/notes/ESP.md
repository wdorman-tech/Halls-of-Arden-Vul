## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 5ft radius per MU level, maximum 90ft
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to overhear the surface thoughts of any thinking creature in the area of effect.

## Details

The caster does not need to be able to see the creature whose thoughts they are reading; the spell can be used to listen beyond a closed door.

The spell cannot penetrate more than two feet of stone or more than two inches of any metal, and it is foiled by even a thin sheet of lead.
