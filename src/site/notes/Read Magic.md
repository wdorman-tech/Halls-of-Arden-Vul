## Notes

[[Read Magic\|Read Magic]] is both a 1st-level [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] spell AND a foundational ability — a magic-user must cast Read Magic on any unfamiliar spell book or scroll before attempting to copy spells from it. It is one of the four starting spells every magic-user must know (chosen as one of the two "chosen" first-level spells if not rolled randomly). In this campaign Read Magic is granted free as a houserule (see [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]).

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Spell Level:** 1st-level arcane spell
- **Mandatory:** Every magic-user must know Read Magic from character creation
- **Use:** Required to read and copy spells from another spellcaster's spell book or arcane scroll
- **Phantasmal Notation:** A magic-user can decipher [[Phantasmal Notation\|Phantasmal Notation]] (illusionist spells) using Read Magic. (Illusionists do not need Read Magic to read their own notation.)
- **Houserule:** In this campaign, [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] characters automatically know Read Magic in addition to two random and two chosen first-level spells.
