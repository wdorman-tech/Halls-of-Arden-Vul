## Notes

Because you are Lawful, you have a strong tendency to obey and support authority — as long as that authority is not Evil or Chaotic in nature. Because you are Good, you have a strong tendency to help those in need and protect the weak.

Lawful Good is the alignment of crusaders for justice, knightly orders, and devoted clerics of benevolent gods of order.

## Key Facts

- **Axis:** Lawful on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Good on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** Lawful Good tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
- **Classes typically associated:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]] (default; restriction lifted in this campaign)
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Ten\|The Ten]]):** [[Ardenia Tessaeron\|Ardenia Tessaeron]], [[Demma\|Demma]], [[Mitra\|Mitra]]
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]):** [[Bisor\|Bisor]]
- **Behavior:** obeys lawful authority that supports the cause of Good; protects the weak; keeps oaths
