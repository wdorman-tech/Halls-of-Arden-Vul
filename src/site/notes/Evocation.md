## Notes

**Evocation** spells call on raw elemental forces, and usually deal destruction. They are the bread-and-butter offensive school of arcane casters.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Evocation
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Evocation
- **Examples:** [[Magic Missile\|Magic Missile]], [[Fireball\|Fireball]], [[Lightning Bolt\|Lightning Bolt]], [[Cone of Cold\|Cone of Cold]], [[Ice Storm\|Ice Storm]], [[Wall Of Fire\|Wall of Fire]], [[Wall of Ice\|Wall of Ice]], [[Flame Strike\|Flame Strike]], [[Meteor Swarm\|Meteor Swarm]], [[Telekinesis\|Telekinesis]]
