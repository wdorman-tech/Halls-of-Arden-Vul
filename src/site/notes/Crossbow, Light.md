## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d4+1 vs S/M, 1d4+1 vs L
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 60ft
- **Rate of Fire:** 1 per round
- **Weight:** 4 lb
- **Cost:** 12 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A lighter crossbow that can be cocked by hand or with a simple lever — fires once per round. Usable from horseback.

Strength bonus does not apply to damage. Fires light [[Bolt, Light Crossbow\|crossbow bolts]].
