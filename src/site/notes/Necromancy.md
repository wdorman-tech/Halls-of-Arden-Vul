## Notes

**Necromancy** spells directly manipulate the forces of life and death — healing wounds, raising the dead, draining life energy, and causing fear of death. In OSRIC the school covers both the helpful (cure spells) and the sinister (animate dead, finger of death) sides of life-and-death magic.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Necromancy
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Necromancy
- **Examples:** [[Cure Light Wounds\|Cure Light Wounds]], [[Cure Serious Wounds\|Cure Serious Wounds]], [[Cure Critical Wounds\|Cure Critical Wounds]], [[Heal\|Heal]], [[Raise Dead\|Raise Dead]], [[Resurrection\|Resurrection]], [[Animate Dead\|Animate Dead]], [[Finger Of Death\|Finger of Death]], [[Death Spell\|Death Spell]], [[Speak With Dead\|Speak with Dead]], [[Feign Death\|Feign Death]], [[Magic Jar\|Magic Jar]]
