## Notes

Tychias is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the chaotic neutral goddess of luck, fate, and wealth — better known to most adventurers as the **Lady of Thieves**. Her cult quietly thrives in every port, every gambling house, every back alley. Her symbol is a crimson coin; her weapons are the dagger and the staff.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]]
- **Alignment:** [[Chaotic Neutral\|Chaotic Neutral]]
- **Gender:** F
- **Areas of Influence:** Luck, fate, wealth, "the Lady of Thieves"
- **Symbol(s):** Crimson coin
- **Weapon(s):** Dagger, staff
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Chaotic Neutral\|Chaotic Neutral]]; favored by many [[Thief\|Thieves]]
