## Stats

- **Value:** standard unit of currency
- **Weight:** 0.1 lb (10 coins = 1 lb)
- **Encumbrance Slots:** negligible per coin

## Exchange

- 1 gp = 100 [[Copper Piece\|Copper Piece]]s
- 1 gp = 10 [[Silver Piece\|Silver Piece]]s
- 1 gp = 2 [[Electrum Piece\|Electrum Piece]]s
- 5 gp = 1 [[Platinum Piece\|Platinum Piece]]
- [[Experience Points\|Experience Points]] are typically awarded at 1 XP per gp of treasure recovered.

## Notes

The basic unit of OSRIC currency. The gold piece is also conceptually treated as a unit of weight by some publishers (though OSRIC standardises 10 coins = 1 lb).

OSRIC prices far exceed real medi'val world prices because gold is far more plentiful in fantasy. This allows GMs to drop huge piles of gold and gemstones in the dungeon without breaking the economy.
