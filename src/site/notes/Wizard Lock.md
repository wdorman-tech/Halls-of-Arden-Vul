## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** A door or portal
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell holds a door or similar aperture closed, as if it were securely locked. The size limit on a portal to be locked is 30 square ft per magic-user level.

## Details

The portal must be something that normally opens and closes, such as a door, gate, valve, or portcullis, and it must be closed for the spell to function.

A wizard locked portal can be battered apart, but will not open normally.

The spell may be negated by means of a [[Knock\|Knock]] or [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]] spell.
