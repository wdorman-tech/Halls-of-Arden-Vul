## Notes

Historical rumours are bits of information about **the history of the dungeon** — old factions, ancient builders, the empire's prior rulers, the meaning of inscriptions, the deities once worshipped here. They might or might not be accurate, but each one has at least some truth in it.

## Sources

- **Starting characters get 1d2** at character creation (see [[Modified Character Creation\|Modified Character Creation]]).
- **Backup / post-start characters get none** at creation.
- **Carousing roll of 9** earns 1 (see [[Carousing\|Carousing]] effects table).
- The GM may award them in play.

## Why this matters

Historical rumours are the campaign's **lore-leak system** — the way long-buried truths trickle back to the players. They reward exploration, conversation, library time, and (via carousing) socialising.
