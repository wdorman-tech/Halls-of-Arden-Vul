## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Cone 5ft long per level, 1ft radius at base per level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (Half damage)

## Notes

A cone of cold is a blast of magical cold originating from the caster's outstretched hand. The cone inflicts 1d4+1 hit points per level of the caster.

## Details

All unprotected items (not held or worn by someone who makes a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]) in the area of effect may be destroyed if they fail item saving throws against Cold (Magical).
