## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Abjuration\|Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Up to 1 turn
- **Area of Effect:** From 1 to 10 creatures, objects, or places
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

When this spell is cast, you obscure the alignments of a certain number of creatures, objects, or places (see below), causing [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]], [[Detect Good\|Detect Good]], and [[Know Alignment\|Know Alignment]] to perceive the creatures as neutral.

## Details

This spell also affects special abilities. Thus, if a monster has the ability to know alignments, it would be fooled by this spell even though it does not have to cast a know alignment spell. A [[Paladin\|paladin's]] detect evil ability is also fooled by the spell.

The duration depends on how many creatures, objects, or places you decide to affect, according to this table:

| Targets | Duration |
|---------|----------|
| 1 | 1 turn |
| 2 | 5 rounds |
| 3 | 3 rounds |
| 4 or 5 | 2 rounds |
| 6 to 10 | 1 round |

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Know Alignment\|Know Alignment]].
