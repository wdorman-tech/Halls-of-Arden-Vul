## Notes

[[Disguise\|Disguise]] is an [[Assassin\|Assassin]] class ability that lets the character alter their apparent appearance, including height, weight, ancestry, and gender. Onlookers have a small percentile chance to see through the disguise — modified up or down by the GM based on circumstances.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]]
- **Effects:** Can change apparent height, weight, ancestry, and gender
- **Base Detection Chance:** 4% per observer
- **Ancestry Modifier:** +2% if disguise changes ancestry
- **Repeated Observation:** If the same observer sees the disguise repeatedly, they may check once per day
- **GM Discretion:** Chance is adjusted up or down based on circumstances. Examples from the rulebook:
    - Distant guard glimpsing in rain at night: ~1%
    - Spouse of the person you're impersonating walking into the room: ~50%
    - Three goblin guards looking at a "goblin" disguise (ancestry change): 6% each (4% base + 2% ancestry)
