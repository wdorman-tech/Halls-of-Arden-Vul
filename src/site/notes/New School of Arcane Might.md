## Notes

The New School of Arcane Might is primarily generalists or necromancers, most following a strict might-makes-right philosophy. It is **disliked by the public** — the kind of collegium whose graduates other mages refuse to introduce to their families. Its connections to necromancy and to the proposition that strength justifies its own use put it at moral odds with the public.

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Generalist or necromancy
- **Reputation:** Disliked by the public
- **Philosophy:** Might makes right
- **Membership profile:** Strivers willing to wield power without much restraint
