## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 13+)
- **Damage:** 1d6+1 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 7
- **Space Required:** 4ft
- **Length:** 2.5ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt
- **Weight:** 10 lb
- **Cost:** 8 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A heavy two-handed mace, wieldable one-handed by characters of STR 13+. Ideal for armoured opponents thanks to the blunt damage type.

Permitted for [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (blunt). Often the [[Cleric\|Cleric]]'s favoured weapon.
