## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** 10ft per illusionist level
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** 20ft x 20ft
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

A certain number of creatures within the spell's area of effect are frozen in place, magically convinced that they cannot move.

## Details

The caster can affect creatures with a total number of hit dice equal to twice their caster level.

Each creature is entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against the spell. The caster can end the paralysis at any time; otherwise a [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]] (or [[Dispel Illusion\|Dispel Illusion]]) spell is the only way to remove the paralysis.
