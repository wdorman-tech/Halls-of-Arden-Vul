## Notes

Law and Chaos are one of the two cosmic axes of [[Alignment\|Alignment]]. They represent opposed metaphysical forces shaping the multiverse.

**Law** seeks ever-increasing perfection. The goal requires efficiency, complexity, and specialisation — individuals are cogs in a machine. The right leaders must lead and the right people must do the right things; rules guide leaders and followers. Rules should not be broken, but a different rule may apply better in a given situation, so it is important to know which rule fits.

**Chaos** is opposed to Law and is focused on simplicity and individualism. Nothing happens without individual inspiration, and every situation is unique. Rules only restrict; orders given personally by a trusted leader are far better than abstract rules. If you cannot hear the king give the order, he should not be your king.

**Neutrality** on this axis is pragmatism: sometimes laws help, sometimes they hinder. Neutral characters do not consider themselves bound by the cosmic forces of either pole.

## Key Facts

- **Lawful:** obeys/supports authority, values rules and oaths
- **Chaotic:** opposes authority, values individualism and personal loyalty
- **Neutral:** pragmatic; rules are tools, not moral imperatives
- **Oath behavior:** Lawful keep oaths because oaths matter; Chaotic keep them as personal honour; Neutrals keep them only to maintain credibility
