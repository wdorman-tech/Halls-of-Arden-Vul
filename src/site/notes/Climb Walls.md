## Notes

[[Climb Walls\|Climb Walls]] is one of the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s core skills (also held by the [[Assassin\|Assassin]] at the same percentages). The thief can scale sheer walls and surfaces, even cling to ceilings. The check is only for challenging climbs — trees, ropes, and ladders need no roll.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] using same table)
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll; succeed if rolled equal to or under the listed percentage
- **When to Roll:** Only for challenging climbs (sheer walls, ceilings) — NOT for trees, ropes, ladders
- **Mid-Climb Check:** Roll again at the midpoint of the climb; failure = fall
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
