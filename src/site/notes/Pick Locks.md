## Notes

[[Pick Locks\|Pick Locks]] is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to open locks without the key. The thief must hold a set of [[Thieves' Tools\|Thieves' Tools]] in one hand and have the other hand free.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] same table; also a [[Monk\|Monk]] thief skill)
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll; takes 1 combat round
- **Equipment Required:** [[Thieves' Tools\|Thieves' Tools]] in one hand, other hand free
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
