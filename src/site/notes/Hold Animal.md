## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 80ft
- **Duration:** 2 rounds per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 4 animals
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (see below)

## Notes

This spell paralyses up to 4 animals.

## Details

If the animal fails its [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]], it is paralysed (see [[Held\|Held]]) for the duration of the spell.

You decide how many animals you will target with the spell: if there is only one target, the saving throw is made at -4; if two, each makes the saving throw at -2; if three, the saving throw is at -1; and if you seek to hold four animals, each makes its saving throw with no penalty.

Creatures with magical abilities are not affected by this spell, which affects only ordinary creatures and giant versions of them. Humans, demi-humans, and humanoids are not considered animals.

Creatures with intelligence of 6+ are not affected by the spell.

**Note:** "Animal" is a term that is defined differently in different places in the original rules. We have used [[Invisibility To Animals\|Invisibility To Animals]] as the model here, rather than [[Animal Friendship\|Animal Friendship]].
