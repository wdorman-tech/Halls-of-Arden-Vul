## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

You issue a one-word command to the targeted creature, and they might be forced to obey for one round. The command must be very clear (unambiguous), so only a few commands are normally used.

## Details

The creature must be able to hear you, and doesn't have to obey unless the command is in a language it understands. The target creature doesn't have to obey unless the command is a verb.

This spell is considered in the nature of a [[Charm\|Charm]] and of mental domination, so many creatures such as [[Golems\|Golems]], [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], and [[Undead\|Undead]] are immune.

The target creature may roll a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs spells and other magical effects only if it meets one or more of the following criteria. Otherwise, the spell takes automatic effect:

- If it is a character, its Intelligence score is 13 or greater
- If it is a monster, its Intelligence stat is High or greater
- If it is level 6 or more
- If it has 6 or more hit dice

The target applies its Wisdom save modifier, if it has one. If it succeeds at the saving throw, it doesn't have to obey.

Some typical commands include: Approach, Die (catatonic for 1 round), Dive, Flee, Grovel (prone for 1 round), Halt. You can give other commands as well — the GM decides how the target reacts.
