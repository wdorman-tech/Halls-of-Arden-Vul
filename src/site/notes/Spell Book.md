## Notes

A [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]] is the indispensable tool of a [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] or [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]. It contains the formulas and words for each spell the caster knows. A spell can only be memorised after it has been copied into the spell book and successfully understood. Without their spell book a caster has only their currently-memorised spells available. Magic-users record their spells in arcane notation; illusionists use [[Phantasmal Notation\|Phantasmal Notation]].

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] (and [[Ranger\|Ranger]] from level 9, see [[Ranger Spellcasting\|Ranger Spellcasting]])
- **Required For Memorisation:** A spell must be copied into the spell book before it can be memorised
- **Understanding vs Copying:** A spell may be copied into the book without being understood — but cannot be memorised or cast unless understood (roll d100 vs INT, see [[Magic-User Spellcasting\|Magic-User Spellcasting]] / [[Illusionist Spellcasting\|Illusionist Spellcasting]])

### Starting Contents
- **[[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]:** 4 first-level spells from the 30 first-level arcane list — 2 random, 2 chosen. [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]] must be one. (Houserule: Read Magic is free; see [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]].)
- **[[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]:** 3 first-level spells from the 12 first-level phantasmal list — 2 random (1d12 each, re-roll doubles), 1 chosen.

### Adding Spells
- **On Level-Up:** Choose 1 spell of any spell level you can cast — added to your spell book free, automatically understood
- **From Scrolls/Other Books:** Roll d100 vs INT to understand each spell. If understood, copy: 100 gp/level for ink, 1 hour/level. Copying erases the source. When the last spell is copied from a scroll/book, that source crumbles to dust.
- **Read Magic Required:** Must cast [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]] on any unfamiliar spell book or scroll before attempting to copy spells from it

### Limits
- Maximum understood spells per spell level depends on INT (see Magic-User / Illusionist spellcasting tables)
- May "drop" an understood spell to attempt a new one (gambling — dropped spell is gone unless re-rolled successfully)

### Special: Ranger
- A [[Ranger\|Ranger]] receives a spell book from their mentor at level 9 containing first-level magic-user spells. Acquisition rules apply as for magic-users.
