## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Hurled)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d3 vs S/M, 1d2 vs L
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 15ft
- **Rate of Fire:** 3 per round
- **Weight:** 0.5 lb
- **Cost:** 2 sp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 per handful

## Notes

A small thrown spike — easy to carry in numbers. The rate of fire (**3 per round**) makes the dart one of the highest-rate-of-fire weapons in OSRIC. Strength bonus applies to thrown damage.

[[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s and [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]s are permitted to use darts.
