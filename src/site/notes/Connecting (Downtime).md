## Notes

Connecting is one of four standard downtime activities (see [[Downtime\|Downtime]]).

## The Rule

- **Cost:** Up to **1,000 GP per week**
- **Benefit:** **0.5 XP per GP** spent. **Reputation** in town improves the more time you spend Connecting.
- **Flavor:** Good characters often do charity work; neutral characters ingratiate themselves politically; evil and chaotic characters might lean into something else. Your character can break the mold — this is a sandbox.

## Why this matters

This is the **reputation-building** downtime. The XP rate is half that of Training, but you accrue social capital — useful when you need a faction to back you, a noble to vouch for you, or the city watch to look the other way.
