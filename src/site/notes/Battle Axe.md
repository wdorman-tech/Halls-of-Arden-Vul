## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 15+)
- **Damage:** 1d8 vs S/M, 1d8 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 7
- **Space Required:** 4ft
- **Length:** 4ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Weight:** 7 lb
- **Cost:** 5 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 2 (large item per Player Doc)

## Notes

A two-handed war axe that can be wielded one-handed by characters of STR 15 or higher. Without sufficient strength, one-handed use suffers the [[Weapon Proficiency\|non-proficiency penalty]] for the wielder's class.

The [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
