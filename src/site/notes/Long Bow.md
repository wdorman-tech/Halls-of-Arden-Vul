## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L (varies by [[Arrow\|arrow]])
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 70ft
- **Maximum Range:** 350ft (5 increments)
- **Rate of Fire:** 2 per round
- **Weight:** 8 lb
- **Cost:** 60 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A long, two-handed pulled bow with excellent range. Wielder cannot use a [[Shield, Small\|shield]]. Strength bonus does **not** apply to damage by default; specially-made bows may allow it.

Fires [[Arrow\|Arrow]]s. Carry them in a [[Quiver\|Quiver]].
