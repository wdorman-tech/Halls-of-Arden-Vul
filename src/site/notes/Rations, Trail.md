## Stats

- **Cost:** 2 gp per day
- **Weight:** 1 lb per day
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 per several days

## Notes

A day's trail rations: hardtack, jerky, dried fruit, and similar long-keeping food. Lighter and longer-lasting than [[Rations, Standard\|standard rations]] but more expensive — the dungeoneer's choice.

Sometimes called "iron rations" in older texts.
