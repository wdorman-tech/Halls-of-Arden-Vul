## Notes

**Hit points** (hp) measure a creature's ability to withstand [[Damage\|Damage]]. When a creature is hit, the damage rolled is subtracted from its current hit points. When hit points reach 0, the character is unconscious and continues to lose 1 hp per round from blood loss until death at -10 hp.

Hit points represent both physical injury and luck/stamina/divine favour — they abstract a fighter's ability to roll with a blow as much as a wound to be patched up. New hp are gained by rolling [[Hit Die\|hit dice]] when [[Gaining Levels\|gaining a level]].

## Key Facts

- **Roll:** [[Hit Die\|Hit Die]] determined by [[Class\|Class]]
- **Modifiers:** [[Constitution\|Constitution]] hp adjustment per hit die (until high level)
- **0 hp:** unconscious, bleeding out at 1 hp/round
- **-10 hp:** dead
- **-6 hp or below:** likely permanent scars
- **Recovery:** see [[Natural Healing\|Natural Healing]] and [[Rest\|Rest]]
- **Minimum gain on level up:** always at least 1 hp (even with negative CON modifier)
