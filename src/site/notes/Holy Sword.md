## Notes

The [[Holy Sword\|Holy Sword]] is a paladin-specific magical weapon mentioned in OSRIC as appropriate for [[Paladin\|Paladins]]. The OSRIC class section establishes that the paladin may wield such a blade; full mechanics belong in the magic-item / weapon-treasure tables.

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]] (signature item)
- **Type:** Magical sword
- **Restrictions:** Paladin's vow restricts them to a maximum of 10 magical items total (only 1 magic armour, only 1 magic shield among them)
