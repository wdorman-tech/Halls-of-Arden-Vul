## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 5ft per MU level
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** Creatures in a 30ft x 30ft area
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell causes the affected targets to view the caster as a trusted friend, if they fail a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs. spells.

## Details

The spell can target a number of hit dice of creatures equal to twice the caster's magic-user level. The caster can select which creatures to target with the spell within the area of effect, provided their total hit dice do not exceed twice the caster's magic-user level.

If a targeted creature fails a saving throw (with a -2 penalty to the roll), it will view the caster as a trusted friend, interpreting every word in its most positive light. [[Charmed\|Charmed]] creatures will place themselves into moderate danger, including combat, on the caster's behalf, but will not undertake risks beyond those the creature might ordinarily take. The caster must be able to talk to the charmed creature if they want to make any sort of complex "request".

Charmed creatures are allowed periodic saving throws to break the charm, based on their hit dice:

| Hit Dice of Monsters | Chance/Week to Break Enchantment |
|---|---|
| <1 to 1+ | 5% |
| 2 to 2+ | 10% |
| 3 to 3+ | 15% |
| 4 to 4+ | 25% |
| 5 to 5+ | 35% |
| 6 to 6+ | 45% |
| 7 to 7+ | 60% |
| 8 to 8+ | 75% |
| 9+ | 90% |
