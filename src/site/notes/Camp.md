## Notes

The party may **camp** in the dungeon for a "night" to recover spells, or in the wilderness for the day. During camp, the party cannot move or perform strenuous actions (passive activities such as mapping are allowed).

## Key Facts

**In the dungeon:**
- A small, out-of-the-way room with a single spikable door is a good camp site
- An open corridor or near a stairway is dangerous
- Wandering monster checks continue
- Spell memorisation: 4 hours uninterrupted rest, then 15 minutes per spell level (see [[Spellcasting\|Spellcasting]])
- Watch rotations recommended

**Outdoors:**
- Step 6 of the [[Outdoor Adventure\|Outdoor Adventure]] sequence: GM presents campsite options, party camps
- Step 7: wandering monster check at camp
- Weather, terrain, and visibility affect available options
- Watches should be set; tired parties may suffer if disrupted
