## Notes

Languages in [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] determine who can speak with whom. Each character starts with several languages: their ancestry's language(s), their [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]] (the alignment tongue of their alignment), and (for humans) their culture's language plus one of choice. The total number of languages a character can ever learn is gated by their [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] score.

In the Halls of Arden Vul setting, **[[Common\|Common]] does not exist** — anyone who would learn it instead learns another language of their choice (Player Doc houserule).

## Key Facts

- **Languages governed by:** [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] (caps additional languages learnable)
- **Free at character creation:** ancestry language(s), alignment tongue, plus extras for humans (culture + choice)
- **No Common in this setting:** see [[Ancestry Houserules\|Ancestry Houserules]]
- **Categories:** ancestry languages, [[Alignment Language\|alignment tongues]], cultural languages, ancient/special tongues, secret tongues
