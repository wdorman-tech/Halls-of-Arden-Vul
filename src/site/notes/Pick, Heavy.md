## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 14+)
- **Damage:** 2d4+1 vs S/M, 1d12 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 7
- **Space Required:** 4ft
- **Length:** 4ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt or Piercing (wielder's choice)
- **Weight:** 6 lb
- **Cost:** 6 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A military pick effective at puncturing heavy armour. One-handed use requires STR 14+. The piercing damage option makes this off-limits to [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] for that mode of attack.
