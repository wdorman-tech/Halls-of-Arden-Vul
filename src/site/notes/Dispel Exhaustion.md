## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 3 turns per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 4 creatures
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates a powerful illusion of physical health. Recipients "gain" 50% of any [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] that have been lost, and function for all intents and purposes as if these hit points are real. All creatures under the influence of this spell can move (but not attack) at double the normal speed.

## Details

The illusory hit points are the first to be subtracted if the character sustains [[Damage\|Damage]]. At the end of the spell's duration, the remaining illusory hit points are lost.
