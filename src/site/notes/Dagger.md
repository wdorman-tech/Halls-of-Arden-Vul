## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee / Missile (Hurled)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d4 vs S/M, 1d3 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 2
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 1ft
- **Damage Type:** Piercing or Slashing (wielder's choice)
- **Range Increment:** 10ft (Hurled)
- **Rate of Fire:** 2 per round (when thrown)
- **Weight:** 1 lb
- **Cost:** 2 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 (often 0 for very small carry; usually 1 for tracking purposes)

## Notes

Fast, light, and usable as both a melee and thrown weapon. The [[Dagger\|Dagger]] is one of the few weapons usable by [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s and [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]s. Usable by all classes EXCEPT [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (edged).

A character can throw 2 daggers per round. Strength bonus applies to thrown damage.
