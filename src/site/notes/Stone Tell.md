## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Divination\|Divination]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 10 cubic feet
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 10 rounds
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell gives stone the ability to hear your questions and mentally reply. It gives brief, truthful answers to your questions, to the best of its knowledge.

## Details

Only you can hear the stone's answers. The stone only knows what has happened within twenty feet of it (which is why the area of effect is important).
