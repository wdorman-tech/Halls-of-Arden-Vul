## Notes

OSRIC (Old School Reference and Index Compilation) is the rules system used for the Halls of Arden Vul campaign. It is a retro-clone of AD&D 1st Edition, faithful to the original mechanics but rewritten under an open license. We are using OSRIC 3rd Edition, authored by Stuart Marshall and Matthew Finch and freely available from DriveThruRPG.

OSRIC is a tactical, lethal old-school system: characters are fragile at low levels, ability scores are rolled rather than chosen, and player skill (planning, exploration, negotiation) matters far more than character abilities at solving puzzles. It belongs squarely to the [[OSR\|OSR]] (Old-School Renaissance) tradition.
