## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** 60ft + 10ft per illusionist level
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell allows the illusionist to "cast" a quasi-real version of any one of the following arcane spells: [[Cloudkill\|Cloudkill]], [[Cone of Cold\|Cone of Cold]], [[Magic Missile\|Magic Missile]], [[Fireball\|Fireball]], [[Lightning Bolt\|Lightning Bolt]], [[Wall Of Fire\|Wall of Fire]], [[Wall of Ice\|Wall of Ice]].

## Details

Anyone who would be damaged by the spell is entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against the illusion, but does not receive a second saving throw against the illusionary spell effect.

- Anyone who succeeds at the saving throw against the illusion (vs. spells) takes 2 hit points of psychic damage per caster level from the offensive spells, the wall spells will inflict 1d4 hit points per caster level, and the cloudkill will only kill creatures of fewer than 2 hit dice (no saving throw).
- Anyone who fails the saving throw takes the full damage that the illusionary spell would inflict, but the damage is psychic damage. Thus, a creature who is immune to fire would still be damaged by a demi-shadow "wall of fire".
