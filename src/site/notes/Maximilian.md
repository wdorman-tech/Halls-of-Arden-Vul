## 📜 Background & Notes

> Rumours live with the player — see [[William\|William]].

> [!note] Background — _The Imperial Academy_
> Maximilian is a graduate of [[The Imperial Academy\|The Imperial Academy]], the small but politically influential collegium drawn from the upper echelons of imperial nobility. The Academy emphasises a versatile, courtly approach to magic — many graduates serve senators, magistrates, and patrician houses. Whatever has drawn Maximilian into the depths of Arden Vul, he carries with him an Academy ring, decent connections in the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]]'s capitals, and a reputation he will either burnish or squander down here in the dark.
