## Notes

The character cannot hear. This doesn't affect most actions in terms of the rules, but the GM will need to handle the fact that the character can't hear what other characters are saying.

The slight effect of deafness is **not large enough to affect surprise, initiative, fighting, or spell casting**.

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** cannot hear; in-fiction communication is blocked
- **Mechanically:** no penalties to surprise, initiative, combat, or spellcasting
- **Duration:** depends on source ([[Power Word, Stun\|Power Word, Stun]], magic, loud noise, deafening blow)
- **Removal:** [[Cure Disease\|Cure Disease]] for some sources, [[Heal\|Heal]], [[Restoration\|Restoration]], [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]] for magical deafness
- **Stacks with:** any other condition
