## Notes

The Collegium of Macrina is a large college of researchers and theorists. Its mages create new spells frequently — a Macrina graduate will have a notebook full of half-finished arcane theorems and a strong opinion about somebody else's research. They are the closest thing the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]] has to an academic university press.

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Spell research, theoretical magic, new spell creation
- **Reputation:** Academic; the place to go if you want to invent rather than apply
- **Membership profile:** Researchers, theorists, scholars
- **Schools of magic favored:** Whatever the current theoretical fashion demands — broad
