## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th (Cleric) / 8th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Conjuration\|Divine Conjuration]] / [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level (Cleric) / Varies (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** Each creature within 100ft that can see the symbol (Cleric) / Varies (Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect for Cleric); None unless noted (Magic-User)

## Notes

The symbol spell is used to enchant magical runes (or, for Cleric, a glowing symbol shaped in the air). Any creature that passes by, over, or under the rune; touches it; or reads it will be affected by its magic. Cleric symbols affect anyone within 100ft who sees them.

## Details

### Cleric Symbols

Clerics can use three symbols: Hopelessness, Pain, and Persuasion. The caster can choose which symbol to use when casting the spell.

- **Hopelessness:** For the next 3d4 turns, the creature suffers a -100 penalty on [[Morale\|Morale]] checks. If attacked in melee, it must either flee or surrender.
- **Pain:** For the next 2d10 turns, the creature experiences horrible pain. Its dexterity score and armour class get 2 points worse, and it takes a penalty of -4 on attack rolls.
- **Persuasion:** For the next 1d20 turns, the creature's [[Alignment\|alignment]] changes to the caster's alignment, and it views the caster as a trusted friend.

### Magic-User Symbols

There are numerous symbols that can be created with this spell. Those best known are as follows:

- **Symbol of Death:** Creatures with hit points totalling not more than 80 are slain. Duration is instantaneous.
- **Symbol of Discord:** All creatures in the area begin arguing with one another. Those that do not share the same alignment have a 50% chance of actually fighting one another. The duration is 5d4 rounds, reduced to 2d4 rounds if a fight breaks out.
- **Symbol of Fear:** As per the level 3 illusionist spell [[Fear\|Fear]], but the [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] is made at -4. Duration is 1 round per caster level.
- **Symbol of Hopelessness:** As Cleric version above.
- **Symbol of Insanity:** Creatures with total hit points of not more than 120 are affected by lunacy, acting per the random actions described in the level 4 magic-user spell [[Confusion\|Confusion]]. The effect is permanent (or until removed by magical means).
- **Symbol of Pain:** As Cleric version above.
- **Symbol of Sleep:** This symbol causes any creature of 8+1 HD or less to fall into an enchanted slumber; it is impossible to awaken the victims for 4d4+1 turns.
- **Symbol of Stunning:** Creatures with a total of 160 or fewer hit points are [[Stunned\|Stunned]] for 3d4 rounds, dropping whatever they are holding.

Material components for the magic-user version cost at least 10,000 gp.
