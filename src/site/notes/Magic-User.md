## Notes

The [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] practices arcane magic — the most complex and dangerous spellcraft in OSRIC, ranging from prestidigitation up to ninth-level world-shaking effects. Driven by [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]], a magic-user must memorise spells from their [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]] before casting; they begin frail and weapon-poor but become the most powerful class in the game by name level. The wizard's tower at level 11 is a campaign milestone.

## Houserule

In this campaign, [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] characters automatically know [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]] in addition to two random and two chosen first-level spells (so the random/chosen four spells are a separate matter from Read Magic, which is granted free). Each magic-user must also choose one of the [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]] as their college of study.

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** DEX 6, CON 6, INT 9, WIS 6, CHA 6
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] (10% XP bonus if INT 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d4 (max 10)
- **Alignment:** Any
- **Armour:** None
- **Weapons:** Dagger, dart, oil, staff
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 1 (additional at level 6, 11, and 16)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -5 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 2d4 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]], [[Elf\|Elf]], [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]] (per ancestry rules)
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Class Abilities:** [[Magic-User Spellcasting\|Magic-User Spellcasting]], [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]], [[Spell Research\|Spell Research]]
- **Spell Acquisition:** Roll d100 vs INT for chance to understand each new arcane spell (Table 1.3.6.2A)
- **Starting Spells:** 4 first-level spells (2 random, 2 chosen). Plus, by campaign houserule, [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]] is automatically known.
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 7 — Eldritch Craft (potions, scrolls, recharging aimed magic items); Lvl 11 — Wizard's Tower; Lvl 12 — Eldritch Power (create magic items if you know [[Enchant an Item\|Enchant an Item]])
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,400 XP; Level 7 at 60,000 XP (Eldritch Craft); Level 11 at 375,000 XP (stronghold); Level 18 at 3,000,000 XP (9th level spells)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Wizard's Tower (or other structure) at level 11; no automatic followers
