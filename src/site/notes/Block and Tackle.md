- **Cost:** 1 gp
- **Weight:** 5 lb

## Notes

A set of pulleys with rope, allowing one person to lift up to four times their normal load. Useful for hauling heavy treasure chests, raising portcullises, or lowering [[Rope, Hemp\|rope]] climbers down a shaft.
