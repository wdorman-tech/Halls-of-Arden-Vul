## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 4 [Ascending: 16]
- **Movement Cap:** 60ft
- **Weight:** 40 lb
- **Cost:** 80 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 9 in 3x3

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot wear metal armour
- [[Thief\|Thief]] cannot wear
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear

## Notes

Vertical metal splints strapped over a backing. Same AC as [[Banded Mail\|Banded Mail]] but heavier and slower (60ft cap). All purchased armour comes with a [[Helmet\|Helmet]] included.
