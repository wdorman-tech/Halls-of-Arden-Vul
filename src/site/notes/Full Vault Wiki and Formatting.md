## Thesis

This vault is a player-side reference for the **Halls of Arden Vul** campaign run on the [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] 3.0 ruleset, with houserules and setting details from [[The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc\|The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc]]. Every game-mechanical concept (spell, class skill, ancestral trait, weapon, alignment, deity, etc.) gets its own atomic note so that backlinks light up wherever a rule is cross-referenced. The goal is fast lookup at the table — no scrolling a 260-page PDF mid-combat.

## Categories

Categories are the primary way of organization. Every category is singular and lowercase; backlinks flow into category bases that auto-aggregate every note tagged with that category. Proper nouns are the only exception (e.g. [[Archontean\|Archontean]], [[Ardenia Tessaeron\|Ardenia Tessaeron]]).

## Note types

- **Concept note** — single mechanic, ability, or term. Always has frontmatter with `aliases`, `categories`, `created`. Body has `## Notes`, then `## Key Facts` (bullet stat block), then `## Related` (cross-links).
- **Spell note** — same template plus a stat block at the top: Range, Duration, Area of Effect, Components, Casting Time, Saving Throw, School+Level. School-of-magic and class are categories on the spell.
- **Deity note** — alignment, areas of influence, symbol(s), weapon(s) listed under Key Facts.
- **Class / Ancestry note** — overview, class abilities by level, level limits, multi-class options listed under Key Facts.
- **Reference note** — used for the source PDF and Player Doc themselves.

## Houserule layer

[[The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc\|The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc]] modifies the base [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] rules in several places. Where a houserule supersedes a base rule, both notes link to each other and the houserule note carries the [[houserule\|houserule]] category. Major houserules are listed in [[Houserules Index\|Houserules Index]].

## Linking conventions

- Use full Wikilink form `[[Note Name]]`, not aliases, for the canonical link in `## Related` sections.
- Use aliased form `[[Note Name|alias]]` only inside prose paragraphs where the natural reading flows differently.
- Every spell links to its school of magic and its class spell list note (e.g. [[Cleric Spells - 1st Level\|Cleric Spells - 1st Level]]).
- Every weapon links to weapon-class concept notes (one-handed, two-handed, reach, etc.).
- Every class ability links back to its parent [[Class\|class]] and any [[trait\|trait]] or [[skill\|skill]] it depends on.
