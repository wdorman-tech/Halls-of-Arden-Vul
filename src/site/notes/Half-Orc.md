## Notes

Half-orcs blend [[Orc\|Orc]] and [[Human\|Human]] heritage. Like half-elves, they lack their own culture and adopt the one in which they were raised. Half-orcs reach adulthood around 15 and live only 60-80 years; like elves they have no soul in the human sense, so they cannot be raised from the dead though they may be reincarnated.

## Key Facts

- **Size:** Medium
- **Base Movement Rate:** 120ft (see [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]])
- **Ability Score Adjustments:** +1 [[Strength\|Strength]], +1 [[Constitution\|Constitution]], -2 [[Charisma\|Charisma]]
- **Required Scores (after adjustments):** STR 6-18, DEX 3-17, CON 13-19, INT 3-17, WIS 3-14, CHA 3-12
- **Languages:** [[Common\|Common]], [[Orcish\|Orcish]], plus [[Alignment Tongue\|Alignment Tongue]]; max two further languages regardless of [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]]
- **Available classes:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Multi-class options:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]/[[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]]/[[Thief\|Thief]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]]/[[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Thief\|Thief]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Assassin\|Assassin]]
- **Level limits:** Assassin 15, Cleric 4, Fighter 10, Thief 7 (DEX 17+) / 6 (DEX 16-)
- **Ancestral traits:** [[Infravision\|Infravision]] (60ft), [[Half-Orc Resilience\|Half-Orc Resilience]] (the Half-Orc version of [[Stalwart\|Stalwart]])
- **Lifespan:** 60-80 years; adulthood at ~15
- **Starting Age:** Cleric 20+1d4; Fighter 13+1d4; Thief or Assassin 20+2d4
- **Random Height/Weight:** 5ft 6in + 3d4 inches; 150 + 5d10 lbs
- **Cannot be [[Raise Dead\|raised from the dead]]** (but [[Reincarnation\|Reincarnation]] works)
