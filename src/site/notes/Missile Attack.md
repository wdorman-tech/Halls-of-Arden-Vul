## Notes

A **missile attack** is an attack with a ranged weapon such as a crossbow, sling, bow, or hurled axe. The attack is made the same way as a [[Melee Attack\|Melee Attack]]: roll d20, add modifiers, compare to the target's [[Armour Class\|Armour Class]].

A character's [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] missile bonus is added to the to-hit roll. If a missile weapon is in hand and the character has declared a missile attack, the dexterity missile bonus is **also subtracted from the [[Initiative\|Initiative]] roll** — meaning skilled archers can attack earlier in the round even if their side lost the initiative.

## Key Facts

- **Roll:** d20 vs. target's AC
- **DEX missile bonus:** to-hit AND initiative segment (lower segment = earlier action)
- **No movement:** cannot move in a round when making a missile attack — *exception:* hurled weapons
- **Firing into melee:** cannot choose target; randomly determined among melee participants — including allies
- **Over-the-head firing:** allowed only if the ally is at least one size smaller than you
- **Range bands:** weapons typically have short / medium / long range — modifiers from the weapon table
- **Cover/concealment:** apply [[Cover\|Cover]] and [[Concealment\|Concealment]] penalties
