## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell causes the target to become [[Blinded\|Blinded]], but causes no damage to the eyes themselves.

## Details

The effect cannot be removed by any spell that restores physical health (such as a [[Healing\|Healing]] spell, [[Cure Disease\|Cure Disease]], etc.), but may be dispelled normally. This particular illusion can be removed with [[Cure Blindness\|Cure Blindness]] because it directly causes blindness.

The caster can end the condition at will.
