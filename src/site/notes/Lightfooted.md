## Notes

Lightfooted gives [[Elf\|elves]] and [[Halfling\|halflings]] a chance to gain a [[Surprise\|Surprise]] attack on enemies, provided they are lightly armoured and far enough from any noisy companions. The base chance is 1-4 on 1d6, dropping to 2-in-6 if the character has to open a door (or do a similarly noisy task) to make the attack.

## Key Facts

- **Base chance:** 1-4 on 1d6 of [[Surprise\|surprise attack]]
- **Reduced chance:** 2-in-6 if you must open a door or similar
- **Armour requirement:** unarmoured, [[Leather Armour\|Leather Armour]], [[Studded Leather\|Studded Leather]], or [[Padded Armour\|Padded Armour]] only
- **Companion requirement:** all non-Lightfooted characters must be at least 90ft away (or they alert enemies)
- **GM may adjust** the 4-in-6 chance based on conditions
- **Who has it:** [[Elf\|Elf]], [[Halfling\|Halfling]]
