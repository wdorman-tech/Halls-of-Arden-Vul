## Notes

A character who **parries** cannot attack in the same round, but may **subtract any [[Strength\|Strength]]-based and all specialisation-based "to hit" bonuses from the melee attack rolls of opponents**.

Parrying may be used while [[Fleeing from Combat\|fleeing from combat]]. Parrying only has value to a character with a strength or specialisation-related bonus to hit. **You must have a weapon in hand to parry.**

## Key Facts

- **Cannot attack** in the same round
- **Effect:** opponent's melee attacks lose any STR-based and specialisation to-hit bonuses
- **Requires weapon:** must have a weapon in hand
- **Useful when fleeing:** allows a parry vs. the +4 fleeing attack
- **Of value vs:** strong opponents and specialised fighters; useless against weak/unmodified attackers
