## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 6 turns + 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Passage 5ft wide, 10ft high, and 10ft long
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By means of a passwall spell, the caster creates an inter-dimensional opening through any non-metallic material, allowing anyone to simply walk directly into the hillside or through a wall.

## Details

More than one spell may be used in succession to create longer passages.

The inside of the passageway is still a part of the material plane; the inter-dimensional warping of space simply acts to "remove" the material through which the passage is created.
