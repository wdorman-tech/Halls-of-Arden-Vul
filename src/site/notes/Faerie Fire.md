## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 80ft
- **Duration:** 4 rounds per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** 1 size M or 2 size S creatures per caster level
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell ignites a fiery-looking aura of bright light around the enemies you designate, making them easier to hit with attacks.

## Details

The aura is visible at a range of 80ft if the observer stands in darkness, and at 40ft if he or she stands near another light source.

The light itself is harmless, but a creature lit by faerie fire is easier to hit; any attacks against it are made with a bonus of +2.
