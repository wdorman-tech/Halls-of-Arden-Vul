## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 5ft per level
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell disintegrates matter of any kind: animal, vegetable, or mineral. The spell cannot be used to destroy both living and non-living matter; if it is targeted at a creature, it does not disintegrate non-living matter, and if targeted at non-living matter (a wall or equipment) it does not disintegrate living matter.

## Details

Matter of a magical nature will also be destroyed, although evocations of magic force (such as a [[Globe of Invulnerability\|Globe of Invulnerability]], or [[Shield\|Shield]]) are not affected since they are not physical substances.

If the spell is used to target a creature, it destroys the entire creature.

If the spell is used to target non-living matter, it obliterates an area up to 10ft x 10ft per level.
