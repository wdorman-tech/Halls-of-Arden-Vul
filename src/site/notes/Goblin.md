## Notes

Goblin is the chattering, pidgin-style language of [[Goblin\|Goblin]] tribes and their close cousins. It is widely understood among various humanoid raider populations and is often the most useful language for a dungeoneer to know.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Goblin\|Goblin]], [[Hobgoblin\|Hobgoblin]] (related), often understood by [[Bugbear\|Bugbear]], [[Orc\|Orc]]
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Negotiation with raiders, eavesdropping in dungeons, deciphering crude graffiti
