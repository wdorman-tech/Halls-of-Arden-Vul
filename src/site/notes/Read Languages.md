## Notes

[[Read Languages\|Read Languages]] is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to decipher non-magical writing they don't otherwise know. Thieves see many purloined documents — title deeds, letters of credit, missives — and over time pick up scraps of many tongues.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] using the same table)
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll
- **Scope:** Any non-magical language originating on the Prime Material Plane
- **Restriction:** Does NOT decipher magical writing — that's the role of [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]]
- **Note:** Read Languages is the one thief skill that may be used while wearing armour heavier than thieves can wear (e.g. by a multi-classed character in heavy armour)
