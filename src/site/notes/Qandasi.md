## Notes

Qandasi is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic evil **Sloe-Eyed Lady of Lust** and **Dancing Maiden of Chaos** — goddess of lust, control, and sex. She is the dark mirror to [[Thelis\|Thelis]]. Her symbol is a human image bearing both breasts and phallus; her weapon, the whip.
