## Concept

Pulled bows are launched [[Missile Weapon\|Missile Weapon]]s — projectiles propelled by drawing back a bowstring. Bows require **two hands** to use (no [[Shield, Small\|shield]] possible).

By default, **Strength bonus does NOT apply to damage** with a pulled bow. In some campaigns, specially-made bows might allow Strength bonus to arrow damage; these are sold at special cost or only as treasure.

- Long bows can't reach beyond 350ft (5 increments).
- Short bows can't reach beyond 250ft (5 increments).

## Ammunition

Bows fire [[Arrow\|Arrow]]s.
