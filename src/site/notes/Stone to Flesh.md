## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One creature or 9 cubic ft of stone per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell transforms stone into flesh. A creature that has been turned to stone will be returned to its normal state (provided that a system shock roll is successful).

## Details

If the spell is used upon a normal stone statue, the statue is not brought to life, it just becomes a chunk of meat shaped like the statue.

## Reversed Form

Reversible as [[Flesh to Stone\|Flesh to Stone]].
