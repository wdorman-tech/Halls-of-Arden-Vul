## Notes

The [[Monk\|Monk]] is a member of an ascetic religious order pursuing mental, spiritual, and physical perfection. Driven by [[Strength\|Strength]], [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]], and [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]], they fight unarmoured (their armour class improving with level) and master a wide variety of mystical abilities. Monks are weak at low levels but approach magic-user power at high level — and uniquely, several of their class abilities depend on minimum [[Ability Scores\|Ability Scores]] (STR 15+, WIS 15+, CON 11+).

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 10, WIS 10, DEX 15. Characters lacking STR 15+, WIS 15+, or CON 11+ may still play Monks but will have weaker class abilities.
- **Prime Requisite:** None (no XP bonus)
- **Hit Die:** d4 (max 18)
- **Alignment:** Any Lawful
- **Armour:** None
- **Weapons:** Club, crossbow, dagger, hand axe, javelin, pole arm, spear, staff. No oil or poisons.
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 1 (additional at level 3, 5, 7, 9, 11, and 13)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -3 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 5d4 (NOT x10)
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]] only (per ancestry rules)
- **Multi-class options:** None
- **Restrictions:** No Strength bonus to hit/damage; no Dexterity bonus to AC; must donate all treasure beyond living expenses; only 5 magic items (max 2 weapons); limited magic item types; no followers until level 6; must defeat an 8th-level monk in unarmed combat to advance to level 8 (and similar combats at every level thereafter)
- **Class Abilities:** [[Open Hand Damage\|Weaponless Attack]], [[Stunning Blow\|Deadly Strike]], [[Climb Walls (Monk)\|thief skills]] including [[Move Silently (Monk)\|Move Silently (Monk)]] and [[Hear Noise (Monk)\|Hear Noise (Monk)]], [[Deflect Missiles\|Deflect Missiles]], improving AC with level, save vs hp damage = no damage if CON 11+
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** See per-level breakdown — [[Speak with Animals\|Speak with Animals]], [[Slow Fall\|Controlled Fall]], [[Mind-Shield\|Mind-Shield]], [[Self-Healing (Monk)\|Heal Self]], [[Self-Induced Catalepsy\|Self-Induced Catalepsy]], [[Immunity to Disease\|Immunity to Disease]], [[Immunity to Poison\|Immunity to Poison]], [[Speak With Plants\|Speak with Plants]], [[Resistance to Mind Control\|Resistance to Mind Control]], Establish Monastery (level 11), Immunity to Geas/Quest (level 12), [[Quivering Palm\|Harmonic Touch]] (level 13), Grand Master (level 17)
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,000 XP; Level 6 at 45,000 XP (followers permitted); Level 11 at 700,000 XP (monastery); Level 17 at 3,250,000 XP (Grand Master, must defeat existing Grand Master)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Establish a monastery at level 11; Temple of the Grand Master at level 17
