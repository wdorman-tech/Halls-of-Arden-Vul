## Notes

Treshub is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic evil god of storms, the sky, lightning, and fertility. He is the storm that breaks the harvest as easily as it nourishes it — capricious, dangerous, but also the bringer of rain. His symbol is a lightning bolt; his weapon, the spear.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]
- **Alignment:** [[Chaotic Evil\|Chaotic Evil]]
- **Gender:** M

- **Symbol(s):** Lightning bolt
- **Weapon(s):** [[Spear\|Spear]]
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Chaotic Evil\|Chaotic Evil]]
