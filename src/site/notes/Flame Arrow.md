## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 segment per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Arrows or bolts
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By means of this spell, the caster can enchant one arrow per segment, causing it to burst into normal flame. Each arrow causes 1 hp of fire [[Damage\|Damage]] in addition to its normal damage. The arrows (or crossbow bolts) will burn up in one round, so they must be shot before this time.

## Details

The enchanted arrows have no bonus to hit, but the enchantment allows them to hit a creature that can be damaged only by +1 magical weapons, even if the target creature is also immune to fire. Against creatures immune to non-magical weapons, the arrow inflicts its full piercing damage, not just the fire damage.
