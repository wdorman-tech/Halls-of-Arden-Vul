## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster separates their mind from the rational reality of the material plane, accepting the fundamental contradictions of extra-planar realities, and makes contact with the powers of the beyond. By means of this contact, the caster can gain "yes or no" answers to the questions they pose. One question may be asked per 2 caster levels. The caster decides how deeply they wish to probe into the realities beyond the material (unless contacting an elemental plane for information about that same plane).

## Details

The difficulty of the mental task is measured in terms of how many fundamental contradictions of reality the caster can assimilate and simultaneously juggle in their mind. The price of failure is insanity.

**Exception:** Contacting an elemental plane provides a 90% chance that the being contacted will have the knowledge sought, if asking about that particular elemental plane.

There is a 25% chance that the information received is wrong, misunderstood, or couched in such inherently contradictory language that it cannot be understood.

| Number of Fundamental Metaphysical Contradictions | Likelihood Knowledge Available | Likelihood Wrong Result | Chance of Insanity |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 60% | 35% | 5% |
| 2 | 65% | 33% | 10% |
| 3 | 70% | 30% | 15% |
| 4 | 75% | 27% | 20% |
| 5 | 80% | 25% | 25% |
| 6 | 85% | 22% | 30% |
| 7 | 90% | 19% | 35% |
| 8 | 95% | 15% | 40% |
| 9 | 98% | 10% | 50% |

(Reduce insanity chance by 5% per point of intelligence over 15.)

If the caster is driven insane, the insanity lasts 1d10 weeks, and there is a 1% chance of dying at the end of this period if the insanity is not removed (by [[Remove Curse\|Remove Curse]], [[Wish\|Wish]], etc.).
