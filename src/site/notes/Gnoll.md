## Notes

Gnoll is the laughing, barking language of [[Gnoll\|Gnoll]] packs. It is rarely friendly to learn — most adventurers who pick it up do so because they expect to fight gnolls, not negotiate with them.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Gnoll\|Gnoll]]
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Eavesdropping on gnoll raiders, rare diplomacy, reading gnoll-pack markings
