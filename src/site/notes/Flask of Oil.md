## Stats

- **Cost:** 1 sp per pint flask
- **Weight:** 1 lb per pint
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A pint flask of [[Oil, Lamp\|lamp oil]] used as a **grenade-like missile**.

- **Range Increment:** 10ft (-2 per increment after the first)
- **Direct hit damage** (when set alight): **2d6** fire damage from the point of impact, plus **1d6** in the second round.
- **Splash damage** (within 5ft, if alight): 1d3 hp
- **Container Must Break:** 25% chance of NOT breaking on impact unless prepared. Preparing is a round-long action; once prepared, the flask is no longer safe to transport.
- A flask of oil can be "prepared" as a Molotov cocktail and lit during the same preparing action, as long as the preparer has an open flame to light it with. Otherwise the spilled oil must be ignited by a successful hit from a lit [[Torch\|Torch]] (AC 10 [10] for moving targets).
- A miss can be calculated by direction (1d8) and automatically hits anyone in the adjacent space; if no one is adjacent, only splash damage occurs.
