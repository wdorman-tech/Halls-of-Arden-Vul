## Notes

A **blinded** creature, if it relies on sight, suffers severe combat penalties. Some creatures (e.g. those that hunt by smell or echolocation) may be unaffected by blindness.

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** -4 to hit, -4 on saving throws, AC worsened by 4
- **Duration:** depends on source (spells, magical darkness, blinding splashes)
- **Removal:** [[Cure Blindness\|Cure Blindness]], natural recovery if temporary, dispelling the source
- **Stacks with:** [[Concealment\|Concealment]] (concealment penalties on top of blindness for visible enemies)
- **Notable:** creatures not relying on sight are not affected
