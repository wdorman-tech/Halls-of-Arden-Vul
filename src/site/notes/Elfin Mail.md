## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 5 [Ascending: 15]
- **Movement Cap:** 120ft (no reduction)
- **Weight:** 15 lb
- **Cost:** Not sold (gift only)
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 4 in 2x2

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- [[Thief\|Thief]] (one of three armour types thieves can use, alongside [[Leather Armour\|Leather Armour]] and [[Studded Leather\|Studded Leather]])
- [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot wear metal armour
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear

## Notes

A fine, supple elven chain — same AC as ordinary [[Chain Mail\|Chain Mail]] but only half the weight, no movement cap, and quiet enough that thieves can wear it without disrupting their abilities.

**Not sold.** Elfin mail isn't bought; the elven smiths who make it occasionally gift it to those who do great deeds for the elven folk.
