## Notes

**Illusion / Phantasm** spells change how a creature, object, or place **appears** — or how the affected thing seems to other senses.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Illusion (rare)
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Illusion / Phantasmal Illusion
- **Primary class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] (the spell description first-word "phantasmal" identifies illusionist spells)
- **Examples:** [[Phantasmal Force\|Phantasmal Force]], [[Improved Phantasmal Force\|Improved Phantasmal Force]], [[Spectral Force\|Spectral Force]], [[Audible Glamer\|Audible Glamer]], [[Invisibility\|Invisibility]], [[Improved Invisibility\|Improved Invisibility]], [[Mirror Image\|Mirror Image]], [[Hypnotic Pattern\|Hypnotic Pattern]], [[Color Spray\|Color Spray]], [[Fascinate\|Fascinate]], [[Hallucinatory Terrain\|Hallucinatory Terrain]], [[Mass Invisibility\|Mass Invisibility]]
