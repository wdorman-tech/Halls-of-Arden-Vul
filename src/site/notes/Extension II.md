## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell lengthens the duration of another spell (level 1-4) that the caster has previously cast by 50%.

## Details

The previous spell must still be active, and may only be of level 1, 2, 3, or 4.

The additional duration on the spell is calculated by 50% of the spell's stated duration, not the time remaining.
