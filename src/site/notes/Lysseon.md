## Notes

Lysseon is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the chaotic neutral god of wine, indulgence, madness, rage, and creativity. He spans the wild span from inspired poet to raving maenad. His symbol is a bunch of grapes; his weapons are the flail and the sickle.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]]
- **Alignment:** [[Chaotic Neutral\|Chaotic Neutral]]
- **Gender:** M
- **Areas of Influence:** Wine, indulgence, madness, rage, creativity
- **Symbol(s):** A bunch of grapes
- **Weapon(s):** [[Flail\|Flail]], sickle
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Chaotic Neutral\|Chaotic Neutral]]
