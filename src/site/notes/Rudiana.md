## Notes

Rudiana is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic neutral goddess of battle, berserkers, and unrestrained violence. Where [[Envalios\|Envalios]] is the contract-killer of the gods, Rudiana is the battle-rage itself — the patron of those who lose themselves in fury. Her symbol is a red teardrop; her weapon, the axe.
