## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 3 rounds + 1 round per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster's outline becomes blurred, as if seen through shimmering waves of heat.

## Details

The caster gains a bonus of +1 against any targeted magical attack.

Any opponent's first attack against the caster has a penalty of -4, and subsequent attacks incur a penalty of -2 (after one attack, the opponent has become partially accustomed to the spell's distorting effect).
