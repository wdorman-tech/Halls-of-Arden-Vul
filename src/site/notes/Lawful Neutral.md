## Notes

Because you are Lawful, you have a strong tendency to obey and support authority. You probably believe that a well-administered set of laws is the best possible way of organising things, even if it goes wrong sometimes. Because you are Neutral on Good and Evil, you do not tend to help those in need unless an authority figure asks you to.

Lawful Neutral is the alignment of judges, bureaucrats, magistrates, and devoted servants of order.

## Key Facts

- **Axis:** Lawful on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Neutral on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** Lawful Neutral tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Ten\|The Ten]]):** [[Lucreon\|Lucreon]]
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]):** [[Basileus\|Basileus]], [[Yanion\|Yanion]]
- **Behavior:** the law is paramount, even when it is harsh
