## Notes

A **humanoid** is an ancestry or species of human-like creatures that are normally hostile or ill-disposed towards humankind. Goblins, hobgoblins, kobolds, and orcs are examples of humanoids. Humanoids contrast with [[Demi-human\|Demi-human]] creatures, which are usually friendly or neutral toward humans.
