## Notes

Recuperating is one of four standard downtime activities (see [[Downtime\|Downtime]]).

## The Rule

- **Cost:** Up to **500 GP per week**
- **Benefit:** **1 XP per GP** spent. **All lasting effects** (poison, injuries, lost HP) recover at **double the rate**.
- **Flavor:** Light training and condition maintenance while recovering from injuries.

## Why this matters

Recuperating turns gold into a healing accelerator. Whenever someone has a long-running condition or just got their teeth kicked in, this is the activity to take. The XP rate is the same as [[Training (Downtime)\|Training (Downtime)]] but capped at half the GP/week — so you cannot mainline this for max XP, but you can use it to bounce back faster.
