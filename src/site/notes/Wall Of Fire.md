## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th (Druid) / 4th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Druidic Evocation\|Druidic Evocation]] / [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 80ft (Druid) / 60ft (Magic-User)
- **Duration:** Concentration plus 1 round per druid level (Druid) / See below (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments (Druid) / 4 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster may evoke a wall of fire in one of two shapes: a wall or a ring. The size of a straight wall is up to one 20ft (height and length) square per caster level. A ring has a radius of up to 5ft per druid level (Druid) or 10ft + 3ft per MU level (Magic-User), with the caster in the centre, and is 20ft high. If the caster manifests the spell as a wall, the effect is stationary. The ring-shaped wall moves with the caster.

## Details

One side of the wall of fire emits the searing heat one would expect, but the other side emits merely a gentle warmth.

**Druid version:**
- Passing through the flames inflicts 4d4 fire damage + 1hp/druid level.
- Creatures as far as 10ft from the hot side of the wall incur 2d4 hit points of fire damage.
- Creatures as far as 20ft from the hot side are burned for 1d4 points of fire damage.
- [[Undead\|Undead]] creatures suffer worse from the power of this druidic magic, taking twice the ordinary damage the wall would inflict.

**Magic-User version:**
- Passing through the flames inflicts 2d6 fire [[Damage\|Damage]] + 1hp per magic-user level.
- Creatures as far as 10ft from the hot side of the wall incur 1d6 hit points of fire damage.
- Creatures as far as 20ft from the hot side are burned for 1d3 points of fire damage.

The wall persists for as long as the caster concentrates upon it, or, if they do not choose to maintain [[Concentration\|Concentration]], will remain for 1 round per caster level. The caster can end the spell at any time.
