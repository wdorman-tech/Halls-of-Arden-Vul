## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L (varies by [[Arrow\|arrow]])
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 60ft
- **Rate of Fire:** 2 per round
- **Weight:** 8 lb
- **Cost:** 100 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 2

## Notes

A bow constructed of laminated horn, wood, and sinew. Shorter than a normal [[Long Bow\|Long Bow]] (60ft vs 70ft increment) but more powerful and resilient. Often the choice of mounted archers.

Strength bonus does not apply to damage by default. Fires [[Arrow\|Arrow]]s.
