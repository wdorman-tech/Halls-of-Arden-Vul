## Notes

A spell is "**interrupted**" if any of these occur **before the caster completes the spell**:

1. **You lose any [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]]** (any damage at all)
2. **You become unable to perform a relevant component**, e.g. [[Grappling\|grappled]] while casting a spell with a somatic component, or [[Silence, 15ft Radius\|silenced]] while casting a spell with a verbal component
3. **You move** (take a movement action), or something else moves you 5 ft or more during casting
4. **A rule says** your spell is interrupted

When the spell is interrupted, **you forget the spell**, the spell has **no effect**, and the casting time ends.

## Key Facts

- **The spell slot is still consumed** even if disrupted — the spell vanishes from memory as if cast, but produces no effect
- **Pre-casting damage:** if the caster suffers damage *after declaring* the spell but *before casting begins* (e.g. enemy initiative beats caster's), the spell is still lost
- **No DEX bonus to AC** while casting
- **No movement** during casting (an action or being moved 5 ft+ disrupts)
- **Engagement OK:** can cast while in [[Engaged in Melee Combat\|melee combat]] but is much more vulnerable
- **Concentration spells:** broken by death, unconsciousness, stun, mesmerise, charm, sometimes substantial damage (GM's call)
- **Continuing casting time:** walking at half speed is allowed during continuing casts (exception to the move rule)
