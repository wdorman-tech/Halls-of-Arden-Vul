## Notes

Keen Detection is the heightened ability of [[Elf\|elves]] and [[Half-Elf\|half-elves]] to spot [[Secret Door\|Secret Door]]s and concealed objects (panels, hidden chests, etc.). The GM usually rolls this in secret. The elven version is stronger than the half-elven version: only full elves get a passive chance just from passing by, while half-elves only benefit when actively searching.

## Key Facts

- **Two categories of hidden things:**
  - **Secret:** built to be undetectable, or magically hidden
  - **Concealed:** simply covered or otherwise hidden
- **[[Elf\|Elf]] (full elf):**
  - Passing by: detect concealed/secret on a 1 on 1d6
  - Actively [[Searching\|searching]]: secret 1-2 on 1d6, concealed 1-3 on 1d6
- **[[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]]:**
  - Passing by: no passive bonus (must search)
  - Actively searching: secret 1-2 on 1d6, concealed 1-3 on 1d6
- **GM rolls in secret** so the player isn't tipped off
- Active searching takes time — see OSRIC Section 1.7.1.8
