## Notes

Wisdom is your alertness, instincts, willpower, self-control, task focus, and empathy. A wise character might be mature and kindly, an aloof hermit, or a manipulative mentor — high Wisdom is not always virtuous Wisdom. It is the prime requisite for [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s and [[Druid\|Druid]]s, and it grants a saving throw bonus against spells and effects that target the mind (charm, fear, and similar).

## Key Facts

- **Range:** 3-18 (19 by magical means)
- **Generation:** 3d6 in order, with one swap — see [[Modified Character Creation\|Modified Character Creation]]
- **Prime requisite for:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Mental saving throw modifier:**
  - 3: -3
  - 4: -2
  - 5-7: -1
  - 8-14: 0
  - 15: +1
  - 16: +2
  - 17: +3
  - 18: +4
  - 19: +5
- **Saving throw modifier (mental):** applies to charm, fear, and other spells/effects that target decisions or memory. The exact list is at GM discretion.
