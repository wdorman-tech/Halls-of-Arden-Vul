## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d8 vs S/M, 1d8 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 5
- **Space Required:** 2ft
- **Length:** 3ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Weight:** 4 lb
- **Cost:** 15 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A curved, slashing blade. Same OSRIC entry covers the cutlass — a naval variant.

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged). The [[Druid\|Druid]] is a notable exception: scimitars are sometimes their permitted blade in OSRIC variants, but base OSRIC restricts druids from edged weapons.
