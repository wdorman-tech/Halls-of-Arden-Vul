## Notes

Half-Orc Resilience is the toughness implied by the [[Half-Orc\|half-orc]] +1 [[Constitution\|Constitution]] adjustment and their high CON minimum (13-19). Unlike [[Dwarf\|dwarves]], [[Gnome\|gnomes]], and [[Halfling\|halflings]], half-orcs do not get the [[Stalwart\|Stalwart]] save bonus chart in the OSRIC text — their resilience is expressed as raw [[Constitution\|Constitution]] and [[Hit Point\|Hit Point]] benefits rather than a named save adjustment. Their only listed ancestral feature beyond [[Infravision\|Infravision]] is the +1 STR / +1 CON / -2 CHA adjustment and their broad class access.

## Key Facts

- **Mechanical expression:** +1 [[Strength\|Strength]], +1 [[Constitution\|Constitution]], -2 [[Charisma\|Charisma]] from ancestry
- **Effective minimum CON:** 13 (after the +1 adjustment, required range is 13-19)
- **Other half-orc trait:** [[Infravision\|Infravision]] (60ft)
- **Who has it:** [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]] only
- **Note:** OSRIC does not give half-orcs a named [[Stalwart\|Stalwart]]-style chart; their toughness manifests through high CON and the resulting [[Hit Point\|Hit Point]] / system-shock benefits
