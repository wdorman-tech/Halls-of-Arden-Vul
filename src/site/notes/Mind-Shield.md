## Notes

[[Mind-Shield\|Mind-Shield]] is a [[Monk\|Monk]] ability that prevents anyone from reading the monk's mind — including ESP spells and natural telepathy. It is gained at level 4 and increases in reliability each level.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 4th
- **Mechanics:** Percentage chance — roll under to succeed
- **WIS Requirement:** Higher base chance with Wisdom 15+
