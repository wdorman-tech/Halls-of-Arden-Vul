## Stats

- **Cost:** 1 gp per dozen
- **Weight:** 5 lb per dozen
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 per dozen

## Notes

Heavy iron spikes — typically driven with a [[Hammer\|Hammer]]. Used for **wedging doors closed or open** (preventing pursuers from following), securing climbing routes, or as anchors. A standard dungeoneering staple.
