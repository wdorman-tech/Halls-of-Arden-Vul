## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** Until completed
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

This spell allows you to set a quest for one target creature, which they are compelled to perform. The target must be a human, demi-human, or humanoid.

## Details

The target can choose to attempt a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] vs spells and other effects, and must add its Wisdom saving throw modifier to the roll. If it passes, the spell ends without effect.

The quest can't be one that would change the target's alignment if completed. It must be within the target creature's power to complete, and it must be possible to complete it within a year and a day.

You can end the spell at any time, and any cleric of higher level than you can end the spell by touching the target and willing it to end.

The spell also ends when your target creature returns to you with proof that the quest has been completed; or, if it doesn't know where you are, to the place where you cast the spell on it; or when the target dies; or if it becomes impossible to complete the quest. For example, if the quest was to kill a creature, and that creature somehow dies; or if the quest was to retrieve an object, and that object is destroyed.

At the end of each 24 hours your target procrastinates or avoids the quest, it suffers a cumulative penalty of -1 to all its saving throws. These 24 hour periods don't have to be continuous. The target is allowed to rest, eat, drink, sleep, prepare spells, and maintain its equipment as part of its daily activities, as long as it's trying in good faith to perform the quest.
