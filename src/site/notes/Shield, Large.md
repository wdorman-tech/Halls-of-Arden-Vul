## Armour Stats

- **AC bonus:** -1 [+1]
- **Movement Cap:** none
- **Weight:** 10 lb
- **Cost:** 15 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A large shield blocks its bonus against **3 incoming attacks per round**. You can only use a shield if your attacker is in front of you or attacking from your shield side, and not against attackers you aren't aware of.
