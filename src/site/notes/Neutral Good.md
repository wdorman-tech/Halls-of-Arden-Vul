## Notes

Because you are Neutral on Law and Chaos, you take a pragmatic approach to laws, organisations, and authority. Organisations can sometimes be the best approach, but they can also become corrupt; you follow the law when it serves Good and disregard it when it does not. You probably do not consider yourself bound by your promises. Because you are Good, you have a strong tendency to help those in need and protect the weak.

Neutral Good is the alignment of pragmatic do-gooders, helpful wanderers, and merchants who genuinely care.

## Key Facts

- **Axis:** Neutral on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Good on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** Neutral Good tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]):** [[Melkarton\|Melkarton]]
- **Behavior:** does good without being constrained by law or chaos
