## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 9th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 9 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell places the victim into a magical sleep so deep that it is akin to suspended animation. The victim does not age or change in any way and does not need to breathe or eat. The caster can also cast this spell to undo a temporal stasis.

## Details

The sleep lasts until the victim is awakened by the use of [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]] or if this spell is cast to awaken the recipient rather than put them into stasis.
