## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 12 turns + 6 turns per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell grants the caster or a creature they touch with the ability to see in the dark, perceiving variations of heat in the infrared spectrum. The range of this vision is 60ft.

## Details

Lamps and other light sources tend to spoil the effects of [[Infravision\|Infravision]].

Note: Infravision does not allow the caster to detect invisibility.
