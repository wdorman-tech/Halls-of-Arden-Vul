## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Cleric) / 4th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Abjuration\|Divine Abjuration]] / [[Arcane Abjuration\|Arcane Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One creature, object, or place
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments (Cleric) / 4 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None (Cleric) / See below (Magic-User)

## Notes

This spell ends one curse that has been placed on the targeted creature, object, or place.

## Details

Curses inflicted by gods, demigods, artifacts, or relics aren't affected by this spell.

This spell doesn't cure [[Vampirism\|Vampirism]] or [[Lycanthropy\|Lycanthropy]].

If the target is a creature, then a curse on it ends. If there are several curses, then the caster can choose which one ends.

If the target is a cursed object, then the curse on it doesn't end, but a creature that's holding the cursed object is allowed to drop the item and be free of it.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Bestow Curse\|Bestow Curse]].
