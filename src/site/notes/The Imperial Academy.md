## Notes

The Imperial Academy is a small collegium of generalist [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s — but its strict admissions and its membership drawn from the upper echelons of nobility make it disproportionately politically influential. If you want to know what a senator's mage is plotting, ask the Academy.

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Generalist (with elite curriculum)
- **Reputation:** Politically influential, exclusive
- **Membership profile:** Upper nobility; patrician families
- **Schools of magic favored:** Broad — emphasizes versatility befitting a courtier
- **Patronage:** Tied to imperial structure
