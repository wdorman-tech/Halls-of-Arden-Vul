## Notes

**Experience points** (XP) measure a character's progress toward the next [[Level\|Level]]. The base [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] rules describe XP as earned in three main ways:

1. **Treasure recovered:** 1 XP per 1 gp value of monetary treasure (coins, gems, jewellery) safely returned to a village, town, or city. Magic items kept (rather than sold) are still worth some XP — typically one-tenth of base value, or one-half for potions.
2. **Defeating monsters:** XP for killing, capturing, or otherwise overcoming monsters. Amounts are listed in the GM Guide.
3. **Mission/story rewards:** the GM may award XP for fulfilling adventure goals.

Treasure gained through non-adventuring means (such as taxes from a stronghold) does not grant XP.

> [!warning] Houserule: gold no longer grants XP in this campaign
> The base-game **1 XP per 1 gp recovered** rule is **removed** by the [[Modified XP and Leveling\|Modified XP and Leveling]] houserule. In the Halls of Arden Vul, gold itself awards no XP — instead, XP comes from monster kills, story/mission rewards, and especially from **[[Exploration Awards\|Exploration Awards]] / [[Tasks (XP)\|Tasks (XP)]]** earned during play. Gold can still be *spent* for XP via [[Downtime\|Downtime]] activities ([[Training (Downtime)\|Training (Downtime)]], [[Connecting (Downtime)\|Connecting (Downtime)]], [[Carousing\|Carousing]], [[Recuperating (Downtime)\|Recuperating (Downtime)]]). Read [[Modified XP and Leveling\|Modified XP and Leveling]] before using anything below.

## Key Facts

- **Treasure (base OSRIC):** 1 XP per 1 gp value, only if recovered through adventure and brought back safely — **removed by [[Modified XP and Leveling\|Modified XP and Leveling]]**
- **Magic items:** 1/10 base value (1/2 for potions) if kept rather than sold
- **Prime Requisite bonus:** up to +10% — see [[Experience Bonus\|Experience Bonus]]
- **Level threshold:** see your class's XP table; reaching it triggers level-up after a safe rest — see [[Gaining Levels\|Gaining Levels]] (training cost is **also removed** by houserule)
- **Houserule sources of XP:** [[Tasks (XP)\|Tasks (XP)]] (Minor / Major / Extraordinary task completion), [[Carousing\|Carousing]] outcomes, monster kills, story rewards, and gold *spent* on downtime
