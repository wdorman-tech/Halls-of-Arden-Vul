## Notes

[[Find/Remove Traps\|Find/Remove Traps]] (Traps) is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to detect and disarm mechanical hazards on objects, doors, and items. Magical components of magical traps cannot be disarmed by this skill — clerics or magic-users have spells for that. The thief may also set traps if they have suitable materials.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] same table; [[Monk\|Monk]] also has it)
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll
- **Find Traps Time:** 1 round per object/item examined
- **Limitations:** Cannot generally check a whole room or floor — that's done with a 10ft pole. Failure on the find-traps roll does NOT trigger the trap, but you also don't know it's there.
- **Magical Traps:** May find the mechanical part, not the magical part. Spells like [[Detect Magic\|Detect Magic]] are needed for the magical components.
- **Disarm Traps:** Once a trap is found (or otherwise spotted), the thief may attempt to remove it. Need to know the mechanism exists already (e.g. tripwire, pressure plate).
- **Setting Traps:** With suitable materials and time. Setting an ordinary tripwire usually takes 1d20 rounds. Some traps may be triggered by passing dungeon vermin rather than the intended target.
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
