## Notes

Charisma is your character's persuasiveness and personal magnetism. High Charisma does not require beauty — picture a successful salesperson or politician. Charisma affects NPC interactions, the loyalty of your followers, and how many henchmen you can keep.

## Key Facts

- **Range:** 3-18 (19 by magical means)
- **Generation:** 3d6 in order, with one swap — see [[Modified Character Creation\|Modified Character Creation]]
- **Prime requisite for:** none (but matters for any face character)
- **Sidekick (henchman) limit:**
  - 3-4: 1
  - 5-6: 2
  - 7-8: 3
  - 9-11: 4
  - 12-13: 5
  - 14: 6
  - 15: 7
  - 16: 8
  - 17: 10
  - 18: 15
  - 19: 20
- **Loyalty modifier:** bonus/penalty to follower morale checks. Scales from -30 at CHA 3 to +50 at CHA 19.
- **Reaction modifier:** bonus/penalty to NPC reaction rolls and persuasion attempts. Scales from -25 at CHA 3 to +40 at CHA 19.
- **Henchmen** are character-class NPCs who stick around long-term. Hiring them is a higher-level activity.
