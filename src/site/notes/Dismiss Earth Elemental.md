## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Conjuration\|Conjuration/Summoning]]
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** 1 turn per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell returns one elemental creature to the elemental plane of earth.

## Details

This spell can affect any creature native to the elemental plane of earth, including (but not limited to) earth elementals and xorn.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Conjure Earth Elemental\|Conjure Earth Elemental]].
