## Notes

Strength of Will is the [[Elf\|elven]] near-immunity to [[Sleep\|Sleep]] and [[Charm Person\|charm]] magic. Whenever a spell would put the elf to sleep or charm them, they roll d100: on 01-90 the spell has no effect; on 91-00 the spell may affect them as normal but they still get any allowed [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]. Half-elves get a much weaker version called [[Strength of Mind\|Strength of Mind]] (30%).

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** total immunity on a roll of 01-90 on d100 against [[Sleep\|Sleep]] or [[Charm Person\|charm]] effects
- **On a 91-00:** the spell may affect normally, but a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] is still allowed if one would normally apply
- **Who has it:** [[Elf\|Elf]] only
- **Half-elven version:** [[Strength of Mind\|Strength of Mind]] (only 30% immunity)
