## Notes

The slot-based inventory grid is a video-game-style tracking tool: every item the character carries is placed on a grid of slots. The grid is purely organisational — there are **no carrying-capacity penalties** and no [[Strength\|Strength]]-based gating. Use it to keep an at-a-glance picture of what each character (and their pack animals) is hauling.

> [!info] **No carry-weight penalties.** Item weights in pounds are kept on equipment notes as a descriptive property, but they are not tracked against any allowance and do not modify movement, surprise, or anything else (campaign houserule — see [[Houserules Index\|Houserules Index]]).

## The Grid

Characters track items on a 7-row × 6-slot grid (42 slots total). Pack animals have their own grid — see species notes (e.g., [[Mule\|Mule]] = 6 × 4 = 24 slots).

## Slot Sizes & Stacking

| Item Size | Slots | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Small** *(stacks ×4)* | 1 (shared) | [[Torch\|Torch]], [[Arrow\|Arrow]], [[Bolt, Light Crossbow\|Bolt, Light Crossbow]], [[Dart\|Dart]], [[Rations, Standard\|Rations, Standard]], [[Dagger\|Dagger]] |
| **Medium** | 1 | most one-handed weapons, [[Hammer\|Hammer]], [[Holy Symbol, Wooden\|Holy Symbol, Wooden]], [[Lock\|Lock]], [[Bedroll\|Bedroll]] |
| **Large** | 2 *(in a line)* | [[Two-Handed Sword\|Two-Handed Sword]], [[Bastard Sword\|Bastard Sword]], [[Halberd\|Halberd]], [[Long Bow\|Long Bow]], [[Heavy Crossbow\|Heavy Crossbow]], [[Shield, Medium\|Shield, Medium]], [[Shield, Large\|Shield, Large]] |
| **Very Large** | 3 *(in a line)* | [[Tent\|Tent]], [[Pot, Iron\|Pot, Iron]], cooking gear, [[Wagon, Small\|Wagon, Small]] |
| **Coins** | 1 *(stacks ×50)* | mixed coinage |

Large/Very Large items can rotate (horizontal or vertical) but slots must always be in a straight line.

## Armour Footprints

Armour is rigid — place the entire footprint as a contiguous block on the grid (or just track it as worn).

| Armour | Slots | Movement |
|---|---|---|
| [[Leather Armour\|Leather Armour]], [[Elfin Mail\|Elfin Mail]], magically light | 1 | None |
| [[Padded Armour\|Padded Armour]], [[Studded Leather\|Studded Leather]], [[Ring Mail\|Ring Mail]], [[Chain Mail\|Chain Mail]], [[Banded Mail\|Banded Mail]] | 4 *(2×2)* | 90 ft cap |
| [[Splint Mail\|Splint Mail]], [[Scale Mail\|Scale Mail]], [[Plate Mail\|Plate Mail]] | 9 *(3×3)* | 60 ft cap |
| Non-AC clothing ([[Robe, Linen\|Robe, Linen]], etc.) | 0 | None |

Worn armour does not occupy grid slots — note it under "Worn / Held" instead. The movement cap on heavier armour comes from [[Armour Class\|Armour Class]] / [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]], not from slot tracking.

## Containers

Containers do not occupy slots themselves — they **encompass** slots that other items go into. Items must be in a container unless **worn** (armour, cloak, belt) or **held** (weapon in hand, lantern).

| Container | Slots Encompassed | Special |
|---|---|---|
| [[Backpack\|Backpack]] | 9 *(3×3)* | Free at character creation |
| [[Quiver\|Quiver]] | 1–2 | Ammunition stacks ×12 (instead of ×4) |
| [[Flask\|Flask]] | 1 | Liquids stack ×3 |
| [[Writing Satchel\|Writing Satchel]] | 1 | 1 book + 10 [[Parchment\|Parchment]] + writing tools (or 2 scrolls in place of parchment) |
| [[Pouch\|Pouch]] / Purse | 1 or 4 *(2×2)* | Coins, small items |
| [[Sack\|Sack]] | 4 *(2×2)* or 9 *(3×3)* | Bulk goods |
| [[Scroll Case\|Scroll Case]] | 1 | Up to 3 scrolls |

## Worn / Held (off the grid)

Items being **worn** or **actively held** do not consume inventory slots:
- Armour you have on
- Weapon in your hand (or sheathed at your belt — GM's call)
- Lantern / torch in your free hand
- Cloak, belt, boots, gloves, helmet
- A pouch or purse worn at the belt
- Holy symbol on a chain around the neck
