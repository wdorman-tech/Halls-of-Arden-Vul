## 📜 Notes

A blank slot-based inventory grid for use with the [[Slot-Based Inventory\|Slot-Based Inventory]] houserule. The grid is purely organisational — there are no carry-weight penalties.

🖨️ This is a printable / fillable sheet — duplicate it and rename for each character, or use the inline grid in [[Templates/Player Template\|Player Template]].

## 🛍️ Containers

```
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ ✦ ━ Sacks, Packs & Pouches ━ ✦ ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

> [!info] 📦 Containers don't occupy slots themselves — they **encompass** slots (item placed inside the encompassed area). Items must be in a container unless **worn** (armour, cloak, belt) or **held** (weapon in hand, lantern).

| 🛍️ Container | 🎒 Slots Encompassed | ✨ Special Rule |
|---|---|---|
| 🎒 [[Backpack\|Backpack]] | 9 *(3×3)* | 🆓 Free at character creation |
| 🏹 [[Quiver\|Quiver]] | 1–2 | Ammunition stacks ×12 (instead of ×4) |
| 🧪 [[Flask\|Flask]] | 1 | Liquids stack ×3 |
| 🪶 [[Writing Satchel\|Writing Satchel]] | 1 | Holds 1 book + 10 [[Parchment\|Parchment]] + writing tools (or 2 scrolls in place of parchment) |
| 👛 [[Pouch\|Pouch]] / Purse | 1 or 4 *(2×2)* | Coins, small items |
| 🛍️ [[Sack\|Sack]] | 4 *(2×2)* or 9 *(3×3)* | Bulk goods |
| 📜 [[Scroll Case\|Scroll Case]] | 1 | Up to 3 scrolls |

> [!tip] 🎒 Containers themselves don't take a slot — they encompass a region of the grid, and items go into those encompassed slots.

## 🧥 Worn / Held *(off the grid)*

```
   ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ ✦ ━ What You Wear, What You Hold ━ ✦ ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
```

> 📖 Items being **worn** or **actively held** do not consume inventory slots:

- 🛡️ Armour you have on
- ⚔️ Weapon in your hand *(or sheathed at your belt — GM's call)*
- 🕯️ Lantern / torch in your free hand
- 🧥 Cloak, belt, boots, gloves, helmet
- 👛 A pouch or purse worn at the belt
- ✝️ Holy symbol on a chain around the neck
