> [!abstract]+ 🎒 Slot rules — quick reference
> - 🪙 **Small** items stack 4 per slot (torches, arrows, daggers, rations)
> - 📦 **Medium** items take 1 slot (most gear, one-handed weapons)
> - 📏 **Large** items take 2 adjacent slots in a line (two-handed weapons, medium+ shields)
> - 🏕️ **Very Large** items take 3 adjacent slots in a line (tents, cooking pots)
> - 💰 **Coins** stack 50 per slot
> - 🛍️ **Containers** (backpack 3×3, sack, pouch, quiver, etc.) don't take slots — they encompass slots
> - 🛡️ **Armour:** 4 slots (2×2) for banded/padded/studded/ring/chain; 9 slots (3×3) for splint/scale/plate; 1 slot for leather/elfin/magically light
