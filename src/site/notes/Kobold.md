## Notes

Kobold is the yipping, hissing language of [[Kobold\|Kobold]] warrens. It is full of words for traps and tunnels — practical for the deep dungeoneer.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Kobold\|Kobold]]
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Negotiation with kobolds, reading their trap markings and warrens
