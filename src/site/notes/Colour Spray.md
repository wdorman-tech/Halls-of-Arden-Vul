## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 segment
- **Area of Effect:** Cone, 5ft wide at origin, 45-degree arc, length of 10ft per illusionist level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

The caster fans out their fingers, and a sheet of unearthly-coloured light springs forth to stun or blind any creature caught in the cone-shaped area of effect.

## Details

**First limitation:** No more than 1d6 creatures caught within the light may be affected.

**Second limitation:** Any creature with hit dice exceeding the caster's (and creatures of 6+ HD regardless of caster level) are entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]. All others are not entitled to a saving throw.

Within those limitations, see what creatures might be affected, starting with the lowest hit dice creatures:

- Creatures with HD equal to or less than the caster's are rendered unconscious for 2d4 rounds.
- If the creature's hit dice exceed the caster's by 1-2, the creature is [[Blinded\|Blinded]] for 1d4 rounds.
- If the creature has 3+ hit dice in excess of the caster's, it is merely [[Stunned\|Stunned]] for 2d4 segments.
