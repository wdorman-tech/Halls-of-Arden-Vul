## Stats

- **Cost:** ~8 gp per vial (varies)
- **Weight:** -- (negligible)

## Notes

A vial of writing ink, used with a [[Quill\|Quill]] to write on [[Parchment\|Parchment]] or [[Vellum\|Vellum]]. Higher-grade and exotic inks (gold, dragon's blood, etc.) are required to inscribe scrolls and high-level spells in a [[Spellbook\|Spellbook]].
