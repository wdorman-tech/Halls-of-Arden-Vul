## Notes

A **prone** character or monster cannot attack and may be attacked at **+4 to hit**. If they are not also [[Overborne\|overborne]], they can rise to their feet **as an action** when it is their turn to act.

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** cannot attack; attacked at +4; cannot use shield bonus; cannot use [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] bonus to AC
- **Recovery:** stand up as a normal action — *unless* also [[Overborne\|overborne]]
- **Duration:** until the character rises (or is no longer overborne)
- **Removal:** rise on your action; remove [[Overborne\|Overborne]] condition first if applicable
- **Stacks with:** [[Overborne\|Overborne]] (typical), [[Staggered\|Staggered]], [[Grappling\|Grappling]], [[Stunned\|Stunned]], [[Unconscious\|Unconscious]]
- **Common sources:** [[Overbearing\|Overbearing]] attacks, falls, certain spells (e.g. [[Earthquake\|Earthquake]], [[Reverse Gravity\|Reverse Gravity]])
