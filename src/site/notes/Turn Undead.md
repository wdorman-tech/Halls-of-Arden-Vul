## Notes

[[Turn Undead\|Turn Undead]] is the [[Cleric\|Cleric]]'s signature ability against the unliving. Good and neutral clerics cause undead and certain lesser fiends to retreat, and may even destroy them outright. Evil clerics instead make undead non-hostile or bring them under their control as servants and minions.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] (full progression); also [[Paladin\|Paladin]] from level 3 (see [[Turn Undead (Paladin)\|Turn Undead (Paladin)]])
- **Mechanics:** Treated as casting a spell with casting time of 0 segments — cannot attack or cast a spell in the same round, but the turning attempt cannot be interrupted
- **Effect (Good/Neutral Clerics):** Undead and lesser fiends retreat, possibly destroyed
- **Effect (Evil Clerics):** Undead become non-hostile or come under the cleric's control as servants/minions
- **Detailed Tables:** See OSRIC Chapter Six, Section 6.5 for the full turning matrix
- **Optional Rule for Evil Clerics:** Some GMs allow evil clerics to turn paladins. The [[Paladin\|Paladin]] counts as Type 6, plus 1 per 2 paladin levels (round down) — so a 5th-level paladin = Type 10.
