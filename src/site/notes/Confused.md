## Notes

**Confusion** normally arises from the [[Confusion\|Confusion]] spell and similar spell-like effects. The confused creature is acting randomly according to a table of results. If not otherwise specified, general confusion references the level 7 druidic spell [[Confusion\|Confusion]].

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** acts randomly each round per the [[Confusion\|Confusion]] table — possible attack on allies, wander, stand confused, etc.
- **Duration:** spell-dependent (typically rounds per caster level)
- **Removal:** [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]], end of duration, possibly [[Remove Curse\|Remove Curse]]
- **Stacks with:** other conditions (you can be confused AND prone, etc.)
- **Sources:** [[Confusion\|Confusion]] spell, [[Insanity\|Insanity]] (phobia trigger), certain monster gazes, [[Symbol\|Symbol]]
