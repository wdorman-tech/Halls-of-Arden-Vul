## Notes

[[Hide in Shadows\|Hide in Shadows]] is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to disappear from view — behind cover, in a crowd, or in any unlit area. Success means observers cannot see the thief; the thief may move at half speed while hidden. Combined with [[Move Silently\|Move Silently]], it sets up a [[Backstab\|Backstab]].

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] using same table; also a [[Monk\|Monk]] thief skill)
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll; succeed if rolled equal to or under the listed percentage
- **OSRIC Note:** You DO know whether the roll succeeded (unlike original AD&D). On failure, you may try again next round.
- **Conditions:** Must have cover, a crowd, or somewhere not directly lit; observers must not already know you're there
- **Movement:** May move at half speed while hidden
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
