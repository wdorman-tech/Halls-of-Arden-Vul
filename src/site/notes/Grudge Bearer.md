## Notes

Grudge Bearer is the [[Dwarf\|dwarven]] offensive trait against their hereditary enemies. Every dwarf is trained from youth to fight [[Goblin\|Goblin]]s, [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]]s, [[Hobgoblin\|Hobgoblin]]s, and [[Orc\|Orc]]s, and when attacking one of these creatures the dwarf adds +1 to their [[To-Hit Roll\|To-Hit Roll]].

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** +1 to-hit
- **Applies vs:** [[Goblin\|Goblin]]s, [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]]s, [[Hobgoblin\|Hobgoblin]]s, [[Orc\|Orc]]s
- **Who has it:** [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]] only
- **Stacks** with other to-hit bonuses (Strength, magic weapons, etc.)
