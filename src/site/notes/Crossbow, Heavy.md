## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d4+1 vs S/M, 1d6+1 vs L
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 80ft
- **Rate of Fire:** 1/2 (one shot every 2 rounds)
- **Weight:** 12 lb
- **Cost:** 20 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A powerful crossbow that requires a winch or windlass to span. **Cannot be used from horseback** — only a footman can brace it correctly before firing. The slow rate of fire (1 shot every 2 rounds) reflects this complex reload.

Strength bonus does not apply to damage. Fires heavy [[Bolt, Heavy Crossbow\|crossbow bolts]].
