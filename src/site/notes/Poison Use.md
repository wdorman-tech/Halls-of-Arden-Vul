## Notes

[[Poison Use\|Poison Use]] (Brew Poison) is a 9th-level [[Assassin\|Assassin]] ability allowing the character to brew their own poisons. Lower-level assassins may use poisons they obtain elsewhere, but the ability to manufacture them in-house is reserved for the most skilled killers.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]]
- **Level Required:** 9th level
- **Mechanics:** Stated in the rulebook as a class ability; specific recipes, costs, and time per poison are determined by the GM
- **Note:** [[Monk\|Monk]] characters are explicitly forbidden from using poisons.
