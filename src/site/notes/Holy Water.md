## Stats

- **Cost:** 25 gp per vial
- **Weight:** 0.5 lb
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1 per vial

## Notes

A vial of consecrated water. Treated as a **grenade-like missile**:

- **Range Increment:** 10ft (-2 per increment after the first; -2 at 11-20ft, -4 at 21-30ft, etc.)
- **Direct hit damage** (vs undead): **2d4** hit points unless otherwise stated
- **Splash damage** (undead within 5ft of impact): 2 hp
- **Container Must Break:** 25% chance of NOT breaking on impact unless prepared. Preparing takes a round; once prepared, the flask is no longer safe to transport — it will leak if not used.
- A miss can be calculated by direction (1d8) and automatically hits anyone in that adjacent space; if no one is adjacent, only splash damage occurs.

Holy water is also used in some [[Cleric\|clerical]] rituals.
