## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Area of Effect:** Fan of flame 5ft long in a 120-degree arc
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

While casting this spell, the caster touches their thumbs together, and a sheet of flame explodes from their fingers to burn opponents within the spell's short (5ft) range. The flames inflict 1 hp of [[Damage\|Damage]] per magic-user level (no [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] applies).

## Details

Any combustible items caught in the sheet of flame must pass a saving throw against normal fire or be destroyed.

Note: The original description of this spell called for a 3ft range. We have changed this to 5ft to clarify that this is a combat spell fully capable of hitting opponents in an adjacent "square", but not beyond.
