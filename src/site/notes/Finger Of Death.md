## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

The casting of this spell culminates when you point a finger at your chosen victim. If the target fails a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]], it dies.

## Details

The spell only affects normally living creatures, so it does not affect [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], [[Golems\|Golems]], or [[Undead\|Undead]].
