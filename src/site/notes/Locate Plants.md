## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Divination\|Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** Radius 5ft per druid level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows you to detect the presence of a specific type of plant you choose to locate.

## Details

You must concentrate in order to gain the insight granted by the spell.

The divination is not blocked by any intervening substance, of any thickness, so it can "see" through walls, thick growths of trees, and into burrows.
