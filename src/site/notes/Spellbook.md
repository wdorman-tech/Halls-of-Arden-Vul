## Stats

- **Cost:** 25 gp (blank)
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A blank, leather-bound book in which a [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] or [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] inscribes their spells. Required for spell preparation. Inscribing new spells uses [[Ink\|Ink]] (often special inks for higher-level spells), [[Quill\|Quill]], and [[Parchment\|Parchment]] (or directly into the book).

[[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s and [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]s receive a starting spellbook free as part of character creation.
