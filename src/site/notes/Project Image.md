## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th (Illusionist) / 6th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]] / [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** 5ft per illusionist level / 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round per caster level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments (Illusionist) / 6 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates an illusory duplicate of the caster, standing in another place within the spell's range. The image must remain visible to the caster or the spell will end. Much like [[Mirror Image\|Mirror Image]], the projected image mimics all of the caster's movements, but it is not affected by attacks of any kind.

## Details

The image possesses a magical link to the caster. If desired, the caster can cast spells originating at the image rather than at their location, as if the image, rather than the caster, had cast the spell. Thus, the effective range of an attack spell can be increased, or a spell that would ordinarily centre on the caster could be brought into effect around the projected image.
