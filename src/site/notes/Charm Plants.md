## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** 30ft x 10ft
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell grants the caster the ability to mentally communicate with (for a period of 1 turn) and command plants (the obedience is permanent) in the spell's area of effect.

## Details

No unusual abilities are granted to the plants; they have no additional capabilities beyond those they had before the spell was cast.

Only intelligent plants are entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]], but at a penalty of -4.
