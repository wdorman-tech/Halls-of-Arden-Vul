## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** 30ft x 30ft square
- **Components:** S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

The caster traces magical patterns in the air with a lit stick of incense or other small light source. Any creature in the area of effect that looks at the traceries of light must make a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] or remain in place, transfixed by the patterns.

## Details

The spell can affect a maximum of 25 hit dice worth of creatures.

The effect continues as long as the caster chooses to continue tracing the patterns in the air.
