## Notes

Cure Disease is both a [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]] spell and a [[Paladin\|Paladin]] class ability.

## Spell Stats (Cleric / Druid)

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Cleric) / 3rd (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]] (Cleric) / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]] (Druid)
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched / One creature
- **Components:** V, S (Cleric) / V, S, M (Druid)
- **Casting Time:** 10 rounds (Cleric) / 1 round (Druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None (Cleric) / Permitted; negates effect (Druid)

## Spell Details

The target creature is cured of all diseases, although full recovery may take up to a week.

Many diseases are described in terms of "the victim will die within X days", and this spell stops that counting process; the target of the spell is no longer at any risk of dying from a disease. The fact that they may not have made a "full recovery" yet simply means that they are not yet at full strength.

This spell cures physical pathologies and removes physical parasites. It can't cure genetic conditions or birth defects, and it only affects mental health conditions to the extent that curing any underlying pathology or parasite could.

The spell only affects normally living creatures, so does not affect [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], [[Golems\|Golems]], or [[Undead\|Undead]].

This spell can only cure [[Lycanthropy\|lycanthropy]] if it's cast before the first time the affected creature changes into lycanthrope form. After the creature has changed into lycanthrope form, its lycanthropy becomes a complex condition that's part disease, part curse, and partly a magical alteration to its essential nature.

The target creature can choose to attempt a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] vs spells and other effects, and if it passes, one or more diseases of its choice are not cured by this spell.

This spell also has some special uses where it cures other effects that aren't precisely diseases.

## Paladin Ability (Curing Touch)

[[Paladin\|Paladin]]s can cast the third-level divine spell Cure Disease without using a spell slot, with use frequency increasing with level.

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)
- **Mechanics:** Cast the 3rd-level divine spell Cure Disease on one living creature touched
- **Spell Slot:** Not required
- **Frequency:**
    - 1st-4th level: Once per week
    - 5th-9th level: Twice per week
    - 10th level and higher: Three times per week
