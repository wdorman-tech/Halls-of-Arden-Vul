## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster may evoke one of two different forms of ice storm with this spell: a hailstorm or a sleet storm.

## Details

The hailstorm is a vicious barrage of huge hailstones that pound into a 40ft x 40ft area, throwing up bits of shrapnel-like ice fragments as the hail shatters. Any creature within the area suffers 3d10 hit points of damage with no [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]].

The sleet storm is a maelstrom of frozen rain in an 80ft x 80ft area, [[Blinded\|blinding]] all creatures in the area of effect and making the footing so slippery that all movement is cut by 50%. There is also a 50% probability that any creature attempting to move while in the sleet storm will slip and fall prone.
