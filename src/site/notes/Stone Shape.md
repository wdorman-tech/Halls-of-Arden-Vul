## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Druid) / 5th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Druidic Transmutation\|Druidic Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** 3 cubic ft + 1ft per druid level / 1 cubic ft per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to shape stone by the power of their will. They can form it into whatever object or shape they desire, from a weapon to a sculpture, to an ornate stone footstool. Whether used to create gargoyle statues or to make an escape hole from a stone prison, this spell is extremely versatile and useful.
