## Notes

Because you are Neutral on Law and Chaos, you take a pragmatic approach to laws, organisations, and authority. You probably believe organisations and laws are sometimes useful and sometimes corrupted; you follow law only when it supports Evil, and you do not feel bound by your word. Because you are Evil, you act in your own interest with no limits on the harm you will do to others.

Neutral Evil is the alignment of cold opportunists, corrupt courtiers, secret-keeping mages whose pursuit of power knows no rule, and professional villains.

- **Axis:** Neutral on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Evil on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** Neutral Evil tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
