## Notes

[[Spell Research\|Spell Research]] covers the high-level magical crafting abilities of the [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] (Eldritch Craft) and [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] (Phantasmal Craft) — brewing potions, writing scrolls, recharging aimed magic items, and eventually creating permanent magic items. Both classes follow nearly identical mechanics. Note that the [[Cleric\|Cleric]] (Divine Craft, level 7) and [[Druid\|Druid]] (Druidic Craft, level 7) have parallel abilities with their own restrictions.

## Key Facts

### Magic-User (Eldritch Craft) — level 7
- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Abilities:** Brew potions; write arcane scrolls; recharge aimed magic items (rods, staves, wands)
- **Scroll Requirement:** Must know all spells on the scroll
- **Ingredients:** Always required; some costly, some hard to acquire

### Illusionist (Phantasmal Craft) — level 7
- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Abilities:** Brew potions; write phantasmal scrolls; recharge aimed magic items
- **Scroll Requirement:** Must know all spells on the scroll

### Brewing Potions (Magic-User and Illusionist)
- **Lab and Alchemist:** Required (ongoing expense)
- **Cost:** Half the gp value of the potion
- **Time:** 1 day of uninterrupted work per 50 gp of cost
- **Health Hazard:** Brewing more than 100 days/year may cause GM-determined health hazard

### Writing Scrolls
- **Time:** Full day per spell level
- **Cost:** 50 gp per spell level
- **Failure:** 20% chance of complete failure (you'll know the scribing failed)
- **Exhaustion:** Writing scrolls more than 30 days/year increases failure risk to 40%

### Recharging Aimed Magic Items
- Cast the appropriate spell into the item (or closest GM-approved spell if the item's effect isn't a normal spell)
- **Risk:** Each new charge: item must save vs. spells using YOUR target number; failure destroys it
- **Zero Charges:** A 0-charge item cannot be recharged — it is no longer magical

### Eldritch Power (Magic-User level 12)
- Create permanent magic items if you know [[Enchant an Item\|Enchant an Item]]
- More difficult than potions/scrolls; rarer ingredients
- Success not guaranteed

### Phantasmal Power (Illusionist level 14)
- Create magic items if you know the spell [[Alter Reality\|Alter Reality]]
- Same caveats as Eldritch Power

### Divine Craft (Cleric level 7) and Druidic Craft (Druid level 7)
- Parallel abilities with religion-specific potion options
- Brewing potion of healing: 200 gp, 2 days
- Brewing potion of extra-healing: 400 gp, 4 days
- Druids: only 10 days/year on the druidic calendar are appropriate for brewing — exceeding this triggers 50% failure chance unless divine intervention
- Cannot create scrolls or potions for personal monetary profit (cleric/druid restriction)
