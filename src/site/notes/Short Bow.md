## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L (varies by [[Arrow\|arrow]])
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 50ft
- **Maximum Range:** 250ft (5 increments)
- **Rate of Fire:** 2 per round
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Cost:** 15 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A shorter, lighter two-handed pulled bow. Less powerful than a [[Long Bow\|Long Bow]] but easier to handle in tight spaces.

Strength bonus does **not** apply to damage by default. Fires [[Arrow\|Arrow]]s.
