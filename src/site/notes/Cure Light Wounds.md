## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st (Cleric) / 2nd (Druid)
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]] / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S (cleric) / V, S, M (druid)
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments (cleric) / 4 segments (druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The target creature regains 1d8 [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]], up to its normal maximum. Any healing over the creature's normal maximum is wasted. You must be able to see and touch the target creature.

## Details

This spell can't cure diseases, reattach limbs, bring back the dead, or heal any other affliction that isn't an injury measured in hit points. These more serious injuries must be healed with more powerful spells.

In general, the spell only affects normally living creatures. It has no effect on [[Golems\|Golems]], [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], or the [[Undead\|Undead]].

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Cause Light Wounds\|Cause Light Wounds]].
