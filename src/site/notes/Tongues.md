## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th (Cleric) / 3rd (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Divination\|Divine Divination]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster (Cleric) / Nil (Magic-User)
- **Duration:** 1 turn (Cleric) / 1 round per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** 30ft radius around caster
- **Components:** V, S, M (Cleric) / V, M (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments (Cleric) / 3 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell magically translates spoken language for the caster. It also magically translates the caster's words for other creatures within the area of effect.

The Magic-User version's area of effect does not move with the caster.

## Details

Only the caster can hear the magical translation when other creatures speak, and only if the words are spoken within the area of effect. Everyone within the area of effect can hear the magical translation of the caster's words.

Only spoken words are affected. Writing, and non-verbal communication such as hand signals, are not.

Creatures only understand the caster if they naturally speak at least one language.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Blither\|Blither]] (Cleric) or [[Babble\|Babble]] (Magic-User).
