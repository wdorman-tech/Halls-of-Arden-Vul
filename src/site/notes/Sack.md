## Stats

| Type | Capacity | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Small sack | 1 cubic ft / 10 lbs | 15 cp |
| Large sack | 4 cubic ft / 40 lbs | 1 gp |

- **Weight:** negligible (empty)
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 (and counts what it carries)

## Notes

A canvas or burlap bag, often used for hauling loot out of a dungeon. The large sack matches a [[Backpack\|Backpack]] in capacity but has no straps — must be hand-carried or shoulder-slung.
