## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 80ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One metal object
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 9 segments
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

Using this spell, you may select a single object of metal and change its substance to wood.

## Details

The spell affects up to 8 lbs of metal per druid level. The item is not entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]], but magic items have a flat 90% chance of being unaffected by the spell.
