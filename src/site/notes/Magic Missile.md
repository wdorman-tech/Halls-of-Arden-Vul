## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 60ft + 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** 1+ creatures in a 10ft x 10ft area
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

When this spell is cast, missiles of magical energy dart forth from the caster's fingertips. They automatically hit the intended targets, even if the targets are in melee or are partially concealed.

## Details

The missiles inflict 1d4+1 points of magical [[Damage\|Damage]], and can be allocated to different targets.

Magic-users of first and second level can launch a single magic missile.

For every two magic-user levels beyond first, the caster gains an additional missile. A first-level magic user can cast 1 missile, a third-level caster may cast 2, a fifth-level caster may cast 3, and so on.
