## Notes

[[Deflect Missiles\|Deflect Missiles]] is the [[Monk\|Monk]]'s ability to bat aside arrows and other thrown weapons. It uses a saving throw vs. petrifaction and only works against non-magical missiles.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Mechanics:** Saving throw vs. petrifaction
- **Restrictions:** Cannot deflect magical missiles
