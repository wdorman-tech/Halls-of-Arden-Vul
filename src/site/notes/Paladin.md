## Notes

The [[Paladin\|Paladin]] is a holy knight, a paragon of righteousness sworn by sacred oaths to the cause of Lawful Good. A sub-class of [[Fighter\|Fighter]] requiring high [[Charisma\|Charisma]] and [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]], the paladin gains [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]], [[Lay on Hands\|Lay on Hands]], [[Cure Disease\|Cure Disease]], [[Aura of Protection\|Aura of Protection]], [[Immunity to Disease\|Immunity to Disease]], [[Turn Undead (Paladin)\|Turn Undead]], a [[Warhorse\|Holy Steed]], and eventually [[Paladin Spellcasting\|cleric spellcasting]]. Breaking their vows costs all paladin powers.

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 12, DEX 6, CON 9, INT 9, WIS 13, CHA 17
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Strength\|Strength]] and [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] (10% XP bonus if both 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d10 (max 9)
- **Alignment:** Lawful Good only
- **Armour:** Any
- **Weapons:** Any
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 3 (additional at level 5, 8, 11, 14, and 17)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -2 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** Available as optional rule (see [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]])
- **Initial Gold:** 5d4 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]] only
- **Multi-class options:** None (Paladin is human-only and cannot multi-class)
- **Restrictions:** Max 10 magic items total (1 magic armour, 1 magic shield); must tithe at least 10% to Lawful Good causes; donate excess income to Lawful Good causes; must always remain Lawful Good; cannot associate with non-Good characters
- **Class Abilities:** [[Aura of Protection\|Aura of Protection]] (Shielded by Faith — better save table), [[Immunity to Disease\|Immunity to Disease]] (Boon of Health), [[Cure Disease\|Curing Touch]], [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]], [[Aura of Protection\|Protection From Evil 10ft]], [[Lay on Hands\|Lay on Hands]] (2 hp/level)
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 2 — Heroic Assault; Lvl 3 — [[Turn Undead (Paladin)\|Turn Undead]] (as cleric 2 levels lower); Lvl 4 — [[Warhorse\|Holy Steed]], Melee Attack Combination 3/2; Lvl 8 — Mighty Blow; Lvl 9 — [[Paladin Spellcasting\|Paladin Spellcasting]] (as 1st-level cleric, advancing to 8th-level cleric at paladin 16); Lvl 9 — Stronghold; Lvl 13 — Melee Attack Combination 2/1
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,550 XP; Level 9 at 325,000 XP (name level — stronghold and spellcasting); Level 16 at 2,750,000 XP (caps at 8th-level cleric casting)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Stronghold at level 9; no automatic followers (most are put off by paladin standards)
- **Special Items:** May seek a [[Holy Sword\|Holy Sword]], a paladin-specific magic weapon
