## Notes

Elven is the flowing, vowel-rich language of the [[Elf\|Elf]] ancestry. Half-elves and many elf-friends learn it; it is a common second language among scholars who study fae or sylvan magic.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Elf\|Elf]] (native), [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]]
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Sylvan diplomacy, ancient elf inscriptions, elf-only spell components and tomes
