## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 day per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell creates a false magical aura around a single object, which cannot weigh more than five pounds per magic-user level of the caster. A [[Detect Magic\|Detect Magic]] spell will incorrectly perceive the item as magical.

## Details

If the item is actually handled by a person who has cast a detect magic spell, the caster is permitted a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] to realise that the aura is, in fact, not a true magical aura but a counterfeit.
