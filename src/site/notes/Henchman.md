## Notes

A **henchman** is a character-class NPC who is willing to accompany a player character on adventures. Henchmen are a long-term relationship that becomes available at higher level — they're more committed and capable than [[Hireling\|hirelings]]. The maximum number a character may have is set by their [[Charisma\|Charisma]] (the [[Sidekick\|Sidekick]] Limit).

## Key Facts

- **Maximum:** the Sidekick Limit from [[Charisma\|Charisma]] table
- **Loyalty:** modified by Charisma; affects [[Morale\|Morale]] checks
- **Class:** has a [[Class\|Class]] like a PC
- **Long-term:** unlike hirelings (typically short-term hires)
- **Earns XP:** typically half of a PC share, advancing on the same tables
- **Reaction modifier:** the PC's CHA reaction can help recruit them
- **In combat:** rolls morale checks like NPCs
- **Loyalty rules:** see Section 1.7.4 / 2.2.4 (Loyalty of Hirelings and Henchmen)
