## Notes

Debellaton is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the lawful evil god of death, might, "order through might," and sacrifice. He is the patron of conquerors who believe the strong must rule, and of cults that sanctify death itself as a structuring force. His symbols — skull, scythe, black disk — leave no doubt about his domain. His weapon is the flail.
