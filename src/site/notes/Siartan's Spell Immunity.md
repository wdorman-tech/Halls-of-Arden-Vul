## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Abjuration\|Arcane Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn per level, divided among recipients
- **Area of Effect:** One creature per 4 MU levels
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round per recipient
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

A creature warded by this spell gains a +8 bonus to [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s against any magical attack that affects the mind.

## Details

The caster may divide the spell's duration among as many recipients as their caster level divided by four. The material component for this spell is a gem of any kind or size (but at least 25gp).
