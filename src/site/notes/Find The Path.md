## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Divination\|Divination]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 rounds
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

You (or another creature you touch to cast the spell) magically know a route to one specific destination of your choice. If there are several routes, the shortest one is the only one known. If there's no route to the destination, you learn this and the spell ends.

## Details

The creature knows the route, but not exact distances or directions, so it can't for example draw an accurate map. Although the route is always one that the creature could use, it doesn't know about any dangers or hazards on the route.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Hide The Path\|Hide The Path]].
