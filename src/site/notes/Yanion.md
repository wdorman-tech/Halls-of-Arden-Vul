## Notes

Yanion is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the lawful neutral goddess of medicine, thresholds, and decisions — the twin of [[Banion\|Banion]]. Where her brother is the wild god of trickery, Yanion governs the sober, lawful moments of healing and decision-making. She is patron of physicians and of liminal spaces: doorways, bridges, the moment between question and answer. Her symbol is a half-empty vial; her weapon, the club.
