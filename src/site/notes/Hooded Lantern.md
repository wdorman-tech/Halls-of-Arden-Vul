## Stats

- **Cost:** 7 gp
- **Weight:** 2 lb
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A lantern with metal hood and shutters that can shield the flame and reduce the [[Light Sources\|light]] in chosen directions. Burns [[Oil, Lamp\|lamp oil]]. Provides 30-foot radius of bright light.

Sheltered from drafts; can be partially shuttered for stealthy approach.
