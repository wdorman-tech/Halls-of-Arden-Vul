## Notes

A **demi-human** is an ancestry or species of human-like creatures who are normally friendly or well-disposed towards humankind. Dwarfs, elves, and gnomes are examples of demi-humans. Demi-humans contrast with [[Humanoid\|Humanoid]] creatures, which are normally hostile or ill-disposed towards humans.
