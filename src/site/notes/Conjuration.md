## Notes

**Conjuration / Summoning** spells move objects and creatures from one place or plane to another (**conjuration**) or call creatures from nearby (**summoning**). Conjuration doesn't create objects, creatures, or effects out of nothing — but since they are often called from other planes of existence and appear out of thin air, it usually appears that the object or creature was created on the spot.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Conjuration
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Conjuration
