## Notes

Training is one of four standard downtime activities (see [[Downtime\|Downtime]]).

## The Rule

- **Cost:** Up to **1,000 GP per week**
- **Benefit:** **1 XP per GP** spent
- **Requirement:** You must train with an established NPC in town who has agreed to train you. The relationship matters — finding and keeping a trainer is part of the activity.

## Why this matters

This is the highest-XP-per-GP downtime option, but it requires an in-fiction trainer relationship. That tethers training to the social fiction: who knows whom, who owes whom, who is willing to teach.
