## Notes

At 9th level — the cleric's name level — the [[Cleric\|Cleric]] gains enough divine favour and mortal renown to establish a temple of their own. This is the cleric's stronghold ability, equivalent to the [[Build Stronghold (Fighter)\|fighter's castle]] or the [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]'s wizard tower.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level Required:** 9th (High Priest/Priestess)
- **Land:** Must be acquired by purchase or conquest
- **Construction:** Must build (or cause to be built) a suitable complex of buildings honouring the deity or pantheon
- **Followers:** 3d6+2 NPC zealots and acolytes who share the cleric's faith and can fight as 0-level mercenaries
- **Note:** Druids do NOT build strongholds — they may occupy a magical grove (see [[Druid\|Druid]])
