## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 10ft radius sphere or 20ft radius hemisphere
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell transforms water or other non-magical liquids into a bubble of air in the area surrounding the caster. The bubble has the same density as water and will not support the caster in the water; the caster will sink or float as normal, surrounded by the bubble's atmosphere.

## Details

The oxygen content of the air in the bubble does not run out until the spell expires.
