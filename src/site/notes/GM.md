## Notes

Short for "**Game Master**," the GM is the person whose job is to create the game world, play all the characters who aren't controlled by anyone else (non-player characters, or NPCs), and adjudicate the rules. The GM is not normally called a "[[Player\|player]]"; the GM runs the game rather than playing it. The GM's job is to make sure the game is fun and challenging for the players.
