## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Magical disembodied hand
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates a magical hand with the same number of [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] as the caster's (uninjured) hit points. The caster can cause the hand to be as small as their own, or as large as a shield. The caster selects a foe, and the hand places itself between the caster and that particular enemy, moving so rapidly that it cannot be avoided. It is strong enough to hold back any creature weighing 200 lbs or less, and will slow heavier opponents to half movement.

## Details

The movement rates of opponents of truly massive size (such as dragons) will be adjudicated by the GM, either being slowed by a quarter movement rate or pushing the hand aside entirely.
