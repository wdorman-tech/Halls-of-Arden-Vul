## Notes

Basileus is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the lawful neutral deity of **The Emperor** — the deified imperial office of the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]]. Basileus is depicted as either male or female depending on the current Emperor, and is venerated wherever imperial authority stretches. The laurel wreath is the symbol; the mace is the weapon.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]

- **Areas of Influence:** The Emperor
- **Symbol(s):** Laurel wreath
- **Weapon(s):** Mace
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Lawful Neutral\|Lawful Neutral]]
