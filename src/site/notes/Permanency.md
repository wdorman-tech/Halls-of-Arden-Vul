## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** See below
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** One object or creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 rounds
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The permanency spell makes the temporary effects of other spells permanent (keeping in mind that "permanent" spells can still be dispelled). It is also used to lock the enchantments of a magic item permanently into the item in tandem with the [[Enchant an Item\|Enchant an Item]] spell, in which case the effect cannot be dispelled. Certain spells can be made permanent upon the caster or another creature, and other spells can be made permanent only if cast upon an area or an object.

## Details

Each use of this spell reduces the caster's constitution by 1 point if it is cast on a creature. If cast on an item or non-living substance, there is only a 5% chance that this will happen.

Spells that can be made permanent upon a person or other creature include: [[Comprehend Languages\|Comprehend Languages]], [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]], [[Detect Invisibility\|Detect Invisibility]], [[Detect Magic\|Detect Magic]], [[Infravision\|Infravision]], [[Protection From Evil\|Protection From Evil]], [[Protection from Normal Missiles\|Protection from Normal Missiles]], [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]], [[Tongues\|Tongues]] and [[Unseen Servant\|Unseen Servant]]. Only a magic user of higher level than the caster can dispel these spells.

Spells that can be made permanent upon an area or object include: [[Enlarge\|Enlarge]], [[Fear\|Fear]], [[Gust of Wind\|Gust of Wind]], [[Invisibility\|Invisibility]], [[Magic Mouth\|Magic Mouth]], [[Prismatic Sphere\|Prismatic Sphere]], [[Stinking Cloud\|Stinking Cloud]], [[Wall Of Fire\|Wall of Fire]], [[Wall of Force\|Wall of Force]] and [[Web\|Web]]. These spells may be dispelled by any caster subject to the normal rules for [[Dispel Magic\|Dispel Magic]].
