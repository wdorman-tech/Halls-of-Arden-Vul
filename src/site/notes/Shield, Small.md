## Armour Stats

- **AC bonus:** -1 [+1]
- **Movement Cap:** none
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Cost:** 10 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 1

## Notes

A small shield can block its bonus against **1 incoming attack per round**. You can only use a shield if your attacker is in front of you or attacking from your shield side, and not against attackers you aren't aware of. Decide whether to use the shield bonus just before the attacker's roll.

[[Druid\|Druid]]s can use only wooden shields without metal bosses or bindings; treat them as small shields.
