## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 round per cleric level
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell creates fear in a targeted creature unless it succeeds at a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]]. The targeted creature must be normally living, so not a [[Constructs\|construct]], [[Elementals\|elemental]], [[Golems\|golem]], or [[Undead\|Undead]]. A to-hit roll is usually required to touch the target.

## Details

The target creature applies its Wisdom save modifier, if it has one, to the saving throw.

Until the spell ends, the target always moves its full movement such that when it's finished moving, it's at the greatest possible distance away from you. If there are several destination points that are equally distant, it can choose which one it moves to.

A creature can't cast spells while it's affected by fear.

An affected creature can't attack unless both (a) the target of the attack is between it and an exit route, and (b) it has no other way round the creature. If it attacks a creature while afraid, it has a penalty of -4 on its attack roll.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Remove Fear\|Remove Fear]].
