## Notes

The [[Thief\|Thief]] lives by their wits and skills, the party's expert at finding traps, picking locks, climbing walls, and getting out of any place they got into. Driven by [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]], thieves use stealth and a devastating [[Backstab\|Backstab]] to compensate for poor combat ability. They speak the secret language [[Thieves' Cant\|Thieves' Cant]] and may at high levels found or take over a [[Thieves' Guild\|Thieves' Guild]].

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 6, DEX 9, CON 6, INT 6, CHA 6
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] (10% XP bonus if DEX 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d6 (max 10)
- **Alignment:** Neutral or evil (some GMs allow any non-Lawful Good)
- **Armour:** Leather, padded, or studded leather only; no shields
- **Weapons:** Club, dagger, dart, oil, sling, most swords (not bastard or two-handed swords), torch
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 2 (additional at level 6, 10, 14, and 18)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -3 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 2d6 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** All
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Class Abilities (Thief Skills):** [[Backstab\|Backstab]], [[Climb Walls\|Climb Walls]], [[Hide in Shadows\|Hide in Shadows]], [[Hear Noise\|Hear Noise]] (Listen), [[Pick Locks\|Pick Locks]], [[Pick Pockets\|Pick Pockets]], [[Read Languages\|Read Languages]], [[Move Silently\|Move Silently]] (Move Quietly), [[Find/Remove Traps\|Find/Remove Traps]] (Traps)
- **Special Languages:** [[Thieves' Cant\|Thieves' Cant]] (automatic for all thieves)
- **Skill Modifiers:** Dexterity adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C) and Ancestry adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 10 — Establish [[Thieves' Guild\|Thieves' Guild]] (or stronghold), Read Scrolls (1d4 — on a 1 it fails; otherwise 5% chance of reversal/harm)
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 1,250 XP; Level 5 at 10,000 XP; Level 10 at 160,000 XP (name level — Guildmaster)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Found or take over a [[Thieves' Guild\|Thieves' Guild]] at level 10; the Guild is typically 6d4 NPC thieves of varying levels
