## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** 6 rounds
- **Area of Effect:** Cube, 50ft x 50ft x 50ft
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The selected creatures in the area of effect can choose to add +1 to their "to hit" rolls in combat and +5% to their morale checks.

This spell is normally used as a blessing on your allies (and yourself), granting bonuses in combat and morale checks. An alternative use is to bless an object or place — possible even if memorised for blessing people. This typically has no game mechanical effects, but can influence events outside the ordinary rules (such as undoing the effects of a wicked action in the past, or laying the dead fully to rest). Part of the ritual for making holy water is to bless the water to be affected, and in some cases blessed missiles might do special harm to some kinds of evil creature.

## Details

This spell can target creatures within the area of effect that aren't in melee when the spell is cast (must be 10ft distant from enemies). When you cast the spell, you can choose any number of creatures and decide they aren't affected.

## Reversed Form

The reverse of this spell is [[Curse\|Curse]].
