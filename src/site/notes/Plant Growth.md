## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Druid) / 4th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Druidic Transmutation\|Druidic Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 160ft (Druid) / 10ft per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** 20x20ft square per druid level / 10x10ft square per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M (Druid) / V, S (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** 1 round (Druid) / 4 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell causes plants and vines to grow with unbelievable speed, forming a tangled barrier of thick vegetation.

## Details

The enchantment may be dispelled, or the plants cleared by normal means. Chopping a way through the barrier is possible, but movement is restricted to 10ft/turn (20ft for size L creatures).

There must be some sort of plants already growing in the area of effect, even if they are sparse. The spell does not create plants, it just causes them to grow.
