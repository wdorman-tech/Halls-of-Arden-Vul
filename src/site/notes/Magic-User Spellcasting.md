## Notes

[[Magic-User Spellcasting\|Magic-User Spellcasting]] is arcane magic — the most complex and powerful spellcasting tradition in OSRIC. The [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] uses a [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]], must understand a spell before memorising it, and must memorise a spell before casting it. The principles are beyond mortal understanding; even brilliant magic-users grasp them only in fleeting inklings.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] (and [[Ranger\|Ranger]] from level 9, see [[Ranger Spellcasting\|Ranger Spellcasting]])
- **Spell Source:** [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]] required
- **Spell Levels:** 1 through 9
- **First 9th-Level Spell Slot:** Earned at magic-user level 18
- **Starting Spells:** 4 first-level spells from among the 30 first-level arcane spells. 2 random, 2 chosen. Read Magic must be one of them — pick it as a chosen spell if you didn't roll it. (See campaign houserule on [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] page — Read Magic is granted free.)
- **Spell Acquisition:** Roll d100 vs INT for each new spell encountered (Table 1.3.6.2A):

| INT | Chance to Understand | Max Spells Understood per Spell Level |
|-----|---------------------|---------------------------------------|
| 9-10 | 35 | 6 |
| 11-12 | 45 | 7 |
| 13-14 | 55 | 9 |
| 15-16 | 65 | 11 |
| 17 | 75 | 14 |
| 18 | 85 | 18 |
| 19 | 90 | 22 |

- **Memorisation:** Empty spell slots filled by memorising spells from the spell book; cast spells "empty" the slot until re-memorised. Same spell may be memorised in multiple slots. Memorised spells may be voluntarily forgotten to free the slot.
- **Free Spell on Level-Up:** When you gain a level, you can choose one arcane spell of any level you can cast and add it to your spell book — no money or roll required.
- **Auto-Understand:** When you gain a level you may automatically understand one spell of your choice (replaces the older "minimum spells understood per level" system).
- **Drop and Reroll:** If you've maxed spells understood for a level, you may drop a known spell to attempt a new one (gambling — dropped spell is gone unless you re-roll for it).
- **Read Magic Required:** Must cast [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]] on any spell book or scroll before attempting to copy spells from it.
- **Copying Cost:** 100 gp per spell level for magical ink; 1 hour per spell level to copy. Copying erases the source spell, eventually destroying the source scroll or book.
- **Scrolls:** May cast any arcane spell from a scroll (destroys the scroll) without prior memorisation
- **No Bonus Spells:** Unlike clerics and druids, magic-users do NOT get bonus spell slots from high attribute scores
