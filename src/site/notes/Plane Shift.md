## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Conjuration\|Conjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Creatures touched
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Depends on use of spell (see below)

## Notes

One unwilling creature, or up to 7 willing creatures whose hands are joined in a circle, plus, optionally, the caster, are instantly transported to one of the Outer Planes such as the Heavens or the Abyss. If the creature is native to an Outer Plane, it's returned to its plane of origin. If it isn't native to an Outer Plane, the caster can choose which Outer Plane it's sent to.

## Details

If you successfully touch a creature who does not want to be affected by the spell, it can choose to attempt a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] vs spells and magical effects. It must apply its Wisdom saving throw modifier to this saving throw. If it passes, the spell doesn't affect it.
