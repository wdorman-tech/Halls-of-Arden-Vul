## Notes

Aranyania is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the True Neutral goddess of woodlands, nature, growing things, time, and balance. She is the natural patron of [[Druid\|Druid]]s and rangers, and a cornerstone of [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] worship. Her symbol is the oak leaf; her weapon, the longbow.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]
- **Alignment:** [[True Neutral\|True Neutral]]
- **Gender:** F
- **Areas of Influence:** Woodlands, nature, growing things, time, balance
- **Symbol(s):** Oak leaf
- **Weapon(s):** Longbow
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[True Neutral\|True Neutral]]; the natural patron of [[Druid\|Druid]]s
