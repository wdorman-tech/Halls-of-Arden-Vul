## Notes

A **sidekick** is OSRIC's general term for a [[Henchman\|Henchman]] — a character-class NPC who is willing to accompany your character on adventures. Your **Sidekick Limit** (the maximum number of henchmen you can have) is determined by [[Charisma\|Charisma]].
