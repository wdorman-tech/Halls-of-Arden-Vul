## Notes

Adventuring rumours are bits of information about **adventuring opportunities** — leads on dungeons, factions, lost loot, monstrous problems, and other actionable hooks. They might or might not be accurate, but each one has at least **some** truth in it.

## Sources

- **Starting characters get 1d3** at character creation (see [[Modified Character Creation\|Modified Character Creation]]).
- **Backup / post-start characters get none** at creation.
- **Carousing roll of 8** earns 1 (see [[Carousing\|Carousing]] effects table).
- The GM may award them in play.

## Why this matters

Adventuring rumours are the campaign's **breadcrumb system** — the way new adventures, factions, and treasure leads enter the players' attention. They are deliberately seeded into character creation and into the carousing economy so that the world keeps offering hooks.
