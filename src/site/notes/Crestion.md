## Notes

Crestion is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the chaotic god of poetry, music, the arts, creative impulses, and selfishness. His worshippers are bards, painters, sculptors, and the kind of artist whose pursuit of beauty knows no fences — for good or ill. The lyre and the cluster of wild berries are sacred to him; the bow is his weapon.
