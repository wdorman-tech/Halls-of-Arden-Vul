## Notes

[[Paladin Spellcasting\|Paladin Spellcasting]] begins at paladin level 9 — at which point the paladin casts divine spells as a 1st-level [[Cleric\|Cleric]]. The casting level advances slowly thereafter, capping at 8th-level cleric casting at paladin level 16. Paladins do NOT receive bonus spells from high Wisdom.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 9th
- **Casting Tradition:** Divine, drawn from the [[Cleric\|Cleric]] spell list
- **Starting Casting Level:** 1st (regardless of paladin level being 9)
- **Casting Level Progression:** +1 per paladin level past 9th, until paladin level 16 (caster level 8). Caps thereafter — caster level remains 8 no matter how many paladin levels are gained.
- **Maximum Spell Level:** 4th (paladin spellcasting reaches a 4th-level slot at paladin 15)
- **Bonus Spell Slots:** None — paladins do NOT get bonus spells for high Wisdom
