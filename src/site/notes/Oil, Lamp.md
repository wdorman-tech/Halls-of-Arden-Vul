## Stats

- **Cost:** 1 sp per pint
- **Weight:** 1 lb per pint
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 per pint

## Notes

Standard fuel for [[Bullseye Lantern\|Bullseye Lantern]]s, [[Hooded Lantern\|Hooded Lantern]]s, and [[Lamp, Bronze\|lamps]]. Sold by the pint. A lantern typically burns one pint over four hours.

A pint of oil can also be used as a [[Flask of Oil\|flask of oil]] grenade — see [[Flask of Oil\|Flask of Oil]] for the combat rules.
