## Notes

Archontean is the language of the [[Archontean\|Archontean]] culture and the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]]. With [[Common\|Common]] absent from the setting, Archontean functions as the de facto common tongue wherever the empire's reach has spread — it is the language of imperial law, commerce, and the [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]].

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Archontean\|Archontean]] (native), most educated peoples in imperial territory
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left; available as a language swap at character creation
- **Setting role:** Imperial lingua franca; written law and contracts; magic curricula at most collegia
