## Stats

- **Cost:** 12 gp
- **Weight:** 3 lb
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A directional lantern with a focusing lens that projects a beam of [[Light Sources\|light]] in a forward cone. Burns [[Oil, Lamp\|lamp oil]]. Ideal for dungeon exploration where you want a bright, narrow shaft of light.

Typical illumination: a 60ft beam in a 20ft-wide cone.
