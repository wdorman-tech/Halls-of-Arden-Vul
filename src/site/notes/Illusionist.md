## Notes

The [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] is an **entirely separate class** that shares many mechanics with the [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] (spell book, Vancian preparation, d4 hit die, no armour, dagger/dart/oil/staff weapon list) but is **not** a sub-class of magic-user. Illusionists practice phantasmal magic — spells that influence senses and minds rather than reshape matter. They need both [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] and [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]], rely on a spell book, and record their formulas in a secret [[Phantasmal Notation\|Phantasmal Notation]] that ordinary magic-users cannot read without [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]]. Their spells go to seventh level, and they may eventually establish a manse, mansion, or manor.

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 6, DEX 16, INT 15, WIS 6, CHA 6
- **Prime Requisite:** None (no XP bonus)
- **Hit Die:** d4 (max 10)
- **Alignment:** Any
- **Armour:** None
- **Weapons:** Dagger, dart, oil, staff
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 1 (additional at level 6, 11, and 16)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -5 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 2d4 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]], [[Gnome\|Gnome]] (per ancestry rules)
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Class Abilities:** [[Illusionist Spellcasting\|Illusionist Spellcasting]], [[Phantasmal Notation\|Phantasmal Notation]] (read phantasmal scrolls without [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]])
- **Spell Acquisition:** Roll d100 vs INT for chance to understand each new phantasmal spell (Table 1.3.5.2A)
- **Starting Spells:** 3 first-level spells (2 random, 1 chosen)
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 7 — Phantasmal Craft (potions, scrolls, recharging items); Lvl 10 — May build a manse, mansion, or manor; Lvl 14 — Phantasmal Power (create magic items if you know [[Alter Reality\|Alter Reality]])
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 2,500 XP; Level 7 at 60,000 XP (Phantasmal Craft); Level 10 at 220,000 XP (stronghold)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Manor or mansion at level 10; no automatic followers
