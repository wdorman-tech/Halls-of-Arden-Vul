## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 12+)
- **Damage:** 2d4 vs S/M, 1d6+1 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 5
- **Space Required:** 4ft
- **Length:** 3.5ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Weight:** 7 lb
- **Cost:** 10 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A heavy-bladed sword, broader and heavier than a [[Long Sword\|Long Sword]]. One-handed use requires STR 12+.

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
