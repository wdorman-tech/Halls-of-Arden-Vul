## Notes

In certain situations, a character's gear may be damaged or destroyed. This is a major risk when facing dragon's breath, falling into acid vats, falling into deep pits, and getting hit by [[Fireball\|fireball spells]].

**Generally**, if a player character has to make a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] and **succeeds**, the gear is assumed to pass all its saving throws automatically. The Item Saving Throw table is only used **when the player character fails their save**.

## Key Facts

- **Roll:** d20, must equal or exceed the listed number for that material vs. that damage type
- **All magical items:** +2 on all saves
- **+1 item:** +2 (the +2 baseline)
- **+2 item:** +3 saves; **+3 item:** +4 saves; etc.
- **Artifacts/relics:** saving throws of 2 or 3 in all categories; even if they fail, only temporarily neutralised (1d6 days)
- **Falls:** one save per 10 ft fallen — UNLESS the character lands on something soft (only one save total)

| Metal | 7 | 1 | 6 | 20 | 1 | 2 | 1 | 11 |
| Paper | 16 | 2 | 11 | 20 | 1 | 20 | 18 | 20 |

| Wood | 10 | 1 | 10 | 20 | 2 | 7 | 5 | 10 |
