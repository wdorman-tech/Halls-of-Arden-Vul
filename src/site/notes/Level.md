## Notes

"**Level**" has several meanings in OSRIC. Context tells you which one is meant.

- **Character Level:** a character's progression on their experience chart. Higher level = more hit points, better attacks, better saving throws, and (for casters) access to higher-level spells. See [[Gaining Levels\|Gaining Levels]].
- **Spell Level:** the relative power and complexity of a spell, from 1 to 9 for full casters. A character's spell level is *not* the same as their character level — for example, a 6th level cleric cannot cast 6th level cleric spells.
- **Monster Level / Hit Dice:** the relative power of a creature, expressed in [[Hit Die\|hit dice]].
- **Dungeon Level:** the depth into a [[Dungeon Adventure\|dungeon]]. Level 1 is closest to the surface; deeper levels contain stronger monsters and larger treasures, allowing players to choose their level of risk.
