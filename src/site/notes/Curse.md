## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** 6 rounds
- **Area of Effect:** Cube, 50ft x 50ft x 50ft
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

Chosen creatures in the area of effect must take a penalty of -1 to their "to hit" rolls in combat and -5% to their morale checks.

## Details

This spell can target creatures within the area of effect that aren't in melee when the spell is cast (must be 10ft distant from enemies). When you cast the spell, you can choose any number of creatures and decide they aren't affected.

Like a [[Bless\|Bless]] spell, a curse spell can be used to curse an object or place, but the effect is too minor to have any rules-based consequence.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Bless\|Bless]].
