## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Abjuration\|Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** 1 turn per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** 10ft radius sphere (or hemisphere)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates an immobile field of force that hedges out all living animal matter.

## Details

[[Undead\|Undead]] creatures are not living, so they are not blocked.

Creatures not of the material plane (djinn, elementals, demons, etc.) are not blocked.

"Animal" has its broadest meaning here, so humans are blocked, creatures with intelligence over 6 are blocked, and so on.
