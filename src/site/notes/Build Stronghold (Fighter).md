## Notes

At 9th level the [[Fighter\|Fighter]] gains nobility, lands, and a stronghold. This is the fighter's name-level milestone — the start of a personal fiefdom and a corps of loyal retainers. Just rule (or for evil fighters, exploitative rule) attracts followers from the surrounding land.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]]
- **Level Required:** 9th (Stronghold and Title)
- **Land:** Acquired by feudal grant, purchase, or conquest
- **Construction:** Build (or cause to be built) a suitable complex of buildings to begin the fiefdom
- **Governance:** Either offer fair taxes for freeholders, decent wages for staff, and just judgment on disputes — OR for evil fighters, offer chances to oppress and extort. Either way, broadly aligned followers will arrive.
- **Followers (see [[Followers (Fighter)\|Followers (Fighter)]]):**
    - 1 mercenary captain — a NPC fighter of level 1d4+3
    - 4d20 NPC 0-level mercenaries
    - All broadly match the PC's alignment
