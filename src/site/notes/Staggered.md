## Notes

The character (or monster) is **off balance or knocked to their knees** by some force. This is usually a result of [[Unarmed Combat\|Unarmed Combat]] (an [[Overbearing\|Overbearing]] attack), but can also be the result of magic, an earthquake, or similar forces.

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** melee and missile attacks against the staggered character are made at **+2 to hit**
- **Duration:** until the character recovers — usually next turn unless [[Overborne\|overborne]]
- **Recovery:** rise as an action (unless overborne — see [[Overborne\|Overborne]] for the locked-down case)
- **Stacks with:** [[Overborne\|Overborne]] (typical), [[Prone\|Prone]], [[Grappling\|Grappling]]
- **Common sources:** [[Overbearing\|Overbearing]] attacks (Table 1.6.12b), earthquake, certain spells
