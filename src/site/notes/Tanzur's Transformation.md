## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell transforms the caster into a berserk warrior, much more capable at melee combat than normal. However, the caster loses control of their decisions and fights madly until the spell ends.

## Details

Upon the completion of this spell, the caster's form and mind alter as they take on the spirit and attributes of a powerful berserker warrior.

The caster's current [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] are doubled, and any damage sustained is first deducted from the "additional" hit points with no deduction from the "true" hit points until the additional hit points are gone. Once the additional hit points are gone, however, the caster sustains twice normal damage from attacks.

The caster's armour class improves by four points, and they attack as a fighter of the same level they have as a magic-user. This includes multiple attacks, if applicable.

The caster may only use a dagger as a weapon while in this berserk state, but they may attack twice per round, inflicting a +2 bonus on all damage.

The caster cannot cancel the effects of this spell, and will continue to attack until all enemies are killed or the spell duration ends. In order to cast this spell, the caster must consume a [[Potion of Heroism\|Potion of Heroism]].
