## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** 1 mile radius per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2d12 hours
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell summons a familiar, who will remain bonded to the caster, providing benefits that depend on what sort of creature responds to the call. There is a downside to establishing such a relationship: if the familiar is ever killed, the caster permanently loses twice the familiar's [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]].

## Details

To summon a familiar, the caster must intone the words of the spell over a stocked fire source, sprinkling the flames with expensive incense and powders (100 gp in total value). The caster must continue casting for as long as necessary (2d12 hours) until a familiar arrives or the casting time expires without success. The spell may be attempted only once per year, and the caster has no control over the type of animal that will respond. When it arrives, the familiar becomes the caster's faithful servant and ally. Normal familiars have 1d3+1 hit points, are AC 7 [13], and are as intelligent as a lower-than-average human. When the familiar is within 120ft of the caster, the caster gains additional hit points equal to the familiar's. If the familiar is ever killed, the caster permanently loses twice the familiar's hit points.

A special familiar is entitled to a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] in order to avoid the effect of the spell. The type of special familiar to appear depends upon the magic-user's alignment:

- Lawful good or Lawful neutral: Brownie
- Chaotic good, Neutral good, or Neutral: Pseudo-dragon
- Lawful evil or Neutral evil: Imp
- Chaotic evil or Chaotic Neutral: Quasit
