## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 8th
- **School:** [[Arcane Abjuration\|Arcane Abjuration]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 24 hours
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The person upon whom this spell is cast becomes completely protected from all divination and scrying magic, and they are also protected against possession. Their thoughts cannot be detected in any way, they cannot be magically overheard when speaking, they are invisible to crystal balls and clairvoyance, and their soul cannot be trapped or affected.
