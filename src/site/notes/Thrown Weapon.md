## Concept

Thrown (Hurled) weapons are [[Missile Weapon\|Missile Weapon]]s propelled by the user's arm rather than a mechanism.

**Strength bonus DOES apply to damage** with thrown weapons (unlike [[Pulled Bow\|Pulled Bow]]s and [[Crossbow\|Crossbow]]s).

## Range Penalties

You take a **-2 penalty** to hit per range increment after the first. Thrown weapons can't be hurled more than five increments.

## Grenade-Like Missiles

- [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]] (vial): 2d4 damage to undead on direct hit
- [[Flask of Oil\|Flask of Oil]]: 2d6 fire damage on direct hit when set alight

These follow special rules — see [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]] and [[Flask of Oil\|Flask of Oil]].
