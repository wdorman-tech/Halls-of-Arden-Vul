## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 9th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 9 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

Upon casting this spell, the caster is able to change shape almost at will (each change takes one segment, and incurs no system shock check). They retain their own mind and [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]], but assume the magical properties of the shape-changed form as well as its physical capabilities.

## Details

The spell does not allow the caster to assume the form of any greater creature native to another plane of existence, but virtually any other form can be assumed, from a tree to an insect to a treasure chest to a dragon.

Example: The caster can change into a dragon in one segment, being able to fly and use a breath weapon. Then they can change into a tree or a gelatinous cube, with the second change also taking a segment to complete.

The caster does not automatically heal any hit points when they return to their normal form.

The material component of the spell is a piece of jewellery worth 5,000 gp.
