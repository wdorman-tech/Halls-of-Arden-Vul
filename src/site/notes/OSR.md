## Notes

The Old-School Renaissance (OSR) is a movement and design tradition centered on the original editions of Dungeons & Dragons (OD&D, B/X, BECMI, AD&D 1E). OSR games emphasize lethal low levels, player skill over character abilities, exploration as the core gameplay loop, sandbox structure, and rulings over rules. [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] is one of the most prominent OSR retro-clones.

## Key Facts

- **Origin:** Mid-2000s, enabled by the OGL and SRD
- **Design pillars:** player skill, lethality, exploration, sandbox play, GM rulings
- **Notable systems:** [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]], Old-School Essentials, Basic Fantasy, Swords & Wizardry
- **Contrast with modern D&D:** less character-mechanical complexity, more environmental and social mechanics
