## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 9th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** 1 segment per 2 MU levels + 1d8 segments
- **Area of Effect:** 15ft radius sphere
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 9 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to act between two moments of time, in a bubble where the outside is frozen in place, not moving in the flow of time's passage.

## Details

The spell's common name is a misnomer, for time does not actually stop; rather the caster gains a few "extra" moments inside a bubble that is insulated from time moving forward.

Effectively, the caster simply gains the duration of the spell as extra time in which they may perform whatever actions they wish, while the rest of the universe is effectively frozen relative to them.
