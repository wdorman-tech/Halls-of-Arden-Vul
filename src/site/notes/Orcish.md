## Notes

Orcish is the harsh, guttural language of [[Orc\|Orc]] tribes. It shares some loanwords with [[Goblin\|Goblin]] but is its own distinct tongue.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Orc\|Orc]] (native), [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]]
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Negotiation with orc tribes, reading orc raid-marks and trophies
