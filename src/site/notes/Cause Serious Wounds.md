## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]] / [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S (Cleric) / V, S, M (Druid)
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments (Cleric) / 6 segments (Druid)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The touched target creature takes 2d8+1 hit points of [[Magical Damage\|Magical Damage]], even if it can only be hit by magical weapons. Touching the creature requires a successful to-hit roll, but no saving throw is allowed.

## Details

If you miss the to-hit roll on the target, no damage is inflicted and the spell is lost.

This spell only affects creatures that are naturally living, so it does not affect [[Golems\|Golems]], [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], or the [[Undead\|Undead]].

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Cure Serious Wounds\|Cure Serious Wounds]].
