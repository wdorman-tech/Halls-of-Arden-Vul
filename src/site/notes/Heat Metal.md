## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** 7 rounds
- **Area of Effect:** All metal worn by one size Medium creature per druid level, or 50 lbs weight/druid level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This extremely powerful spell causes metal to heat up to searing temperatures. It affects all metal worn by one size Medium creature per druid level, or 50 lbs weight per druid level if the metal is not worn by creatures. Magic armour, if metal, is entitled to a saving throw against magical fire, which negates the effect on the armour if successful. Elfin chain mail is entirely unaffected by the spell.

## Details

Damage from contact with the metal changes over time as the metal heats and then cools.

| Round | Effect |
|---|---|
| 1 | No effect other than discomfort |
| 2 | 1d4 hp fire damage |
| 3-5 | 2d4 hp fire damage, severe blistering to extremities (hands, feet), and unconsciousness if the metal is a helmet |
| 6 | 1d4 hp fire damage |
| 7 | No effect other than discomfort |

Immersion in water will negate the effects of the spell.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Chill Metal\|Chill Metal]].
