## Notes

[[Immunity to Disease\|Immunity to Disease]] is granted to both [[Paladin\|Paladins]] (from level 1, "Boon of Health") and [[Monk\|Monks]] (from level 5 with CON 11+, or from level 6 if CON is below 11). The character cannot contract diseases.

## Key Facts

### Paladin
- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate, called Boon of Health)
- **Mechanics:** Automatic — never contracts disease

### Monk
- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 5th (CON 11+) or 6th (CON <11, catch-up ability)
- **Mechanics:** Automatic — never contracts disease
