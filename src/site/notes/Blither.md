## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Noise:** Casting quiet, effect quiet
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

This spell renders a creature's words meaningless. It can't communicate via spoken word for the duration of the spell.

## Details

The creature you touch can apply its Wisdom [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] modifier to the saving throw. If it fails, although the creature's spoken words become meaningless, it can still gesture, write, and otherwise communicate.

Note: The affected creature can't cast spells that have a verbal component until the spell ends.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Tongues\|Tongues]].
