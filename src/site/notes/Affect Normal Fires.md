## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 5ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 1.5ft radius fire
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to command small fires (up to a limit of 1.5ft in radius), to grow smaller or larger. Any fire within the spell's size limitations may be made as small as a candle flame or turned into a bonfire up to 5ft in diameter.

## Details

An affected fire only consumes fuel appropriate to its new size, but will continue to radiate the same heat as a fire of its original size. Example: Turning a candle flame into a bonfire creates more light, roughly the equivalent of a [[Light\|Light]] spell, but the bonfire inflicts no measurable damage since its heat is still only that of a candle's flame.
