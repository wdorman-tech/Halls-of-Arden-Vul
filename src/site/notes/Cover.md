## Notes

**Cover** is protection behind something that physically blocks incoming attacks, such as a wall or arrow slit. (Visual obscurement that doesn't block attacks is [[Concealment\|Concealment]], not cover.)

If you have **25% or more cover**, you also receive a **+2 bonus on any [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] that involves [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]]** (such as agility-based saves) — including the agility saving throw bonus.

## Key Facts

| Cover | Penalty to enemy attacks | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| 25% | -2 | behind a flimsy fence |
| 50% | -4 | fighting from behind a tree |
| 75% | -7 | behind a low wall |
| 90% | -10 | shooting from an arrow slit |

- **Bonus to saves:** +2 to dexterity-based saving throws at 25%+ cover
- **Distinct from concealment:** cover physically blocks attacks; concealment just obscures vision
