## Notes

Not all classes can cast spells. Refer to a [[Class\|class]] description (Chapter 3) to see how many spell slots a caster has and which spells they can memorise.

## Key Facts

**Memorisation rules:**
- Can only cast spells that are **memorised**
- Memorising a spell fills one spell slot of the spell's level
- Cannot memorise unless an empty slot is available
- Can choose to forget a spell without casting (empties the slot)
- Spell slot level must match spell level (cannot use a 1st-level slot for a 2nd-level spell, or vice versa)
- After casting, the spell is forgotten — recast requires a separate memorisation

**Memorisation time:**
- 4 hours of uninterrupted rest in a quiet place is required first
- After that: **15 minutes per spell level** (e.g. one 3rd + two 2nd + four 1st = 45+30+30+15+15+15+15 = 165 minutes)

**General rules:**
- Default casting line-of-sight: spell energy must travel in a straight line from origin to target (unless spell says otherwise)
- A creature can only be affected by **one instance** of a spell at a time (unless duration is Instantaneous)
- Spells with a duration **do not stack**; second casting doesn't increase effect, but may extend duration
- Saving throws apply per casting (a save against the second casting doesn't end the first)

**Reading a spell:**
- First word: arcane (mage), divine (cleric), druidic (druid), or phantasmal (illusionist)
- Second word: school of magic ([[Abjuration\|Abjuration]], [[Conjuration\|Conjuration]], [[Divination\|Divination]], [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]], [[Evocation\|Evocation]], [[Illusion\|Illusion]], [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]], [[Alteration\|Transmutation]])
- Then: level

**Range:**
- "Caster": originates from caster, moves with caster
- "Nil": originates from caster but doesn't move with caster
- "Touch": affects a creature you touch (roll to hit if unwilling)
- Specific feet: maximum to centre of area of effect

**Duration:**
- "Permanent" — lasts indefinitely; can be dispelled
- "Instantaneous" — happens once, can't be dispelled afterwards
- "Continuing" — lasts as long as caster keeps casting
- "Concentration" — caster must concentrate; can't cast another spell, fight with another weapon, use another power; broken by death/unconsciousness/stun/charm/etc.
- Time scale: 10 segments = 1 round (1 min); 10 rounds = 1 turn (10 min); 6 turns = 1 hour

**Casting time:**
- Spell takes effect in segment when caster begins + casting time - 1
- "Fast" = 1 segment (instant)
- "1 round" = takes effect in next round, same segment number
- "1 turn" = 9 rounds later, same segment
- Spells that exceed segment 10 spill into next round
- "Continuing casting time" = takes effect after listed time but must be sustained; walking allowed at half speed

**[[Spell Components\|Spell Components]]:** V (verbal), S (somatic), M (material) — see linked file.

**[[Spell Interruption\|Spell Interruption]]:** see linked file for what breaks a spell.

**Reverse:**
- Some spells are reversible — memorised in one form or the other
- Cannot switch mid-adventure; must use a fresh memorisation
- Two slots can hold both forms
