## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 7 [Ascending: 13]
- **Movement Cap:** 90ft
- **Weight:** 35 lb
- **Cost:** 30 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 9 in 3x3

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot wear metal armour
- [[Thief\|Thief]] cannot wear (only leather, studded leather, and elfin mail)
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear armour
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear armour

## Notes

Heavy leather with metal rings sewn over it. All purchased armour comes with a [[Helmet\|Helmet]] included in the price.
