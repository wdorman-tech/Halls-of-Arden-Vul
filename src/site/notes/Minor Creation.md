## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 6 turns per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** 1 cubic ft per illusionist level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

With a small piece of material, the caster may use this spell to create an object made of that same material. The base material (a) cannot be alive and (b) must come from a plant.

## Details

Within the level-based limit on the item's volume, the caster could create a basket from a piece of straw, a door or club from a splinter of wood, a cloak from a piece of cotton, etc. The item exists only for the duration of the spell, at which time it turns back to the small piece of basic material.
