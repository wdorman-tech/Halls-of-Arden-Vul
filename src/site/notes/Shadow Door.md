## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** 1 round per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** Magic door
- **Components:** S
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

With a mystical gesture, the caster creates an illusory door (either in a wall or free-standing). If the caster steps toward it, they appear to walk through the door and disappear from sight. If anyone else opens the door, they will perceive a small empty room. What actually happens is that the caster becomes invisible (with an unusually powerful type of invisibility) and never actually walks into the illusory room.

## Details

The caster's invisibility after passing through the shadow door is particularly powerful and cannot be seen by use of [[Detect Invisibility\|Detect Invisibility]], although the caster can be seen with more powerful divination magic. A powerful monster can still detect the caster's presence and approximate location if their hit dice allow them to detect invisibility, but they cannot see the caster well enough to attack without a -2 penalty (as opposed to the normal -4). More powerful divinations (such as a [[True Seeing\|True Seeing]] spell) will reveal the caster normally.
