## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 5 [Ascending: 15]
- **Movement Cap:** 90ft
- **Weight:** 30 lb
- **Cost:** 75 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 9 in 3x3

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot wear metal armour
- [[Thief\|Thief]] cannot wear
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear

## Notes

Interlocking metal rings — the standard armour for many fighters in OSRIC, balancing AC and movement (90ft cap). All purchased armour comes with a [[Helmet\|Helmet]] included.
