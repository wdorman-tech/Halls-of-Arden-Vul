## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th (Cleric) / 6th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Transmutation\|Divine Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 120ft (Cleric) / 80ft (Magic-User)
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level / 5 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 10ft square per cleric level (Cleric) / 5ft x 5ft square area per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 10 rounds (Cleric) / 1 turn (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

Water level in the affected area sinks by 5ft. If the caster is high enough level to affect the same area twice or more, then the caster can choose to lower it by another 5ft for each iteration.

Example: A dungeon contains a square pool, 30ft on a side and 50ft deep. The caster casts Lower Water. The caster can affect a 30ft square area once for every 3 levels they have in their class, so if 7th or 8th level then the caster can lower it by 10ft; if 9th, 10th, or 11th level then the caster can lower it by 15ft; and so on.

## Details

Displaced water is held in a magical interdimensional space for the duration of the spell, and returns when the spell ends. It will be slightly chilled.

The target body of liquid must be a non-magical fluid of reasonably similar viscosity to water, so a pool of blood might well be affected but flowing lava wouldn't.

If the target is a creature (e.g. a [[Water Weird\|Water Weird]] or [[Water Elemental\|Water Elemental]]), it is considered [[Held\|Held]] unless this spell is mentioned in the monster description. See [[Held\|Paralysis (Held)]] for details.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Raise Water\|Raise Water]].
