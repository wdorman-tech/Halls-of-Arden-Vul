## Notes

If two combatants are within **10 ft** of each other, they are in melee range — **whether or not they are actually targeting each other**, and whether or not it's possible for either to attack the other. Even someone using a sword or dagger can attack across an empty 5 ft square (the "range" of a melee blow is a full 10 ft because of chaotic combat movement).

You **cannot attack past an ally** unless you have a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]]. You **cannot attack past an enemy** to a different enemy at all — even with a reach weapon. **Moving outside 10 ft** of an enemy who is otherwise able to attack you triggers a potential attack (a [[Fleeing from Combat\|fleeing attack]]).

**Moving into melee range** of an enemy ends your movement (you've closed into combat).

Spells may be cast while engaged in melee combat, but may be **disrupted** (see [[Spell Interruption\|Spell Interruption]]).

Missile weapons fired at a target engaged in melee combat **can't be aimed** — they hit randomly among melee participants, including allies.

## Key Facts

- **Range:** 10 ft (squares adjacent + their adjacent)
- **No attack past ally:** unless [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]]
- **No attack past enemy:** ever (front rank blocks)
- **Moving away triggers:** +4 fleeing attack — see [[Fleeing from Combat\|Fleeing from Combat]]
- **Moving in ends:** your move — you've closed into combat
- **Spells:** castable but interruptible
- **Missile fire:** random target among melee participants
- **Tiny creatures:** exempt from spacing rules; can move freely in/out
