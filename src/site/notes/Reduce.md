## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 5ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature or object
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell causes a creature or object to shrink in size and weight. The spell decreases a creature's size and weight by 20% for each level of the caster (to as low as 20% of original size) or decreases an object's size and weight by 10% per caster level (to as low as 50% of original weight).

## Details

The caster can affect no more than 10 cubic ft of living matter, or 5 cubic ft of non-living matter, per level. A creature's strength will be affected by the reduction in size.

If the spell is cast on an unwilling recipient, a successful [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] will negate the spell, but willing recipients may choose to be affected without making a saving throw.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Enlarge\|Enlarge]].
