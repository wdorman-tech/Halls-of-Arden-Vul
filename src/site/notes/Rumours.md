## Notes

Rumours are pieces of information your character knows that may or may not be entirely true. There are two types: [[Adventuring Rumour\|Adventuring Rumour]] (actionable hooks for adventures) and [[Historical Rumour\|Historical Rumour]] (lore about the dungeon's history). Each rumour contains **at least some truth** but is not necessarily accurate in every particular.

## Sources

- Starting characters: 1d3 adventuring + 1d2 historical at creation (see [[Modified Character Creation\|Modified Character Creation]]).
- Backup characters and post-start characters: none at creation.
- During play: rolling 8 or 9 on [[Carousing\|Carousing]] (8 = adventuring, 9 = historical).
- The GM may award them at any time.
