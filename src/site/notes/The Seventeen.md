## Notes

The Seventeen are minor gods to the Archonteans but are followed faithfully by other cultures — especially the [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] and [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]]. They include nature deities, regional patrons, deified abstractions, and gods that the imperial cult subordinated as it expanded. A [[Cleric\|Cleric]] is just as free to pick from the Seventeen as from [[The Ten\|The Ten]].
