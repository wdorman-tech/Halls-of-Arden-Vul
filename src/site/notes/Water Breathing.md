## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Druidic Transmutation\|Druidic Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 6 turns per druid level (Druid) / 3 turns per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments (Druid) / 3 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell grants the ability to breathe underwater while the spell lasts. The recipient does not lose the ability to breathe air.

## Reversed Form

Reversible as [[Air Breathing\|Air Breathing]].
