## Notes

**Combat modifiers** adjust attack rolls, damage rolls, and AC based on circumstances. The most common modifiers come from positioning, conditions, and special situations.

## Key Facts

**Concealment / Cover (defender):** see [[Concealment\|Concealment]] and [[Cover\|Cover]] for tables.

**Rear Attack:** an attack from directly behind an opponent **negates the shield bonus**, **negates [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] bonus to AC**, and is made at **+2 to hit**.

**Two-Weapon Fighting:** off-hand weapon must be a **dagger** or **hand axe**. Primary hand attacks at **-2**, off-hand at **-4**. The Dexterity *missile* bonus (or penalty) applies to both attacks. Penalties can be reduced but never become bonuses. Off-hand cannot affect parrying.

**Unhelmed Opponent:** 1 in 6 chance any attack is delivered to the head (base AC 10 [10]) instead of the body. Not a called shot; just the attacker's opportunity that round.

**Conditions (quick reference):**
- [[Invisible\|Invisible]]: -4 to hit if location known; otherwise can't be attacked. +4 saves.
- [[Paralyzed\|Paralyzed]] (held): automatic hit, max damage minimum; instant kill if no allied threat in melee range.
- [[Prone\|Prone]]: attacked at +4; no shield, no DEX bonus.
- **Sleeping:** depends on magical or normal sleep — see [[Magically Sleeping\|Magically Sleeping]]
- [[Staggered\|Staggered]]: attacked at +2.
- [[Stunned\|Stunned]]: attacked at +4; no shield, no DEX bonus.
- [[Charge\|Charge]]: charger gets +2 to hit; defender with longer weapon attacks first.
