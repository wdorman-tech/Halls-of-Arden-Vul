## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 8 [Ascending: 12]
- **Movement Cap:** 90ft
- **Weight:** 10 lb
- **Cost:** 4 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 4 in 2x2

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- [[Druid\|Druid]]
- [[Thief\|Thief]]
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear armour
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear armour

## Notes

Quilted, padded fabric — a gambeson. Cheap, easily made, and surprisingly effective at stopping cuts. Same AC as [[Leather Armour\|Leather Armour]] but cheaper, although it caps movement at 90ft (whereas leather doesn't reduce movement).
