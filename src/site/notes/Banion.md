## Notes

Banion is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic neutral god of decision-making, trickery, and deceit — the twin of [[Yanion\|Yanion]]. Where his sister governs sober medicine and lawful thresholds, Banion governs the wild moment of choice that can swing either way. His symbol is an idealized face, half white and half black, with red eyes; his weapon is the dagger.
