## Notes

The Order of the Fifth Circle is primarily summoners and those interested in researching the outer planes. Its members traffic in fiends, elementals, and stranger things — bound, bargained, or simply observed. It walks a moral edge: outer-planes research is not inherently evil, but it shares territory with darker arts.

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Summoning, outer-planes research
- **Reputation:** Specialist; legally permitted, but watched
- **Membership profile:** Summoners, planar scholars, conjurers
- **Schools of magic favored:** Conjuration, summoning, planar magic
