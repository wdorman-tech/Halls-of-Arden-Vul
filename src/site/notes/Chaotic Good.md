## Notes

Because you are Chaotic, you tend to work against authority unless you trust the individuals in charge. You do not believe in abstract systems of justice or government — you prefer personal connections. You do not force others to behave a certain way; you respect individualism. Because you are Good, you have a strong tendency to help those in need and protect the weak.

Chaotic Good is the alignment of free-spirited heroes, traveling artists with a moral compass, and rebels who fight for the downtrodden.

## Key Facts

- **Axis:** Chaotic on [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]; Good on [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** Chaotic Good tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Ten\|The Ten]]):** [[Crestion\|Crestion]], [[Thelis\|Thelis]]
- **Behavior:** helps the weak, opposes authority, prefers personal loyalty
