## Notes

Certain monsters, magic items, and spells can drain "**life energy levels**," perform a "**level drain**," or an "**energy drain**" (the phrases are interchangeable in OSRIC).

When a player character is drained of a life energy level, they lose **one complete level of experience** and are placed at the **beginning point of the new (lower) level**. NPCs and monsters lose a level or [[Hit Die\|Hit Die]], as applicable.

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** lose 1 full character level; XP set to start of new level
- **Multi-class / dual-class:** the highest level attained is the one lost
- **Two equal levels:** roll randomly for which is lost
- **Below level 1:** the character is **slain** and may rise as some kind of [[Undead\|Undead]] creature
- **Common sources:** [[Wraith\|Wraith]], [[Wight\|Wight]], [[Spectre\|Spectre]], [[Vampire\|Vampire]], certain spells
