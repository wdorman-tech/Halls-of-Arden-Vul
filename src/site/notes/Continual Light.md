## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Cleric) / 3rd (Illusionist) / 2nd (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Transmutation\|Divine Transmutation]] / [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 120ft (Cleric) / 60ft (Illusionist/Magic-User)
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** Globe of 60ft radius
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments (Cleric) / 3 segments (Illusionist) / 2 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

A divine, phantasmal, or arcane light is created, illuminating the target (a creature, object, or point in space) and everything in a 60ft radius around the target.

## Details

If the target is an area of magical [[Darkness\|Darkness]], both spells (darkness and light) are cancelled.

The light is stationary, unless cast on a creature or object that moves, in which case it moves with that creature or object.

If the target is a creature's eyes or other sense organs, then it can roll a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs spells. The target creature applies its Dexterity save modifier, if it has one, to this saving throw. If it passes the saving throw, then the spell produces a stationary light in the air just behind the target creature, rather than affecting the creature itself. If it fails the saving throw it is effectively [[Blinded\|Blinded]] until the spell ends or is dispelled.

The caster can't have more than three instances of this spell running at the same time. If they cast it again, the earliest-cast Continual Light spell that's still in effect ends.

At the end of every complete month the spell has been running, there is a 1 in 12 chance that the spell ends.

The arcane version of this spell cannot be reversed to create continual darkness.

## Reversed Form

The cleric and illusionist versions are reversed as [[Continual Darkness\|Continual Darkness]].
