## Notes

[[Identify Plants and Animals\|Identify Plants and Animals]] (Druid's Knowledge) is a 3rd-level [[Druid\|Druid]] ability granting reliable identification of natural species, tracks, water purity, and edibility of wild foods. It is automatic — no roll required — and represents the druid's deep training in the natural world.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level Required:** 3rd
- **Mechanics:** Automatic, no roll
- **Effects:**
    - Sight of any plant, animal, or bird → know its species and breed
    - Sight of any tracks or spoor → know what kind of animal made them
    - Found water in the wilderness → know whether it's pure and safe to drink
    - Found fruit, nuts, roots, or berries → know whether they're safe to eat
