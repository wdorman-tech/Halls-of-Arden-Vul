## Notes

[[Pick Pockets\|Pick Pockets]] is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to lift small items from a target's person without being noticed. Targets above level 3 are harder to steal from, and a botched attempt may alert the mark.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] same table)
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll; takes 1 combat round
- **Conditions:** Hand free; adjacent to target; target unaware of you
- **Penalty for High-Level Targets:** -5% per level/HD above level 3 (so -5% at level 4, -10% at level 5, -15% at 6 HD, etc.)
- **Failure:** If you fail by 20% or more, the target notices you
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
