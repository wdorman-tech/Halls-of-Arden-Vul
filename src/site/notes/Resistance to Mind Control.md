## Notes

[[Resistance to Mind Control\|Resistance to Mind Control]] is a [[Monk\|Monk]] ability gained at 9th level. It grants a percentage chance to resist any spell or magic that controls the mind — charm person, hypnosis, geas, quest, suggestion, and similar. It does NOT resist illusions. If the resistance fails, a normal saving throw may still apply.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 9th
- **WIS Requirement:** Higher base chance with Wisdom 15+
- **Affected Spells:** Charm person, hypnosis, geas, quest, suggestion (and similar mind-control magic)
- **Not Affected:** Illusions
