## Notes

A character's **ancestry** (originally called "race") means their species, not their ethnicity. OSRIC games feature a wide variety of non-human ancestries, some of whom (such as elves or dwarfs) can be player characters, but most of whom (such as goblins) can only be NPCs. The word "ancestry" is used instead of "species" because "species" sounds too modern.
