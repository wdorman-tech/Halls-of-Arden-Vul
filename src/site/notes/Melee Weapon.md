## Concept

Melee weapons are used in close combat — within reach of the wielder. The OSRIC melee weapons table lists their hands required, damage versus M-and-smaller and L+ opponents, [[Weapon Speed Factor\|Weapon Speed Factor]], space required, length, and damage type.

## Damage by Size

Many weapons inflict different damage on smaller versus larger opponents:
- **Damage vs M and smaller** — used against Tiny, Small, or Medium opponents.
- **Damage vs L+** — used against Large, Huge, or Gigantic opponents.

## Space Required

Each character takes up about 3ft side-to-side. Weapons requiring more than 3ft of side-to-side space restrict the number of characters who can fight side-by-side in a corridor or other limited space.

## Damage Types

- **Slashing** — cutting blades and axes
- **Piercing** — spear-tips, picks, daggers, and arrows
- **Blunt** — clubs, maces, hammers, flails

Some weapons offer a choice of damage type per attack (e.g., [[Halberd\|Halberd]], [[Pick, Heavy\|Pick, Heavy]]).
