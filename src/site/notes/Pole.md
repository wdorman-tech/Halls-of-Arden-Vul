## Stats

- **Cost:** 2 sp per 10 ft
- **Weight:** 8 lb per 10 ft
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

The legendary **ten-foot pole**. Used to probe for traps, test floor stability, prod suspicious objects, vault chasms, and generally avoid any direct contact with whatever's down there. The first essential tool of any cautious dungeoneer.
