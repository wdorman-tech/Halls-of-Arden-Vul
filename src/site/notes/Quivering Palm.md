## Notes

[[Quivering Palm\|Quivering Palm]] (Harmonic Touch) is the iconic 13th-level [[Monk\|Monk]] ability — a delayed death strike. The monk lands an unarmed touch attack establishing a connection, then at any later moment within a generous window may sever it, killing the target outright.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 13th
- **Frequency:** Once per week
- **WIS Requirement:** Wisdom 15+ (until WIS reaches 15, the monk does not have this ability at all)
- **Connection:** Must hit the victim with an unarmed strike within 2 rounds of declaring intent. If the connection fails to land in 2 rounds, the attempt is wasted for the week.
- **Activation Window:** Once connected, the monk may trigger the kill any time within 1 day per level
- **Effect:** Target dies
- **Immune:**
    - Non-living creatures (undead, golems)
    - Creatures with more hit dice than the monk
    - Creatures with more than twice the monk's hit points
    - Creatures only hit by magical weapons
