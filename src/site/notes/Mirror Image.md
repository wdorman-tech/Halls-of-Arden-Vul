## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]] / [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 3 rounds per illusionist level / 2 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 6ft radius
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The mirror image spell creates 1d4+1 (Illusionist) or 1d4 (Magic-User) phantasmal images of the caster, all mirroring the caster's actions. The spell's 6ft radius also becomes slightly blurred to sight, like the reflection of a slightly distorted mirror. The combination of these two magical phenomena makes it impossible to distinguish the images from the real person without aid of a magical nature, such as [[True Seeing\|True Seeing]].

If an opponent attempts to attack the caster while obscured by this spell, it is randomly determined whether the "to hit" roll is directed toward the caster or toward one of the images.

## Details

When an opponent makes a successful hit against one of the images, the image breaks up and disappears (the others remain).

At the end of the spell's duration, the images fade from sight.
