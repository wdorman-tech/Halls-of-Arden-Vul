## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Conjuration\|Conjuration/Summoning]]
- **Range:** 80ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One elemental creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 rounds
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell returns one elemental creature to the elemental plane of fire.

## Details

This spell can affect any creature native to the elemental plane of fire, including (but not limited to) fire elementals, efreet, and salamanders.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Conjure Fire Elemental\|Conjure Fire Elemental]].
