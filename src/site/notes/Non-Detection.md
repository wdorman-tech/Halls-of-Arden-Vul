## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Abjuration\|Phantasmal Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 turn per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** 5ft radius
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell makes the caster invisible, inaudible, and undetectable to all divination spells and other means of magical spying, such as crystal balls and thought detection.

## Details

The spell hides the caster from divination magic, but is not the same as actual [[Invisibility\|Invisibility]] — it doesn't prevent normal sight, only divination magic.
