## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 8 [Ascending: 12]
- **Movement Cap:** 120ft (no reduction)
- **Weight:** 15 lb
- **Cost:** 5 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 4 in 2x2

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Druid\|Druid]]
- [[Thief\|Thief]], [[Assassin\|Assassin]] (the only "real" armour available to thieves)
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear armour
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear armour

## Notes

The lightest "real" armour. Usable by thieves while still maintaining most thief abilities. All purchased armour comes with a [[Helmet\|Helmet]] included in the price.
