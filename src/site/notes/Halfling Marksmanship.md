## Notes

Halfling Marksmanship is the [[Halfling\|halfling]] mastery of ranged weapons. When attacking with any [[Pulled Bow\|Pulled Bow]] (longbow, shortbow, composite bow) or any [[Sling\|Sling]], the halfling adds +3 to their [[To-Hit Roll\|To-Hit Roll]]. This is one of the largest unconditional to-hit bonuses any ancestry receives — making halfling slingers and archers disproportionately deadly.

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** +3 to-hit
- **Applies with:** any [[Pulled Bow\|Pulled Bow]] (longbow, shortbow, composite bow) or [[Sling\|Sling]]
- **Does not apply to:** [[Crossbow\|Crossbow]]s, thrown weapons, melee weapons
- **Who has it:** [[Halfling\|Halfling]] only
- **Stacks** with other to-hit bonuses (Dexterity, magic weapons, etc.)
