## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Missile (Launched)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d6 vs S/M, 1d6 vs L (varies by [[Arrow\|arrow]])
- **Damage Type:** Piercing
- **Range Increment:** 50ft
- **Rate of Fire:** 2 per round
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Cost:** 75 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots:** 1

## Notes

A composite-construction short bow — laminated horn, wood, and sinew. More powerful than a basic [[Short Bow\|Short Bow]] for the same range.

Strength bonus does not apply to damage by default. Fires [[Arrow\|Arrow]]s.
