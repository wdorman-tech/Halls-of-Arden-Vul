## Notes

Dwarfish is the language of the [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]] ancestry — a guttural, consonant-heavy tongue with deep tradition in stone-mason's marks, runes, and clan-names. [[Gnome\|Gnome]]s are also commonly able to speak it, given their shared subterranean traditions.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]] (native), [[Gnome\|Gnome]] (commonly)
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left
- **Setting role:** Trade, diplomacy with dwarven enclaves, deciphering inscriptions in old underground halls
