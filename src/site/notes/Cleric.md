## Notes

The [[Cleric\|Cleric]] is a moral and spiritual leader, a person of faith who preaches, interprets omens, and strikes down enemies of their god. Powered primarily by [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]], clerics fight on the front line in armour and grant essential support through divine [[Cleric Spellcasting\|spellcasting]] and [[Turn Undead\|Turn Undead]]. Each cleric must select either one patron deity or a pantheon, and their [[Holy Symbol\|Holy Symbol]] is the focus they touch to cast most spells.

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 6, CON 6, INT 6, WIS 9, CHA 6
- **Prime Requisite:** [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] (10% XP bonus if WIS 16+)
- **Hit Die:** d8 (max 9)
- **Alignment:** Any
- **Armour:** Any
- **Weapons:** Club, flail, hammer, mace, staff, torch (and flaming oil for some faiths)
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 2 (additional at level 4, 7, 10, and 13)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -3 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 3d6 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** All
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Class Abilities:** [[Cleric Spellcasting\|Cleric Spellcasting]], [[Turn Undead\|Turn Undead]]
- **Bonus Spell Slots:** [[Wisdom\|Wisdom]] 13+ grants extra spell slots (cumulative)
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 7 — Divine Craft (potions, scrolls); Lvl 9 — High Priest/Priestess and [[Build Stronghold\|Build Stronghold]] (temple); Lvl 16 — Divine Power (permanent magic items)
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 1,500 XP; Level 9 at 220,000 XP (name level — temple); Level 16 at 1,800,000 XP
- **Stronghold/Followers:** Establish a temple at level 9; attracts 3d6+2 zealots and acolytes who fight as 0-level mercenaries
