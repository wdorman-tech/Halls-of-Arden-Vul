## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee (Reach)
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d6+1 vs S/M, 1d8+1 vs L (varies by specific pole arm; the entry covers a category)
- **Speed Factor:** 13
- **Space Required:** 1ft
- **Length:** 8ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt, Piercing, or Slashing (wielder's choice based on weapon head)
- **Weight:** 8 lb
- **Cost:** 7 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

The OSRIC "Pole Arm" entry is a generic representation of the historical European pole arms. Specific named pole arms (the Bardiche, Bec de Corbin, Bill, Glaive, Glaive-Guisarme, Guisarme, Lochaber Axe, Lucern Hammer, and Voulge) all share similar mechanics. Choose damage type to match the weapon head.

Used as a [[Reach Weapon\|Reach Weapon]] from the second rank. Inflicts **double damage** when [[Set Weapon Against Charge\|set against a charge]].

The very high speed factor (13) reflects the unwieldiness of the long haft.
