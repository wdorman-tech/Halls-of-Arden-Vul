## Notes

**Alteration / Transmutation** spells can change the nature of a creature, object, or place. They might transform one thing into another, or make a thing weaker, stronger, bigger, or smaller. Many of these spells are minor alterations in the fabric of reality rather than a mere change in substance.

## Key Facts

- **Cleric/Druid prefix:** Divine Transmutation
- **Magic-User/Illusionist prefix:** Arcane Transmutation
- **Aliases:** OSRIC sometimes uses "Alteration," sometimes "Transmutation" — they refer to the same school
- **Examples:** [[Bless\|Bless]], [[Polymorph Self\|Polymorph Self]], [[Polymorph Other\|Polymorph Other]], [[Stone to Flesh\|Stone to Flesh]], [[Flesh to Stone\|Flesh to Stone]], [[Enlarge\|Enlarge]], [[Reduce\|Reduce]], [[Haste\|Haste]], [[Slow\|Slow]], [[Animate Object\|Animate Object]], [[Stone Shape\|Stone Shape]]
