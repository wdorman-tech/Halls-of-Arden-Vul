## Armour Stats

- **AC bonus:** -1 [+1]
- **Movement Cap:** none
- **Weight:** 8 lb
- **Cost:** 12 gp
- **Encumbrance Slots (Player Doc):** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A medium shield blocks its bonus against **2 incoming attacks per round**. You can only use a shield if your attacker is in front of you or attacking from your shield side, and not against attackers you aren't aware of.
