## Notes

Kaerion is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the True Neutral genderless **Lord of Time** — god of endlessness, prophecy, and creation. Their cult tends toward the cosmological and the mystical: those who study the ages, prophesize, or seek the first moment of making. Their symbol is the hourglass; their weapon, the staff.
