## Notes

Wiskin is the language of the [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] people of the northern lands — clipped, harsh, full of seafaring and battle terminology. It is the language of raiders, merchants, and the saga-tellers who memorise the deeds of their kin.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] (native)
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left; available as a language swap at character creation
- **Setting role:** Negotiation with Wiskin raiders/traders, reading runic inscriptions, hearing northern sagas
