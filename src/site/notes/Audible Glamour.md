## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st (Illusionist) / 2nd (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]] / [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** 60ft + 10ft per illusionist/MU level
- **Duration:** 3 rounds per illusionist level (Illusionist) / 2 rounds per MU level (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** Hearing range from targeted point
- **Components:** V, S (Illusionist) / V, M (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments (Illusionist) / 2 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell creates sounds from a target focal point in space within range. The caster decides what sort of sound is created, but the loudness is limited by caster level. A 3rd level caster can produce the noise of 4 people shouting (or quieter activity). Each additional caster level adds the sound of four more people. As a benchmark, the sound of an explosion or landslide would require approximately 8th caster level.

## Details

The focal point can be moved at will (within range) for the spell's duration. The spell can create any type of sound, but the maximum volume depends on caster level.

Listeners are only entitled to [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s if they have cause to doubt the apparent sounds. Players must state such disbelief, while the GM determines this for monsters and NPCs based on believability of the sounds.
