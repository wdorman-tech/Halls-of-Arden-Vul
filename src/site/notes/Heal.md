## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The target creature regains almost all lost hit points, up to its normal total minus 1d4. All blindness, deafness, diseases, madnesses, and poisons it is suffering from are completely cured. If your target has been affected by a [[Feeblemind\|Feeblemind]] spell, the feeblemind ends.

## Details

This spell can't bring back the dead, and does not remove [[Lycanthropy\|Lycanthropy]].

In general, the spell only affects normally living creatures. It has no effect on [[Golems\|Golems]], [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], or the [[Undead\|Undead]].

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Harm\|Harm]].
