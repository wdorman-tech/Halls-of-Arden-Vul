## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st (Cleric, Druid, Magic-User) / 2nd (Illusionist)
- **School:** [[Divine Divination\|Divine Divination]] / [[Druidic Divination\|Druidic Divination]] / [[Phantasmal Divination\|Phantasmal Divination]] / [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 turn (cleric) / 12 rounds (druid) / 2 rounds per caster level (Illusionist/Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** Path 10ft wide and 30ft long (cleric) / 40ft long (druid) / 60ft long (Illusionist/Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S, M (Cleric, Druid, Illusionist) / V, S (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** 1 round (cleric) / 3 segments (druid) / 2 segments (Illusionist) / Fast (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster sees an aura around each magical creature, object, or place within the area of effect. If invisible, the caster knows where it is but can't see it; the "to hit" roll penalty for attacking an invisible target is halved.

For [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] casters, the spell additionally reveals which school of magic creates the effect. Other classes (Cleric, Druid, Illusionist) cannot determine school.

## Details

Only the caster can "see" the magical auras. The spell reveals enchantments, not powers, so it would detect a magic item but it wouldn't detect a druid (or other spellcaster) unless they were using a spell.

Solid material can block the spell. The spell can only penetrate metal if it's one inch thick or less, and brick or stone if it's one foot thick or less.

The caster must concentrate on maintaining the spell, or it ends. See [[Concentration\|Concentration]].
