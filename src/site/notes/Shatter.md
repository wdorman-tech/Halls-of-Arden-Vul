## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One object
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (see below)

## Notes

This spell causes the targeted item to shatter into pieces. The item is permitted a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] against a crushing blow to avoid destruction (see Table 1.6.4a: Item Saving Throws).

## Details

The spell can affect objects of up to 10 lbs weight per magic-user level of the caster. Only brittle materials are affected by the spell (glass, earthenware, etc.).

Magic items are immune to the effects of this spell (potion bottles are not magical).
