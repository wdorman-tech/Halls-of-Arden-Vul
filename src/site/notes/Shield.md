## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 5 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates an invisible barrier of magical force that moves with the caster and protects them in various ways.

## Details

- [[Magic Missile\|Magic Missile]]s cannot penetrate the barrier at all.
- The caster gains an armour class of 2 [18] against any hurled weapon.
- The caster gains an armour class of 3 [17] against propelled weapons such as arrows.
- The caster gains an armour class of 4 [16] against all other attacks.
- All [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s against frontal attacks (by wands, dragon breath, etc.) are made at +1 during the spell's duration.

The shield is a frontal defence and grants no benefits against attacks from behind or from the rear flanks.

Regular armour (including magic armour), shields, and certain items (such as bracers of defence) do not "stack" with this spell. The shield's armour class replaces armour if it offers a better armour class, and if it is inferior to the armour the caster is wearing, all it does is provide the protection against magic missiles and the saving throw bonus.

If the caster has a dexterity bonus or is wearing items that give enemies a penalty to hit them, these still function as benefits.
