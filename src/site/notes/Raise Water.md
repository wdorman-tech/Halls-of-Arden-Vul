## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th (Cleric) / 6th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Transmutation\|Divine Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 120ft (Cleric) / 80ft (Magic-User)
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level / 5 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 10ft square per cleric level / 5ft x 5ft square area per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 10 rounds (Cleric) / 1 turn (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

Water level in the affected area rises by 5ft. The cleric version can be cast multiple times to raise it more, while the magic-user version is limited to 5ft (unlike [[Lower Water\|Lower Water]]).

## Details

The additional water comes from the [[Elemental Plane of Water\|Elemental Plane of Water]] for the duration of the spell, and returns when the spell ends. It will be about room temperature.

The target body of liquid must be a non-magical fluid of reasonably similar viscosity to water, so a pool of blood might well be affected, but flowing lava wouldn't.

If the target is a creature (e.g. a water weird or water elemental), it's affected as if by an [[Enlarge\|Enlarge]] spell.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Lower Water\|Lower Water]].
