## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Illusionist) / 2nd (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 2 turns per caster level
- **Area of Effect:** One piece of rope
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments (Illusionist) / 2 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell enchants a rope, making one end into a portal to an extra-dimensional pocket of unreality opened by the spell. The rope rises in the air and then hangs, suspended by its connection to the extra-dimensional hideaway.

## Details

Up to six medium-size people can climb up and hide in the extra-dimensional space (five, if the rope is to be pulled up and into the space as well). At the spell's expiration, the inhabitants or contents of the space will fall into normal reality if they have not already departed. True reality is visible through the portal, but cannot be affected from inside.
