## Notes

Good and Evil are the second cosmic axis of [[Alignment\|Alignment]] (the first being [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]]). They represent opposed moral forces.

**Good** is a mystical power that seeks to build universal compassion and empathy throughout the multiverse. We are all in it together. Help those in need, and respect their lives.

**Evil** seeks to build universal selfishness and tyranny. It is all about you. Use or destroy anyone who gets in your way, whatever gives you the best advantage.

**Neutrality** on this axis means actions are not geared toward helping or hurting others. Neutral characters help when it is convenient or requested, and harm when interest demands.

## Key Facts

- **Good:** compassion, protection of the weak, sacrifice for others
- **Evil:** selfishness, willing to harm others without limit
- **Neutral:** indifferent at the cosmic level; pragmatic in practice
- **The two axes are independent** — a Lawful Good Mitran paladin and a Lawful Evil Debellaton enforcer are both Lawful but irreconcilable
