## Notes

[[Surprise (Ranger)\|Surprise (Ranger)]] (Alert and Stealthy) is the [[Ranger\|Ranger]]'s wilderness instinct, modifying surprise rolls for and against them. The ranger is harder to surprise and better at surprising others.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)
- **When the Ranger Tries to Surprise:** May choose to modify the surprise roll by -1
- **When Others Try to Surprise the Ranger:** May choose to modify their surprise roll by +1
- **Optional:** The ranger chooses whether to apply the modifier — useful when not wanting to disrupt a planned encounter
