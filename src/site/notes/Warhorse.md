## Notes

[[Warhorse\|Warhorse]] (Holy Steed) is the [[Paladin\|Paladin]]'s sacred mount — a divinely blessed heavy warhorse that serves the paladin faithfully until either dies. The Holy Steed has double the hit points of a normal heavy warhorse and a +2 armour class bonus.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 4th
- **Steed Stats:** Heavy warhorse with double the normal hit points and +2 to armour class
- **Loyalty:** Serves faithfully until either the paladin or the steed dies
- **Resurrection:** If the Holy Steed is killed, it can be raised from the dead or resurrected as if it were a player character
- **Frequency:** Once per ten years — after summoning a Holy Steed, the paladin can't summon another for ten years
