## Notes

[[Immunity to Poison\|Immunity to Poison]] is an 11th-level [[Monk\|Monk]] ability. With sufficient [[Constitution\|Constitution]], the monk becomes wholly immune to poison; without it they receive a generous saving throw bonus.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 11th
- **CON 11+:** Fully immune to poison
- **CON <11:** +2 to saving throws against poison (NOT full immunity)
