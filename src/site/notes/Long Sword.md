## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d8 vs S/M, 1d12 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 5
- **Space Required:** 3ft
- **Length:** 3.5ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Weight:** 4 lb
- **Cost:** 15 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

The classic knightly sword. The most popular weapon for most fighter-types because it is one-handed (allowing a [[Shield, Small\|shield]]) and inflicts excellent damage versus large opponents.

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
