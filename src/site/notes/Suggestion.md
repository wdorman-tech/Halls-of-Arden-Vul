## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Enchantment\|Phantasmal Enchantment]] / [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 4 turns + 4 turns per illusionist level / 6 turns + 6 turns per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Negates effect

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to make a factual suggestion or suggest a course of action to the target creature. If the creature fails a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]], it adopts and follows the suggestion.

## Details

The suggestion must be made as soon as the casting time is completed; duration applies to the length of time the creature follows the suggestion.

The caster can make a factual suggestion ("These are not the halflings you seek"), suggest a course of action ("Don't mention you saw us"), or make a combination of both.

Even a victim who has failed a saving throw against the spell will not undertake a course of action that is unreasonable, but the experienced caster can easily phrase requests in such a way as to avoid this problem. Example: No creature will leap off a cliff for no reason; but the caster might "need to scout the bottom of a chasm" and promise to cast a [[Feather Fall\|Feather Fall]] spell, or hand the victim a "magical" torch that supposedly permits the power of flight.

The creature to be influenced must be able to hear and understand the language spoken by the caster.
