## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Necromancy\|Necromancy]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

You utter a dark incantation, speak an Abominable Word, and attempt to touch the target. If the target fails a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] vs. spells, it takes enough [[Magical Damage\|Magical Damage]] to reduce its hit points to a die roll of 1d4.

## Details

In some GMs' campaigns, the Abominable Words are lost secrets, which you can only learn from ancient books, consulting extraplanar powers, or speaking to certain liches.

In general, the spell only affects normally living creatures. It has no effect on [[Golems\|Golems]], [[Constructs\|Constructs]], [[Elementals\|Elementals]], or the [[Undead\|Undead]].

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Heal\|Heal]].
