## Notes

[[Self-Induced Catalepsy\|Self-Induced Catalepsy]] is a [[Monk\|Monk]] ability gained at 6th level allowing the monk to slow their body's internal processes almost to suspended animation. The monk appears dead, and poisons cannot affect them in this state until they emerge from it.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Level Required:** 6th
- **Frequency:** Once per day
- **Duration:** 2 turns per level of experience
- **Effects:** Appears dead; poisons do not affect the monk while in this state
