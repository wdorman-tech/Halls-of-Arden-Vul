## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Magical eye
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 turn
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The wizard eye is a scrying spell allowing the caster to create a material (but invisible) visual organ that transmits whatever it sees into their mind. It floats through the air, moving as the caster directs it to.

## Details

The eye has [[Infravision\|Infravision]] with a range of 100ft and can see at a distance of 600ft in normal lighting.

The eye travels at a rate of 30ft per round if it is not scrutinising its surroundings closely, but can proceed no faster than 10ft per round if it is examining floors, ceilings, and walls.

The caster can detect secret doors through the eye as per their normal chance to do so, but cannot look through the eye with any special vision the magic user has (magical lenses, for example), because the eye's vision is limited to its own sensory capabilities.

The eye cannot pass through solid substances, but it can move through a hole no more than an inch in diameter.
