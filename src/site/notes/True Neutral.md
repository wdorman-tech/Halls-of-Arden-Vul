## Notes

You are not Lawful, Chaotic, Good, or Evil. True Neutral characters are pragmatists who look out for themselves, their families, communities, and friends regardless of law or morality. You probably keep oaths only as a matter of maintaining credibility — oaths are not terribly important to Neutral characters.

Druids are traditionally True Neutral as guardians of the natural balance, though the campaign has lifted that class restriction.

## Key Facts

- **Axis:** Neutral on both [[Law and Chaos\|Law and Chaos]] and [[Good and Evil\|Good and Evil]]
- **Alignment tongue:** True Neutral tongue (see [[Alignment Language\|Alignment Language]])
- **Classes typically associated:** [[Druid\|Druid]] (default; restriction lifted in this campaign)
- **Deities of this alignment ([[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]):** [[Aranyania\|Aranyania]], [[Chibalba\|Chibalba]], [[Kaerion\|Kaerion]], [[Nicetoreon\|Nicetoreon]]
- **Behavior:** pragmatic, balance-oriented, focused on practical results
