## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee / Missile (Hurled)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d4+1 vs S/M, 1d4 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 6
- **Space Required:** 2ft
- **Length:** 2ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt
- **Range Increment:** 10ft (Hurled)
- **Rate of Fire:** 1 per round (when thrown)
- **Weight:** 5 lb
- **Cost:** 1 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

A one-handed hammer usable in melee or thrown. Permitted for [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (blunt). Strength bonus applies to thrown damage.

This is a war hammer, not the [[Hammer\|Hammer]] tool.
