## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

Clairvoyance, together with [[Clairaudience\|Clairaudience]], is one of the classic "scrying" spells -- spells that allow the caster to extend their perception and senses far into the distance to spy upon enemies and scout locations. Clairvoyance is a means of seeing (but not hearing) events in a distant location. There is no effective limit to the spell's range, but the location must either be familiar to the caster (they have been there before) or be obvious, such as the top floor inside a tower they can see, or just beyond a dungeon door.

## Details

The spell cannot penetrate metal; sheets of any type of metal between the caster and the target area will prevent the magic user from being able to scry upon the area.

If the area is dark, the caster can see in a 10ft radius of the spell's focal point as if the area were illuminated. [[Infravision\|Infravision]] does not boost the spell because the caster is not seeing with their physical eyes.

If the area is not dark, the caster can see as if standing at the spell's focal point, for a normal distance but in all directions at once (the vision is in the caster's mind's eye).

The spell cannot be used to scry across planes of existence; it is limited to locations in the same plane in which it is cast.
