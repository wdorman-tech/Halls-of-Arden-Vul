## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** 1 cubic ft per druid level
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None (see below)

## Notes

This spell makes water unsafe to drink.

## Details

Both [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]] and unholy water are turned to normal water by this spell.

Contaminated water is obviously fouled and dangerous to drink. Anyone who does so must make a [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] versus poison, and if it fails, the person suffers from a mild [[Disease\|disease]] for 2d6 days. This disease reduces 3 ability scores (randomly determined by the GM) by 1d4 points each until the disease has run its course.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Purify Water\|Purify Water]].
