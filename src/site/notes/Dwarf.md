## Notes

Dwarfs are short, burly mountain folk known for loyalty, fierce combat, and an aesthetic love of gold, gems, and worked metal. They are quite resistant to [[Poison\|Poison]], [[Spell\|Spell]]s, and spell-like effects, and their underground heritage gives them keen senses for stonework and a hatred of certain humanoids. Dwarfs reach adulthood around 50 and can live 350-450 years.

- **Size:** Small
- **Base Movement Rate:** 90ft (see [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]])
- **Ability Score Adjustments:** +1 [[Constitution\|Constitution]], -1 [[Charisma\|Charisma]]
- **Required Scores (after adjustments):** STR 8-18, DEX 3-17, CON 12-19, INT 3-18, WIS 3-18, CHA 8-18
- **Languages:** [[Common\|Common]], [[Dwarfish\|Dwarfish]], [[Gnomish\|Gnomish]], [[Goblin (Language)\|Goblin]], [[Kobold (Language)\|Kobold]], [[Orcish\|Orcish]], plus [[Alignment Tongue\|Alignment Tongue]]; max two further languages regardless of [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]]
- **Available classes:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Multi-class options:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Level limits:** Assassin 9, Cleric 8, Fighter 9 (STR 18+) / 8 (STR 17) / 7 (STR 16-), Thief unlimited
- **Ancestral traits:** [[Infravision\|Infravision]] (60ft), [[Stalwart\|Stalwart]], [[Stone-Kenning\|Stone-Kenning]], [[Giant Slayer\|Giant Slayer]], [[Grudge Bearer\|Grudge Bearer]]

- **Starting Age:** Cleric 250+2d20; Fighter 40+5d4; Thief or Assassin 75+3d6
- **Random Height/Weight:** 4ft + 3d4 inches; 150 + 5d10 lbs
