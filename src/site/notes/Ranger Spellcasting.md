## Notes

[[Ranger Spellcasting\|Ranger Spellcasting]] is unique among the classes in drawing on TWO traditions — druidic spells starting at level 8, and arcane spells starting at level 9. The ranger casts at much lower caster level than their actual level, capping eventually at 6th-level druid casting at ranger level 18. Rangers do NOT receive bonus spells for high Wisdom.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- **Druidic Spells Start:** Level 8
- **Arcane Spells Start:** Level 9
- **Initial Casting Level (both):** 1st (regardless of ranger level)
- **Casting Level Progression:** +1 per 2 ranger levels above 8th
- **Casting Cap:** 6th-level druid at ranger level 18 (caster level stays at 6 thereafter)
- **Arcane Casting Level:** Always equal to the druid casting level
- **Spell Acquisition (Arcane):** As [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] — must roll vs INT to understand new arcane spells (Table 1.3.6.2A in [[Magic-User Spellcasting\|Magic-User Spellcasting]])
- **Starting Arcane Spell Book:** At level 9, the ranger receives a [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]] from their mentor containing the same spells as a 1st-level magic-user
- **Bonus Spell Slots:** None — rangers do NOT get bonus spells for high Wisdom
