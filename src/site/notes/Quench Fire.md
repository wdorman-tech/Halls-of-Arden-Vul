## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** 1 round
- **Area of Effect:** 60ft radius
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell extinguishes all normal fires within the area of effect. It has no effect on magical fire.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Produce Fire\|Produce Fire]].
