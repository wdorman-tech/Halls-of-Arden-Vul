## Notes

Humans are the standard ancestry: no special abilities, no level caps (except [[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Druid\|Druid]], and [[Monk\|Monk]]), and unique access to [[Dual-Class\|dual-classing]]. They live 90-120 years. In [[Halls of Arden Vul\|Halls of Arden Vul]], every human player character must select a [[Culture\|Culture]] — [[Archontean\|Archontean]], [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]], or [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] — which determines starting languages and cultural background.

## Key Facts

- **Size:** Medium
- **Base Movement Rate:** 120ft (see [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]])
- **Ability Score Adjustments:** None — all abilities 3-18
- **Required Scores:** none beyond class minimums
- **Languages:** [[Common\|Common]] plus [[Alignment Tongue\|Alignment Tongue]]; campaign-specific based on [[Culture\|Culture]] choice ([[Archontean\|Archontean]], [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]], or [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]]); bonus languages by [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]]
- **Culture (Halls of Arden Vul):** must select [[Archontean\|Archontean]], [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]], or [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] — see [[Culture\|Culture]]; each grants its own native tongue in addition to [[Common\|Common]]
- **Available classes:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Druid\|Druid]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]], [[Magic-User\|Magic-user]], [[Monk\|Monk]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]], [[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Multi-class options:** None — humans cannot multi-class, but uniquely can [[Dual-Class\|Dual-Class]]
- **Level limits:** Assassin 15, Druid 14, Monk 17 (these are class caps, not racial); all other classes unlimited
- **Ancestral traits:** None (no [[Infravision\|Infravision]], no special bonuses)
- **Lifespan:** 90-120 years; adulthood at ~20
- **Starting Age:** Cleric/Druid/Monk 20+1d4; Fighter/Paladin/Ranger 15+1d4; Magic-user/Illusionist 24+2d8; Thief/Assassin 20+1d4
- **Random Height/Weight:** 5ft 4in + 3d4 inches; 140 + 6d10 lbs
