## Stats

- **Cost:** 500+ gp
- **Encumbered Weight:** 500 lbs
- **Maximum Weight:** 800 lbs

## Notes

A heavy warhorse, called a destrier — the elite mount of the [[Paladin\|Paladin]] or knightly [[Fighter\|Fighter]]. Trained to fight, kick, and bite. A character riding a charging heavy warhorse with a [[Lance\|Lance]] inflicts **double damage** on the charge attack.
