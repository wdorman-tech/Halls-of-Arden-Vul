## Notes

The Halls of Arden Vul Player Doc is the campaign-specific rulebook supplement. It lists all houserules, the available human cultures, the [[Pantheon\|Pantheon]] of [[The Ten\|The Ten]] and [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the [[Seven Collegia\|Seven Collegia]], the downtime activities (and the [[Carousing\|Carousing]] effects table), and the exploration / Task XP system. Where it conflicts with [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] 3.0, the Player Doc wins.
