## Notes

[[Lay on Hands\|Lay on Hands]] is the [[Paladin\|Paladin]]'s sacred healing touch. The paladin may heal another creature (or themselves) once per day for a fixed amount per paladin level.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)
- **Mechanics:** Touch a creature
- **Healing:** 2 hp per paladin level
- **Self-Targeting:** Allowed
- **Frequency:** Once per day, recharging at dawn
