## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** Instantaneous
- **Area of Effect:** 1 cubic ft per druid level
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell removes all impurities from water, making it clear and drinkable.

## Details

Both [[Holy Water\|Holy Water]] and unholy water are turned to normal water by this spell.

## Reversed Form

The reverse is [[Contaminate Water\|Contaminate Water]].
