## Notes

A toxic herb. **Used to combat lycanthropy:** ingesting belladonna within an hour of being bitten by a [[Werewolf\|werewolf]] (or other lycanthrope) gives a save vs. poison; success prevents the curse. Failure means severe poisoning.
