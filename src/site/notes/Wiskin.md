## Notes

The Wiskin are viking-like people from the northern lands. They are seafarers, raiders, traders, and hardy warriors. Their culture skews martial; they tend toward gods of battle and storm — [[Rudiana\|Rudiana]] (berserker rage), [[Treshub\|Treshub]] (storms and sky), and [[Envalios\|Envalios]] (mercenaries, fire) all find followers among them. The [[Wiskin (language)\|Wiskin (language)]] is harsh and clipped.

- **Class associations:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]] (especially), [[Ranger\|Ranger]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]] of battle gods, occasional [[Thief\|Thief]]
