## Notes

The Order of Thoth was the first and only collegium for a long time, and it remains one of the most prestigious. Its members are generalist [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]s with deep ties to the nobility — the kind of mages a count keeps on retainer or a duke calls his "court wizard." Its age and connections make it the safe, respectable choice.

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Generalist
- **Reputation:** Oldest collegium; respectable; connected to nobility
- **Membership profile:** Court mages, advisors, traditional academics
- **Schools of magic favored:** No specific specialty — broad foundation
