## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 10ft radius + 10ft per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell increases the caster's charisma to all creatures within the area of effect that fail [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]]s.

## Details

For creatures failing the save, the caster's charisma is increased by 2d4 (one roll for all).

For those who succeed in making the saving throw, the caster's charisma is lowered by 1d4.

Unless the exact numbers are required for some purpose, the normal result of the spell will be that those who failed the saving throw will be disposed and friendly toward the caster, and those that succeeded in making the save will find them distasteful and somewhat suspect.
