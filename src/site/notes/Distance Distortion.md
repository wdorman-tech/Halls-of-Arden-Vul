## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 100 square ft per MU level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell is cast with a summoned earth elemental as its focus. When the casting is completed, the earth elemental disperses into the substance of the earth as directed, where it creates a dimensional distortion in the spell's area of effect. Travel through the distorted area takes twice as long (and seems twice as far) as it should, or seems half as long and takes half the normal travel time (determined by the caster when casting the spell).

## Details

The caster must already have an [[Earth Elemental\|Earth Elemental]] summoned before they cast this spell.

Casting the spell causes the elemental to become fascinated, and the caster will not lose control over it while casting, although they cannot give instructions to the elemental while casting.

The area of effect does radiate magic, slightly, and the reality will be perceived by [[True Seeing\|True Seeing]] (although the travel time will still be altered even for those who know that the distance is distorted). Otherwise, the spell is utterly undetectable.
