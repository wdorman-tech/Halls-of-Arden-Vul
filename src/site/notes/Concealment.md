## Notes

**Concealment** is anything that obscures an opponent's vision (such as tree limbs or smoke) but does not physically block incoming attacks. Physical barriers are [[Cover\|Cover]], not concealment. Concealment imposes a penalty on enemy attack rolls but does not stack with [[Invisible\|invisibility]] (which is essentially total concealment).

## Key Facts

- **1/4 Concealment:** -1 to enemy attacks
- **1/2 Concealment:** -2 to enemy attacks
- **3/4 Concealment:** -3 to enemy attacks
- **Total Concealment:** -4 to enemy attacks
- **Does not stack** with invisibility
- **Distinct from cover:** concealment doesn't block physical attacks
