## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell lengthens the duration of another spell that the caster has previously cast.

## Details

The previous spell must still be active. If it is level 1, 2, or 3, the duration is doubled. If it is level 4 or 5, the duration is increased by 50%.

The additional duration on the spell is calculated from the spell's stated duration, not the time remaining.
