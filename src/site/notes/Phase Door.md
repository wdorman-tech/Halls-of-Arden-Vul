## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** See below
- **Area of Effect:** Passage 5ft wide, 10ft high, and 10ft long
- **Components:** V
- **Casting Time:** 7 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By means of this spell the caster opens a dimensional pathway through a solid object, 10ft in depth. It is a pathway that only the caster may use or see, and it remains until they have traversed it twice.

## Details

More than one spell may be used in succession to create longer passages.

This spell is not more powerful than [[Passwall\|Passwall]]; the benefit is that only the caster can see and use the passage.

The inside of the passageway is still a part of the material plane; the inter-dimensional warping of space simply acts to skip the caster through the material through which the passage is created.
