## Notes

[[Druidic\|Druidic]] (Druids' Cant) is the secret language of the druidic order, learned by every [[Druid\|Druid]] as part of their training. It cannot be learned by non-druids — except by an [[Assassin\|Assassin]] of 9th level or higher who has Intelligence 15+, who may add it as one of their special languages.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]] (automatic upon training); [[Assassin\|Assassin]] (learnable at level 9+ with INT 15+)
- **Type:** Secret spoken language
- **Restrictions:** Non-druids cannot learn Druidic except via the Assassin's 9th-level special-languages ability
- **Use:** Communication between druids; hierarchy and ritual
