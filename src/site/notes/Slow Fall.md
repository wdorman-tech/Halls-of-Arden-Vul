## Notes

[[Slow Fall\|Slow Fall]] (Controlled Fall) is the [[Monk\|Monk]]'s ability to mitigate damage from falls when within reach of a wall. The reduction increases with level until eventually the monk takes no fall damage at all.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Mechanics:** Automatic if monk is within the listed range of a wall
- **DEX Requirement:** Reduced effect if Dexterity is lower than 15

### Progression

| Monk Level | Wall Distance | DEX 15+ Damage Reduction | DEX <15 Damage Reduction |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------------|-------------------------|
| 4 | 1 ft | -20 ft (usually -2d6) | -10 ft |
| 6 | 5 ft | -30 ft (usually -3d6) | -20 ft |
| 13 | 10 ft | No damage, regardless of fall distance | (DEX <15 still partial) |
