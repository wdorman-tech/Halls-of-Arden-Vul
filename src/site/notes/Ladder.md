## Stats

- **Cost:** 5 sp per 10 ft
- **Weight:** 20 lb per 10 ft

## Notes

A wooden ladder. Useful for crossing pits, scaling walls, or reaching high passages. Bulky to transport — generally cached at one location or carted by [[Mule\|Mule]] or [[Horse, Cart\|cart]].
