## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st (all classes)
- **School:** [[Divine Transmutation\|Divine Transmutation]] / [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 60ft
- **Duration:** 6 turns + 1 turn per cleric level (Cleric) / 1 turn per illusionist or MU level (Illusionist/Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** 20ft radius (sphere for cleric)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments (Cleric) / Fast (Illusionist/Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

The caster creates a magical light, centred on the target, which may be a point in space, a creature, or an object. The light can be extinguished at will. If the target is an area of magical [[Darkness\|Darkness]], then the darkness is nullified for the spell's duration but there is no additional magical light created; the spells cancel each other.

## Details

The light is stationary, unless cast on a creature or object that moves, in which case it moves with that creature or object.

If the target is a creature, then it can choose to roll a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs spells. The target creature applies its Dexterity save modifier, if it has one.

- If it passes the saving throw, then the spell produces a stationary light in the air just behind the target creature, rather than affecting the creature itself.
- If it fails the saving throw and the target was its eyes or light-sensing organs, the target is [[Blinded\|Blinded]] for the spell's duration. If the spell was just targeted on the creature in general, there is no blinding effect.

## Reversed Form

For clerics, the reverse is [[Darkness\|Darkness]]. The arcane/phantasmal version cannot be reversed.
