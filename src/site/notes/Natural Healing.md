## Notes

A character recovers **1 hit point per day of uninterrupted rest**.

If the character has a [[Constitution\|Constitution]] penalty to hit points, they must rest for a number of days equal to the constitution penalty *before* rest begins to restore any hp at all.

A character with high constitution gains a commensurate benefit after the first week of rest: the hp regained during the **second week** is increased by the amount of the character's hp bonus at the start of the week.

**Four weeks of rest will restore any character to full hit points** regardless of how many hp were lost.

## Key Facts

- **Rate:** 1 hp per day of full rest
- **CON penalty delay:** rest a number of days equal to the penalty before any healing
- **CON bonus boost:** during week 2+, daily hp gain is increased by the CON bonus
- **Full restoration:** 4 weeks of rest restores full hp
- **Subdual recovery:** [[Subdual Damage\|Subdual Damage]] recovers at 1 hp per hour
- **Temporary recovery:** [[Damage\|Temporary damage]] recovers at 1 hp per round
