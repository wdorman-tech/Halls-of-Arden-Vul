## Notes

Gnomish is, in the standard [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] rules, the language of the [[Gnome\|Gnome]] ancestry. **In the Halls of Arden Vul setting, gnomes do not exist** — see [[Ancestry Houserules\|Ancestry Houserules]] — and the [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]] ancestry uses Gnome statistics. Imperial Goblins do **not** automatically learn Gnomish; they learn an additional language instead. Anyone who would learn Gnomish from their ancestry also instead learns a language of their choice.

In practice, the only place Gnomish appears in this setting is on old non-imperial documents, in pockets of the deeply buried, and in reference books — it has no living native speakers in the campaign region.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers (in setting):** Effectively none; this language is functionally absent
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left, but mostly historical
- **Setting role:** Antiquarian — old glyphs, dusty tomes, possible ancient ruins
