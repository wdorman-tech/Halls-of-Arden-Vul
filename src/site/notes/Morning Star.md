## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 16+)
- **Damage:** 2d4 vs S/M, 1d6+1 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 7
- **Space Required:** 5ft
- **Length:** 4ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt or Piercing (wielder's choice)
- **Weight:** 12 lb
- **Cost:** 5 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A spiked club or mace. Can be wielded one-handed by characters of STR 16+. Permitted for [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (blunt option).
