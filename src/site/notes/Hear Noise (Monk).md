## Notes

[[Hear Noise (Monk)\|Hear Noise (Monk)]] is one of several thief skills granted to the [[Monk\|Monk]] class. As with thieves, the monk cannot Hear Noise while wearing a helmet that covers their ears.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll; spend 1 round listening
- **Restrictions:** Cannot use if wearing a helmet that covers the ears
