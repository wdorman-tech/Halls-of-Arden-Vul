## Notes

[[Open Hand Damage\|Open Hand Damage]] (Weaponless Attack) is the [[Monk\|Monk]]'s unarmed combat — striking with the hands. The number of attacks per round and the damage die both improve substantially with level, eventually outpacing many weapons.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Monk\|Monk]]
- **Mechanics:** Standard to-hit roll (note: monks do NOT receive Strength bonuses to hit or damage)

### Weapon Damage Bonus
Monks gain a separate **Weapon Damage Bonus** when wielding a permitted weapon. The bonus depends on STR:
- Level 2: +1 (STR 15+) / +0 (STR <15)
- Level 4: +2 (STR 15+) / +1 (STR <15)
- Level 6: +3 (STR 15+) / +2 (STR <15)
- Level 8: +4 (STR 15+) / +3 (STR <15)
- Level 10: +5 (STR 15+) / +4 (STR <15)
- Level 12: +6 (STR 15+) / +5 (STR <15)
- Level 14: +7 (STR 15+) / +6 (STR <15)
- Level 16: +8 (STR 15+) / +7 (STR <15)
