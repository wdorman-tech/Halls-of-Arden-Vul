## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 4 turns + 1 turn per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** 10ft radius
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows you to magically affect the temperature of your immediate surroundings, to a radius of 10ft.

## Details

The temperature may be raised or lowered by 9° Fahrenheit (4° Celsius) per druid level. Once the temperature is "set," the spell will attempt to maintain that temperature unless you decide to change it.
