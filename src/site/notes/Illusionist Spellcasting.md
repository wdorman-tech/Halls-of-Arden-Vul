## Notes

[[Illusionist Spellcasting\|Illusionist Spellcasting]] is phantasmal magic — spells that affect senses and minds. The mechanics are nearly identical to [[Magic-User Spellcasting\|Magic-User Spellcasting]]: the [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] uses a [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]], must understand a spell before memorising it, and must memorise a spell before casting. Phantasmal spells are recorded in [[Phantasmal Notation\|Phantasmal Notation]], a secret system that ordinary magic-users cannot read without [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]].

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Spell Source:** [[Spell Book\|Spell Book]] required
- **Notation:** [[Phantasmal Notation\|Phantasmal Notation]] — readable by any illusionist without [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]]; magic-users need [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]]
- **Spell Levels:** 1 through 7
- **First 7th-Level Spell Slot:** Earned at illusionist level 14
- **Starting Spells:** 3 first-level phantasmal spells from among the 12 first-level spells. 2 random (roll 1d12 twice, re-roll doubles), 1 chosen.
- **Spell Acquisition:** When acquiring a new spell from a scroll or other illusionist's spell book, roll d100 vs INT (Table 1.3.5.2A):

| INT | Chance to Understand | Max Spells Understood per Spell Level |
|-----|---------------------|---------------------------------------|
| 15-16 | 65 | 11 |
| 17 | 75 | 14 |
| 18 | 85 | 18 |
| 19 | 90 | 22 |

- **Memorisation:** Empty spell slots filled by memorising spells from the spell book; cast spells "empty" the slot until re-memorised. Same spell may be memorised in multiple slots. Memorised spells may be voluntarily forgotten to free the slot.
- **Free Spell on Level-Up:** When you gain a level, you can choose one phantasmal spell of any spell level you can cast and add it to your spell book — no money or roll required.
- **Auto-Understand:** When you gain a level you may automatically understand one spell of your choice (replaces the older "minimum spells understood per level" system).
- **Drop and Reroll:** If you've maxed your spells understood for a level, you may "drop" a known spell to attempt a new one. The dropped spell is gone unless you re-roll for it.
- **Copying Cost:** 100 gp per spell level for magical ink; 1 hour per spell level to copy. Copying erases the source spell, eventually destroying the source scroll or book.
- **Scrolls:** May cast any phantasmal spell from a scroll (destroys the scroll) without prior memorisation
- **No Bonus Spells:** Unlike clerics and druids, illusionists do NOT get bonus spell slots from high attribute scores
