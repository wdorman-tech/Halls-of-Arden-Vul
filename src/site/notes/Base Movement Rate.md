## Notes

The **Base Movement Rate** comes from a character's [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] and represents their unencumbered, unrestricted speed. The character's actual [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]] is the BMR adjusted for [[Encumbrance\|Encumbrance]] and any movement caps imposed by [[Armour\|Armour]].

## Key Facts

- **Source:** [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] (humans typically 120, demi-humans typically 60-90)
- **Adjusted by:** [[Encumbrance\|Encumbrance]] category and [[Armour\|Armour]] movement cap
- **Armour cap:** independent of weight; bulkiness sets a maximum MR
- **Encumbrance reductions:** Three Quarter / Half / Quarter / Cannot Move
