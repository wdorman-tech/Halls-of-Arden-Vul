## Notes

The **Base Movement Rate** comes from a character's [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] and represents their unrestricted speed. The character's actual [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]] is the BMR after applying any movement cap imposed by [[Armour\|Armour]].

## Key Facts

- **Source:** [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] (humans typically 120, demi-humans typically 60-90)
- **Adjusted by:** [[Armour\|Armour]] movement cap (if any)
- **Armour cap:** bulkiness sets a maximum MR (90 ft for medium armour, 60 ft for heavy)
