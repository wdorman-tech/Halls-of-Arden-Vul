## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 20ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster or one creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 2 segments
- **Saving Throw:** See below

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to levitate themselves or another person, up to a maximum weight of 100 lbs per level. If the caster casts the spell on themselves, they can move vertically up or down at a rate of 20ft per round. If levitating someone else, the levitation rate is 10ft per round.

## Details

If the target is willing to allow the spell, no [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] is necessary.

The spell may be cast upon an unwilling enemy, but in this case the intended target is entitled to a saving throw. The spell does not allow any sort of horizontal movement (aerial agility level I), but it is possible for a levitated person to pull themselves along if there is something to grab, such as a wall.
