## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Divine Transmutation\|Divine Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 30ft per cleric level / 10ft per MU level
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level / 5 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Path 10ft wide and up to a mile deep, length 30ft per cleric level / See below (Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments (Cleric) / 1 turn (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** Depends on use of spell (see below)

## Notes

This spell opens a dry pathway through a body of water (not exceeding a mile in depth). For this use, there is no [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]]. It can be used to destroy water elementals.

## Details

Only bodies of water are affected. This spell doesn't function on the Elemental Plane of Water.

If the target is a water elemental, or contains water elementals, then each affected elemental must attempt a saving throw vs spells and other magical effects. Affected water elementals that fail their saving throws are destroyed. Those that succeed still take 6d6 points of raw [[Magical Damage\|Magical Damage]].
