## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 30ft
- **Duration:** 2d4 rounds + 1 round per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** 1 Cube (20ft) per illusionist level
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates a curtain of obscuring fog in the area of effect. Vision of all kinds is reduced to 2ft through the swirling vapour.

## Details

The fog can be affected by normal or magical wind and breezes (usually shortening the duration).

If the caster desires, they can reduce the size of the area of effect.
