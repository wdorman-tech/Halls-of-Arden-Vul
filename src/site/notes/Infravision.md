## Notes

Infravision is the demi-human ability to see in the dark by detecting heat. All non-human ancestries in OSRIC have 60ft infravision; full mechanics are in [[OSRIC\|OSRIC]] Section 1.5.4 (Light and Vision). It is what lets [[Dwarf\|dwarves]], [[Elf\|elves]], [[Gnome\|gnomes]], [[Half-Elf\|half-elves]], [[Halfling\|halflings]], and [[Half-Orc\|half-orcs]] navigate lightless tunnels without a torch — and why [[Human\|Human]]s are the only ancestry that genuinely needs a [[Light Source\|Light Source]] underground.

## Key Facts

- **Range:** 60ft
- **Who has it:** every demi-human ancestry — [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]], [[Elf\|Elf]], [[Gnome\|Gnome]], [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]], [[Halfling\|Halfling]], [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]] (also [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]] in this campaign)
- **Who lacks it:** [[Human\|Human]]
- **Reference:** OSRIC 3.0 Section 1.5.4: Light and Vision
