## Notes

The Ten are the primary deities of the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]] — the gods of imperial state worship. Their cults are well-funded, their temples grand, and their clerics common throughout the empire. Other cultures may acknowledge or even worship them, but it is the Archonteans who place these ten at the head of the [[Pantheon\|Pantheon]].
