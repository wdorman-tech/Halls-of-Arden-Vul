## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** 40ft
- **Duration:** 3 rounds + 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Summoned creatures
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By casting this spell, the caster conjures up 1d6 monsters to serve them as allies in combat or to perform other services. Technically, this spell is a conjuration: the monsters appear from thin air within 1d4 rounds of the spell's completed casting.

## Details

If the caster is in combat, the monsters will fight on their behalf, attacking whatever foes they direct, or guarding the caster. For more complex tasks, the caster must somehow have the ability to communicate these more specific commands.

The GM has the ultimate discretion as to what monsters will appear, but the general likelihood is described in the following table:

| D6 | Monster Summoned |
|---|---|
| 1 | Centipede, giant |
| 2 | Devil, lemure |
| 3 | Gnoll |
| 4 | Stirge |
| 5 | Toad, giant |
| 6 | Troglodyte |
