## Notes

An **invisible** opponent can only be attacked if their general location is known. The attack is at **-4 to hit**. If an opponent is invisible to the attacker, the attacker cannot use rear/flank bonuses against the invisible target.

Note that more powerful monsters (those with sensitive smell or hearing, or **more than 6 HD**) will frequently be able to detect invisible opponents — see the GM Guide table. The monster cannot "see" the invisible creature and still attacks at -4, but since the location is known, the attack can occur.

Invisible creatures have a **+4 bonus on all saving throws**.

## Key Facts

- **Attack penalty:** -4 to hit (if location known)
- **Cannot be attacked at all:** if location unknown
- **No flank/rear bonuses** against invisible targets
- **Saves:** +4 on all saving throws
- **Detection:** sensitive senses, magic, monsters with >6 HD frequently detect
- **Doesn't stack with [[Concealment\|Concealment]]** (concealment is "partial invisibility")
- **Common sources:** [[Invisibility\|Invisibility]] spell, [[Improved Invisibility\|Improved Invisibility]], [[Cloak of Invisibility\|Cloak of Invisibility]], [[Ring of Invisibility\|Ring of Invisibility]]
- **Distinct from [[Ethereal\|Ethereal]]:** invisibility is not intangibility
