## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Evocation\|Arcane Evocation]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 hour per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to make a written copy of a spell they don't yet have the power to cast, and it is a somewhat dangerous spell to use.

## Details

In order to succeed in this task, the caster must make a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs spells, with a modifier determined by the difficulty of the spell being transcribed.

- If the spell is up to one level higher than the caster can cast, the saving throw is made at +2.
- If the spell is two levels higher than the caster can cast, there is no bonus or penalty to the saving throw.
- If the spell is more than 2 levels higher than the caster can cast, each additional level adds a -1 penalty.

If the caster fails this saving throw, the spell is not successfully copied, the caster takes 1d4 points of magical [[Damage\|Damage]] per level of the spell attempted, and they are knocked unconscious for 1 turn per hit point sustained thereby.

The damage sustained cannot be healed more quickly than 4 hp per day, even with the assistance of magical healing.

If the saving throw is successful, the caster may copy the spell, which takes one hour per level of the spell.

The material component for the spell is ink, costing at least 200 gp.
