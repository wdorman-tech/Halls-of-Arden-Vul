## Notes

In a dungeon, adventurers need a [[Light Sources\|light source]] unless all characters have **infravision**.

**Infravision** is the ability to see in the dark and is common to almost all subterranean creatures. It detects heat patterns, not colour, and is of little help while searching or making minute examinations — sapient creatures such as orcs may prefer torchlight even if they possess infravision.

Infravision **cannot be used within the ambit of any light source**. Unless otherwise stated, the range is **60 feet**, though some exceptional subterranean creatures see farther.

## Key Facts

- **Infravision range:** 60 ft (default)
- **Disabled by:** any nearby light source
- **Detects:** heat patterns, not colour
- **Limited for:** searching, fine examination, reading, secret door location
- **Common ancestries:** [[Dwarf\|Dwarf]], [[Elf\|Elf]], [[Half-Elf\|Half-elf]], [[Gnome\|Gnome]], [[Half-Orc\|Half-orc]] (most demi-humans)
- **Undead via infravision:** appear as possibly statues (no heat)
- **Used in dungeon survey:** see [[Searching\|Searching]]
