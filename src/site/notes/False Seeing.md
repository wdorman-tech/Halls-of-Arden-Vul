## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Divination\|Divination]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 round per cleric level
- **Area of Effect:** 120ft radius around the creature you touch
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

This spell affects one creature. The target sees wild hallucinations. It is [[Blinded\|Blinded]] for the spell's duration, and must attempt a [[Morale\|Morale]] check each round or flee screaming in a random direction.

## Details

The target must add its Wisdom [[Saving Throw\|saving throw]] modifier to the saving throw attempt.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[True Seeing\|True Seeing]].
