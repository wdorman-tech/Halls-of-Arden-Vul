## Notes

Demma is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the lawful good goddess of fertility, crops, households, childbirth, and magic. She is a hearth-mother in the Archontean cult — patron of farmers, midwives, and household magics — but the explicit listing of magic among her domains makes her a surprisingly common patron for benevolent rural magic-users as well. Her symbol is a seed; her weapon, a flail.
