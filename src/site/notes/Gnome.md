## Notes

Gnomes are small folk loosely related to dwarfs — shorter, slimmer, longer-nosed and bearded, with a knack for illusion, misdirection, gemcutting, and fine handicrafts. They prefer burrows and tunnels and share dwarven resistance to magic and sensitivity to stone. Gnomes reach adulthood around 90 and live 600-750 years.

## Houserule

**Gnomes do not exist in the [[Halls of Arden Vul\|Halls of Arden Vul]] setting.** Per the Player Doc, the [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]] ancestry uses Gnome statistics, except that Imperial Goblins learn an additional language instead of [[Gnomish\|Gnomish]]. See [[Imperial Goblin\|Imperial Goblin]] for the campaign-specific version of this stat block.

> [!warning] Additional Halls of Arden Vul houserule (applies to the Imperial Goblin replacement)
> **[[Common\|Common]] does not exist in this setting either.** Anywhere "Common" appears in the language list below, it is **replaced by one additional language of player choice** (see [[Ancestry Houserules\|Ancestry Houserules]]). For Imperial Goblins this stacks with the language swap that already replaces Gnomish.

## Key Facts

- **Size:** Small
- **Base Movement Rate:** 90ft (see [[Movement Rate\|Movement Rate]])
- **Ability Score Adjustments:** None
- **Required Scores:** STR 6-18, DEX 3-18, CON 8-18, INT 7-18, WIS 3-17, CHA 3-18
- **Languages:** [[Common\|Common]], [[Dwarfish\|Dwarfish]], [[Gnomish\|Gnomish]], [[Goblin (Language)\|Goblin]], [[Halfling (Language)\|Halfling]], [[Kobold (Language)\|Kobold]], [[Burrowing Animal Tongue\|Burrowing Animal Tongue]], plus [[Alignment Tongue\|Alignment Tongue]]; max two further languages regardless of [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]]
- **Available classes:** [[Assassin\|Assassin]], [[Cleric\|Cleric]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]], [[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Multi-class options:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Illusionist\|Illusionist]], [[Fighter\|Fighter]]/[[Thief\|Thief]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]/[[Thief\|Thief]]
- **Level limits:** Assassin 8, Cleric 7, Fighter 6 (STR 18+) / 5 (STR 17-), Illusionist 7 (INT+DEX 35+) / 6 (both 17+) / 5 (others), Thief unlimited
- **Ancestral traits:** [[Infravision\|Infravision]] (60ft), [[Stalwart\|Stalwart]], [[Stone-Kenning\|Stone-Kenning]] (gnomish percentages), [[Giant Slayer\|Giant Slayer]], [[Ancestral Foes\|Ancestral Foes]]
- **Lifespan:** 600-750 years; adulthood at ~90
- **Starting Age:** Cleric 300+3d12; Fighter 60+5d4; Illusionist 100+2d12; Thief or Assassin 80+5d4
- **Random Height/Weight:** 2ft 10in + 3d4 inches; 45 + 4d10 lbs
