## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Druid\|Druid]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th (Cleric, Druid) / 6th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Conjuration\|Divine Conjuration]] / [[Druidic Transmutation\|Druidic Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Nil
- **Duration:** 4d12 hours (Cleric) / 8d12 hours (Druid) / 4d6 hours (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** Up to 4d4 square miles (Cleric) / 4d8 square miles (Druid) / 4d4 square miles (Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 10 rounds (Cleric) / 1 turn (Druid, Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell allows the caster to control precipitation, temperature, and wind speed in a large area. The new weather takes 10d4 minutes to reach full strength. The druid version brings about a more rapid change (10-40 minutes for full effect across the vast area).

## Details

This spell won't generate weather conditions that don't occur naturally where the caster is. For example, it can't generate a snowstorm in the tropics, nor a monsoon in the tundra.

This spell only works on the Prime Material Plane, the Elemental Plane of Air, or the Elemental Plane of Water; or in some other part of the multiverse where the GM says there is weather. This spell can't affect the rare forms of weather on the Astral or Ethereal Planes. In the Outer Planes, the weather is usually under the direct control of a god or other power.

This spell affects the weather, not the geological or astronomical conditions. The caster can't use this spell to create an earthquake or eclipse.

The cleric and magic-user versions: The caster can move the weather one step on the precipitation and wind speed columns. Temperature can be altered by no more than 1d10 + 10 degrees Fahrenheit.

The druid version: Existing weather conditions cannot be utterly changed by the force of this spell, but druids can cause more change than clerics; effectively twice the amount of change is possible (two steps on the precipitation/wind speed columns). This spell may be cast more than once in succession to bring about a dramatic change.

### Precipitation (cumulative steps)

Clear → Light clouds → Cloudy → Light precipitation → Medium precipitation → Heavy precipitation

### Wind Speed (cumulative steps)

Calm → Light breeze → Wind → Strong wind → Gale
