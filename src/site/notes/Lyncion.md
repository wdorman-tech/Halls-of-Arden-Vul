## Notes

Lyncion is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the neutral evil god of secrets, magic, demons, hidden knowledge, and power. He is the patron of the [[New School of Arcane Might\|New School of Arcane Might]] in spirit if not in formal sponsorship — the god whose followers will trade morality for arcane advantage. The blue eye is his symbol; the dagger his weapon.
