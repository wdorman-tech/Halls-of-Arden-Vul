## Notes

In the Halls of Arden Vul setting, every human PC must select a **culture** at character creation. The culture grants a starting language and shapes the character's place in the world. There are three available cultures: [[Archontean\|Archontean]] (the spreading empire), [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] (rural natives of the continent), and [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]] (viking-like northerners).

A human always starts with: their **culture's language**, their **alignment tongue**, and **one other language** of their choice.

## Key Facts

- **Available cultures:** [[Archontean\|Archontean]], [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]], [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]]
- **Restricted to:** [[Human\|Human]] characters
- **Default starting languages:** culture language + alignment tongue + 1 free choice
- **Optional swaps:** Player Doc allows replacing starting languages with [[Archontean (language)\|Archontean (language)]], [[Thorcin (language)\|Thorcin (language)]], [[Wiskin (language)\|Wiskin (language)]], or [[Mithric\|Mithric]]
