## Notes

Bisor is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the lawful good god of the household, security, and lineage. He is the quiet god of locked doors and family lines — patron of those who guard the home. His symbol is a blue key; his weapon, the staff.

- **Pantheon:** [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]

- **Symbol(s):** Blue key
- **Weapon(s):** Staff
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Lawful Good\|Lawful Good]]
