## Notes

The pantheon of the Halls of Arden Vul setting is divided into two tiers from the perspective of the [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]]:

- **[[The Ten\|The Ten]]** are the major Archontean deities — the gods of state worship.
- **[[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]** are minor deities to the Archonteans, but many are followed faithfully by other cultures (especially the [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] and [[Wiskin\|Wiskin]]).

A [[Cleric\|Cleric]] must choose a deity from one of these lists, and (per houserule in [[Alignment\|Alignment]]) must match that deity's alignment. The deity also typically restricts what weapon a cleric may use (often a flail, hammer, or other blunt weapon, but not always).

## Key Facts

- **Total deities:** 27 (10 + 17)
- **Restricted to clerics:** must pick one and match its alignment
- **Areas of influence:** spelled out in each deity's note
- **Cleric weapon:** generally limited to the deity's listed weapon(s)
