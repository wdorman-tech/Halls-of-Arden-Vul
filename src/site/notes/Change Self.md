## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Illusion\|Phantasmal Illusion]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 2d6 rounds + 2 rounds per illusionist level
- **Area of Effect:** Caster
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By means of this spell, the caster changes their appearance into that of any bipedal humanoid form desired, such as an orc, or a particular person they want to impersonate.

## Details

Size and weight can only be altered by one foot and 50 lbs, respectively. The face may be changed as desired, including alterations to make the caster appear identical to an existing person.

Personal gear and clothing may also be changed in appearance.
