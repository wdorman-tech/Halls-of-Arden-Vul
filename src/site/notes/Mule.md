## Stats

- **Cost:** 18 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 24 (6 × 4 grid)

## Notes

A surefooted hybrid of horse and donkey — strong, patient, and capable of navigating rough terrain. **Mules are the only draft animal that will go belowground**, making them invaluable for hauling treasure and supplies into and out of a [[Dungeon\|Dungeon]].

Mules carry items on a **6 × 4 = 24 slot pack-saddle grid**, using the same slot sizes as a player's inventory grid. See [[Slot-Based Inventory\|Slot-Based Inventory]].
