## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 3rd
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Permanent
- **Area of Effect:** Creature touched
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 8 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

This spell causes the target to become blind. The blindness is potentially permanent; it lasts until the spell is removed by the caster, dispelled, or removed by [[Cure Blindness\|Cure Blindness]].

## Details

A miss on the to-hit roll means the spell is lost.

This spell only affects living creatures (not [[Undead\|Undead]]) and has no effect on creatures that don't rely on vision.

Some good-aligned religions ban their clerics from casting this spell. Others allow their clerics to cast it only at times of great need.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Cure Blindness\|Cure Blindness]].
