## Notes

Thieves' Cant is the secret slang and code-language of the [[Thief\|Thief]] class — a working dialect of street terms, hand-signs, and concealed phrasings that lets thieves recognise each other and trade information without alerting marks or guards. [[Assassin\|Assassin]]s also know Thieves' Cant.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (automatic), [[Assassin\|Assassin]] (automatic)
- **Available to:** automatic for those classes; not normally learnable by others
- **Setting role:** Identifying fellow rogues, fencing goods, reading street-marks for safe houses and danger spots
