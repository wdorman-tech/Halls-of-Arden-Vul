## Notes

Strength of Mind is the [[Half-Elf\|half-elven]] partial resistance to [[Sleep\|Sleep]] and [[Charm Person\|charm]] magic — the watered-down version of the elven [[Strength of Will\|Strength of Will]]. On a d100 roll of 01-30 the spell has no effect; on 31-00 it may affect normally but the half-elf still gets any allowed [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]].

## Key Facts

- **Effect:** total immunity on a roll of 01-30 on d100 against [[Sleep\|Sleep]] or [[Charm Person\|charm]] effects
- **On a 31-00:** the spell may affect normally, but a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] is still allowed if one would normally apply
- **Who has it:** [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]] only
- **Elven version:** [[Strength of Will\|Strength of Will]] (90% immunity instead of 30%)
