## Notes

**Listening** may be performed in most places, often at doors before opening them. The entire party must be still and quiet, and headgear (helms) must be doffed, or only the loudest noises will be heard.

The GM normally rolls this die in **secret**, because the player has no way of knowing whether nothing was heard because of the roll or because there was nothing to hear.

## Key Facts

- **Roll:** d100, must be ≤ chance
- **Standard chance:** 10%
- **Demi-human bonus:** 15% for [[Elf\|elves]], [[Gnome\|gnomes]], [[Halfling\|halflings]], [[Half-Orc\|half-orcs]]
- **Thieves/Assassins:** enhanced chance (see "Thief Skills") — improves with level
- **Time:** 1 round per attempt
- **Maximum attempts:** 3 per character before strain prevents further attempts; rest 1 turn to reset
- **Stealthy monsters:** [[Bugbear\|bugbears]] and other stealthy creatures cannot be detected by listening
- **Information given:** GM should describe sounds (a "scuttling, rattling sound") rather than identifications ("a giant spider")
- **Note:** monsters can hear the party in the same way
