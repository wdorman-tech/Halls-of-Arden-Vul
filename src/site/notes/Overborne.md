## Notes

The **overborne** condition can happen when a defender is knocked [[Prone\|prone]] or [[Staggered\|staggered]] by an [[Overbearing\|overbearing attack]] and cannot easily recover balance because of an ongoing attack. The victim is usually "**staggered and overborne**" or "**prone and overborne**," and multiple conditions like "grappled, staggered, and overborne" are possible.

Being overborne **does not create any penalties of its own**. What it does is **restrict the ability to get out of a [[Staggered\|staggered]] or [[Prone\|prone]] condition**.

## Key Facts

- **Effects:** none on its own; *prevents recovery* from prone/staggered
- **Duration:** until one of the escape conditions is met
- **Escape conditions:**
  - Overborne combatant is **larger** than the attacker AND attacker is the only enemy in melee range — overbearing lasts only that round
  - The opponents are grappling and the overborne combatant gets a result allowing them to rise
  - The overbearing combatant **dies, is knocked unconscious, is overborne by another, or leaves melee combat range**
- **Stacks with:** [[Prone\|Prone]], [[Staggered\|Staggered]], [[Grappling\|Grappling]] — typically inflicted alongside one of these
- **Source:** [[Overbearing\|Overbearing]] attacks (Table 1.6.12b results)
