## Notes

Thorcin is the language of the [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] people, native to this continent. It predates the Archontean expansion and is still the language of rural villages and woodland clans. Knowing Thorcin is invaluable for anyone trying to negotiate with native populations resentful of imperial rule.

## Key Facts

- **Speakers:** [[Thorcin\|Thorcin]] (native)
- **Available to:** any character with sufficient [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]] and a slot left; available as a language swap at character creation
- **Setting role:** Rural diplomacy, negotiation with native populations, reading Thorcin folk-songs and oral histories
