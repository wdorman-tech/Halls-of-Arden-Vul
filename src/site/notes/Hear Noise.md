## Notes

[[Hear Noise\|Hear Noise]] (Listen) is the [[Thief\|Thief]]'s ability to detect quiet sounds — such as a creature trying to be quiet — by spending a round listening. The percentage improves with thief level; any character may listen with the same chance as a 1st-level thief, but only thieves improve.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Thief\|Thief]] (and [[Assassin\|Assassin]] same table; [[Monk\|Monk]] also has it). Any character may use the level-1 chance.
- **Mechanics:** d100 roll; spend 1 round listening
- **Restriction:** Cannot listen if wearing a helmet that covers the ears
- **Modifiers:** [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4C); [[Ancestry\|Ancestry]] adjustments (Table 1.3.10.4D)
