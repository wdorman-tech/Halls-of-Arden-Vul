## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st (Cleric) / 2nd (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Divination\|Divine Divination]] / [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 1 turn, plus 5 rounds per cleric level / 5 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Path 10ft wide and 120ft long (Cleric) / 60ft long (Magic-User)
- **Components:** V, S, M (Cleric) / V, S (Magic-User)
- **Casting Time:** 1 round (Cleric) / 2 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The caster sees an aura around each good-aligned creature, object, or place within the area of effect. If invisible, the caster knows where it is but can't see details. The penalty for attacking an invisible target is halved.

## Details

Only the caster "sees" the aura of good presences. This spell reveals good alignments, not safety or benefit, so won't necessarily spot a healing potion or helpful item.

Opaque materials can interfere with the spell. The spell can only penetrate metal if it's one inch thick or less, and brick or stone if it's one foot thick or less.

This spell only detects a creature, object, or place if it has a good alignment or if something in the rules specifically describes it as good or holy.

The caster must concentrate on maintaining the spell, or it ends. See [[Concentration\|Concentration]].

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]].
