## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Druid\|Druid]]
- **Level:** 2nd
- **School:** [[Druidic\|Druidic]] [[Transmutation\|Transmutation]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 4 rounds + 1 round per druid level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

The barkskin spell magically toughens a creature's skin, making it as strong as tree bark.

## Details

The recipient of the spell improves their [[Armour Class\|Armour Class]] by one point and also gains a +1 bonus on all saving throws, with the exception of [[Saving Throw\|saving throws]] against Spells.
