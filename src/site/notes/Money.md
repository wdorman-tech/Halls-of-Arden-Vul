## Coinage System

The basic unit of currency in OSRIC is the gold piece (gp). OSRIC prices far exceed those of the real medieval world; gold is more plentiful and worth less, allowing for huge fantasy treasure piles without destroying the game economy.

### Exchange Rates

- 1 [[Platinum Piece\|Platinum Piece]] = 5 [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]]s
- 1 [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]] = 2 [[Electrum Piece\|Electrum Piece]]s
- 1 [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]] = 10 [[Silver Piece\|Silver Piece]]s
- 1 [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]] = 100 [[Copper Piece\|Copper Piece]]s

### Coin Weight

Ten coins weigh one pound. All coins weigh the same regardless of denomination, so in OSRIC a "coin" is a unit of weight equal to 0.1 lbs. Some OSRIC-compatible publishers use "gold pieces" ("gp") as units of weight as well as currency.
