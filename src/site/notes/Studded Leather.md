## Armour Stats

- **AC (Descending):** 7 [Ascending: 13]
- **Movement Cap:** 90ft
- **Weight:** 20 lb
- **Cost:** 15 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 4 in 2x2

## Allowed for

- [[Fighter\|Fighter]], [[Paladin\|Paladin]], [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- [[Thief\|Thief]] (one of the few "studded" choices that still permits thief skills, though more restrictively than plain leather)
- [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot wear (uses leather only)
- [[Monk\|Monk]] cannot wear armour
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]], [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] cannot wear armour

## Notes

Leather reinforced with metal studs or rings. Provides better protection than [[Leather Armour\|Leather Armour]] but reduces movement to 90ft. All purchased armour comes with a [[Helmet\|Helmet]] included in the price.
