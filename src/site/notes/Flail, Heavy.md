## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2, or 1 (STR 14+)
- **Damage:** 1d6+1 vs S/M, 2d4 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 7
- **Space Required:** 6ft
- **Length:** 4ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt
- **Weight:** 15 lb
- **Cost:** 8 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A weighted head connected to a shaft by a chain. Effective against shields. Permitted for [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (blunt).

The wide space requirement (6ft) restricts the number of wielders side-by-side in narrow corridors.
