## Notes

Thelis is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the chaotic good goddess of love, desire, aspiration, and planning. Where [[Crestion\|Crestion]] is the artist who creates from impulse, Thelis is the lover who dreams large and plans the way. Her symbol is a diamond; her weapon, a sword.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]]
- **Alignment:** [[Chaotic Good\|Chaotic Good]]
- **Gender:** F
- **Areas of Influence:** Love, desire, aspiration, planning
- **Symbol(s):** Diamond
- **Weapon(s):** Sword
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Chaotic Good\|Chaotic Good]]
