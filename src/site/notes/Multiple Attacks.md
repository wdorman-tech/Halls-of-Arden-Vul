## Notes

[[Multiple Attacks\|Multiple Attacks]] covers the level-based attack-rate progression for [[Fighter\|Fighter]] (and similarly for [[Paladin\|Paladin]] and [[Ranger\|Ranger]]). At low levels fighters get [[Multiple Attacks\|Heroic Assault]] against truly weak foes; at mid level they gain Melee Attack Combinations of 3/2 and eventually 2/1 against any opponent. These bonuses do NOT stack with [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]]; specialisation replaces the level-based progression for the specialised weapon.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Fighter\|Fighter]] primarily; analogous progressions for [[Paladin\|Paladin]] and [[Ranger\|Ranger]] (see those classes)

### Heroic Assault (Fighter level 2; also Paladin level 2 and Ranger level 2)
- **Eligible Targets:** Enemies that COULDN'T have rolled 8 or more hp on their hit dice (e.g. 1d6+1 OK; 2d4 or 1d8 NOT eligible)
- **Timing:** Acts on segment 1 of the round, regardless of initiative
- **Number of Attacks:** One per level the character has in the class (vs eligible targets in weapon range)
- **Restriction:** Cannot also make a regular attack or Melee Attack Combination that round
- **Example:** A 5th-level fighter facing 5 kobolds (1d4 hd each) may attack each kobold

### Melee Attack Combination 3/2 (Fighter level 7; Paladin level 7; Ranger level 8)
- **3 attacks over 2 rounds**
- Round 1: attack in segments 1 and 10, regardless of initiative
- Round 2: no extra attack — attack in normal initiative segment
- Round 3: pattern restarts
- **Specialised Weapon:** Improves to 2/1 (see [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]])

### Mighty Blow (Fighter level 8; Paladin level 8; Ranger level 8)
- **When the to-hit number needed is negative,** convert the negative to a positive and add it to the damage roll
- **Example:** 20th-level fighter, STR 19, +5 sword vs AC 2 [18] dragon needs -9 to hit → adds +9 to damage

### Melee Attack Combination 2/1 (Fighter level 13; Paladin level 13; Ranger level 15)
- **2 attacks per round** in segments 1 and 10, regardless of initiative
- If something delays the second attack past segment 10, it doesn't happen
- **Specialised Weapon:** Improves to 5/2 (see [[Weapon Specialisation\|Weapon Specialisation]])

### Stacking
- These class progressions only apply to NON-specialised weapons. With a [[Weapon Specialisation\|specialised weapon]], the specialist attack rate replaces (does not stack with) this progression.
