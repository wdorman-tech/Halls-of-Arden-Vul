## Notes

Lucreon is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the lawful neutral god of profit, amassing wealth, negotiation, merchants, and impartiality. He is the patron of every honest (and many merely-correct) ledger in the empire. His symbol is a gold coin; his weapon is the hammer — the auctioneer's gavel as much as the smith's tool.
