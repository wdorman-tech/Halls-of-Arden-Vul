## Notes

Ardenia Tessaeron is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the lawful goddess of wisdom, battle tactics, invention, and justice. Her owl symbol marks her as a goddess of insight, and her spear of her readiness to fight justly when the moment demands. She is a natural patron of cunning officers, judges, and inventors who serve the cause of order.
