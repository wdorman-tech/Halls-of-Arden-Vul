## Notes

[[Turn Undead (Paladin)\|Turn Undead (Paladin)]] is the [[Paladin\|Paladin]]'s version of the cleric ability — gained at 3rd level and operating two cleric levels behind the paladin's level. See [[Turn Undead\|Turn Undead]] for full mechanics.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 3rd
- **Mechanics:** Treats the paladin as a cleric two levels lower than the paladin's level (e.g. a 3rd-level paladin turns as a 1st-level cleric, a 5th-level paladin turns as a 3rd-level cleric)
- **Effect:** As a good-aligned [[Cleric\|Cleric]] — turns and may destroy undead
