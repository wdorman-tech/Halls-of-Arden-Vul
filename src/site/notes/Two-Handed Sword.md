## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee
- **Hands:** 2
- **Damage:** 1d10 vs S/M, 3d6 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 10
- **Space Required:** 6ft
- **Length:** 6ft
- **Damage Type:** Slashing
- **Weight:** 25 lb
- **Cost:** 30 gp
- **Inventory Slots:** 2 (large item)

## Notes

A great two-handed slashing sword. Excellent damage vs large opponents (3d6) but very slow speed factor (10) and wide space requirement (6ft) limit its use in narrow corridors.

The wielder cannot also carry a [[Shield, Small\|shield]].

[[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] cannot use this weapon (edged).
