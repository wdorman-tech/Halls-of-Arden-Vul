## Notes

Heschius Ban is among [[The Ten\|The Ten]], the lawful evil god of triumph, water, inevitability, power, and magic. He is the god of triumphal certainty — the conqueror who arrives like a tide that cannot be turned back. His symbols are shackles and a green oval; his weapon is the flail.

## Key Facts

- **Pantheon:** [[The Ten\|The Ten]]
- **Alignment:** [[Lawful Evil\|Lawful Evil]]
- **Gender:** M
- **Areas of Influence:** Triumph, water, inevitability, power, magic
- **Symbol(s):** Shackles, a green oval
- **Weapon(s):** [[Flail\|Flail]]
- **Followers:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]s must be [[Lawful Evil\|Lawful Evil]]
