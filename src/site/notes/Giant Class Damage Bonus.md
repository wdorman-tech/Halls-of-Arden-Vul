## Notes

[[Giant Class Damage Bonus\|Giant Class Damage Bonus]] (Fell Handed) is the [[Ranger\|Ranger]]'s class-defining bonus damage against evil humanoids and giants. Each ranger level adds +1 to damage when the ranger uses a melee weapon to attack such a creature.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)
- **Mechanics:** When using a MELEE weapon to attack an evil humanoid or giant, may choose to add +1 damage per ranger level
- **Eligible Targets:** Evil humanoids; giants (giant-class creatures)
- **Restrictions:** Melee only — not for ranged attacks (per source text wording)
