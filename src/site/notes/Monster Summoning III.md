## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 5th
- **School:** [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** 50ft
- **Duration:** 4 rounds + 1 round per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** Summoned creatures
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 5 segments
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By casting this spell, the caster conjures up 1d4 monsters to serve them as allies in combat or to perform other services. Technically, this spell is a conjuration: the monsters appear from thin air within 1d4 rounds of the spell's completed casting.

## Details

If the caster is in combat, the monsters will fight on their behalf, attacking whatever foes they direct, or guarding the caster.
