## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Divination\|Arcane Divination]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 2 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 round
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell makes magical writings completely unreadable for the spell's duration.

## Details

The area of effect is a single scroll, book, or inscription that the caster must touch in order for the spell to take effect.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Read Magic\|Read Magic]].
