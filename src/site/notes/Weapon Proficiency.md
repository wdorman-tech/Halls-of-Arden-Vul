## Concept

Each character class has a set number of weapon proficiency slots indicating which weapons they have trained with. Using a weapon you are not proficient in incurs a **non-proficiency penalty** to attack rolls, which varies by class.

A weapon used with a strength below its required minimum (e.g., wielding a [[Bastard Sword\|Bastard Sword]] one-handed without STR 15+) also imposes the non-proficiency penalty for the wielder's class.

## Class Restrictions on Weapons

- [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]]: cannot use **edged** weapons (only blunt: [[Mace, Heavy\|Mace]], [[Club\|Club]], [[Quarterstaff\|Quarterstaff]], [[Flail, Light\|Flail]], [[Warhammer, Heavy\|Warhammer]], [[Sling\|Sling]]).
- [[Druid\|Druid]]: only wooden shields (treated as small shields).
- [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]] / [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]]: very limited weapon options ([[Dagger\|Dagger]], [[Quarterstaff\|Quarterstaff]], [[Dart\|Dart]]).
- [[Monk\|Monk]]: limited weapon list per class.
- [[Thief\|Thief]]: any weapon they can wield, but weapon damage cap rules apply.
