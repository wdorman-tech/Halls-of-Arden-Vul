## Notes

Once combat begins, the order of events for each [[Combat Round\|round]] follows a fixed sequence:

## Key Facts

**Round 1 only — at start of encounter:**
1. **Determine [[Surprise\|Surprise]] (d6) and [[Encounter Distance\|Encounter Distance]]** (1d4+4 x 10 ft normally; 1d3 x 10 ft if anyone is surprised)

**Every round:**
2. **Declare spells and general actions** ([[Combat Action\|Combat Action]])
3. **Determine [[Initiative\|Initiative]] (d6)** — low roll goes first; missile bonuses may move characters earlier; fighter-types with multi-attacks act in specific segments
4. **Party with initiative acts first** — spells, attacks, etc. Effects take effect (spells finish casting before they go off). Some actions allow the other side to interrupt (fleeing attacks, set-against-charge, etc.)
5. **Party that lost initiative acts** — same rules
6. **Round complete** — declare actions for the next round if combat continues; loop to step 2
