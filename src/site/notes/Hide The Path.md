## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]]
- **Level:** 6th
- **School:** [[Divine\|Divine]] [[Enchantment\|Enchantment]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** 1 turn per cleric level
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 3 rounds
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

The creature you touch loses all sense of direction. If it tries to move, then its heading is randomly determined.

## Details

The creature can make weapon attacks or unarmed attacks, and cast spells, as normal. Only its directional movements are random.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Find The Path\|Find The Path]].
