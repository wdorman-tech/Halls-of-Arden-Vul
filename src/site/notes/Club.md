## Weapon Stats

- **Type:** Melee / Missile (Hurled)
- **Hands:** 1
- **Damage:** 1d4 vs S/M, 1d3 vs L
- **Speed Factor:** 4
- **Space Required:** 3ft
- **Length:** 3ft
- **Damage Type:** Blunt
- **Range Increment:** 10ft (Hurled)
- **Rate of Fire:** 1 per round (when thrown)
- **Weight:** 3 lb
- **Cost:** 2 cp or found
- **Inventory Slots:** 1

## Notes

The simplest weapon — a heavy stick. Often free or scavenged from the environment. Permitted to all classes including [[Cleric\|Cleric]] and [[Druid\|Druid]] (blunt).
