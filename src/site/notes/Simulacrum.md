## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 7th
- **School:** [[Arcane Illusion\|Arcane Illusion]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous (permanent)
- **Area of Effect:** One creature
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 1 day
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This eerie spell permits the magic user to make a living duplicate of another creature using ice or snow as the raw material of the duplicate's substance.

## Details

The simulacrum is identical in appearance to the original in even the minutest detail. The simulacrum will always be weaker than the original, having only half of the original [[Hit Points\|Hit Points]] and a lower level of experience (1d4+1 x 10%). The simulacrum does not have its own personality; it is under the caster's control and has no volition without the caster's spoken command.

A simulacrum can be improved by the use of other spells; a [[Reincarnation\|Reincarnation]] spell will provide it with its own personality, and a [[Limited Wish\|Limited Wish]] may be used to give it the original's personality and 40-60% of the original's memories.

Casting the spell requires material components of 1,000 gp in value, and a part (even so small as a piece of hair) of the creature to be duplicated.
