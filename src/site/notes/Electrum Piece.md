- **Weight:** 0.1 lb (10 coins = 1 lb)

## Exchange

- 2 ep = 1 [[Gold Piece\|Gold Piece]]
- 1 ep = 5 [[Silver Piece\|Silver Piece]]s

## Notes

An alloy of gold and silver, electrum coins are less common than gold or silver but appear frequently in dungeon hoards. Often associated with ancient or non-human civilisations.
