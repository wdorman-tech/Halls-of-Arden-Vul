## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 4th
- **School:** [[Arcane Enchantment\|Arcane Enchantment]]
- **Range:** 10ft
- **Duration:** 2 rounds per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 15ft radius around fire
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** 4 segments
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (see below)

## Notes

This spell transforms an existing fire into a powerful mental trap.

## Details

Any creature within the spell's area of effect that even glances at the fire must make a [[Saving Throw\|Saving Throw]] vs spells or be entranced by the dancing flames. Entranced creatures do not take any actions.

While entranced, the creature becomes susceptible to the caster's words, in the same manner as with a [[Suggestion\|Suggestion]] spell. A second saving throw is allowed against the suggestion, with a penalty of -3 to the second saving throw. The second saving throw is only against the suggestion effect; success does not free the creature from staring into the flames for the spell's duration.

The caster can make a factual suggestion, suggest a course of action, or make a combination of both. Even a victim who has failed the second saving throw and believes the suggestion will not undertake a course of action that is unreasonable. However, the caster can phrase requests in such a way as to avoid this problem.

The creature to be influenced must be able to hear and understand the language the caster speaks.

The enchantment will be broken if the entranced creature can no longer see the flames or if it is attacked.
