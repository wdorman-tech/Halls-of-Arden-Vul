## Stats

- **Cost:** 2 gp per dozen
- **Weight:** 4 lb per dozen
- **Inventory Slots:** 1 per [[Quiver\|Quiver]] (12 or 24 capacity)

## Notes

Standard ammunition for the [[Long Bow\|Long Bow]], [[Short Bow\|Short Bow]], [[Composite Long Bow\|Composite Long Bow]], and [[Composite Short Bow\|Composite Short Bow]]. Sold by the dozen.

Arrows used in combat may be lost or broken — recover what you can after a fight.
