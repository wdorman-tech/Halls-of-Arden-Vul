## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Illusionist\|Illusionist]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Phantasmal Transmutation\|Phantasmal Transmutation]] / [[Arcane Transmutation\|Arcane Transmutation]]
- **Range:** 40ft + 10ft per caster level
- **Duration:** 2 rounds per caster level
- **Area of Effect:** See below
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

This spell creates small lights at a chosen distance, under the caster's control. The caster may create 1-4 (at their option) torchlike lights, 1-4 (at their option) spherical lights, or a single, faintly human-like glow.

## Details

The lights move at the caster's mental command, without any particular [[Concentration\|Concentration]] required.

If the caster allows the lights to move beyond the spell's range, the lights will disappear and the spell will end.
