## Notes

[[Detect Evil (Paladin)\|Detect Evil (Paladin)]] is a [[Paladin\|Paladin]] class ability — the paladin can cast the first-level divine spell [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]] at will, without using a spell slot, and with no daily limit. This is distinct from the cleric/divine spell [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]], which is the underlying effect.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Paladin\|Paladin]]
- **Level Required:** 1st (immediate)
- **Mechanics:** Cast the 1st-level divine spell [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]]
- **Spell Slot:** Not required — innate class ability
- **Frequency:** No limit — at will
- **See:** [[Detect Evil\|Detect Evil]] for the underlying spell mechanics (range, duration, components, etc.)
