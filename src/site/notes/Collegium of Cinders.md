## Notes

The Collegium of Cinders trains elementalists and battle mages, with strong ties to the Archontean military. A Cinders graduate is often part of an imperial legion, deployed to the front lines as living artillery. They are the most martial of the Seven.

## Key Facts

- **Specialties:** Elementalism, battle magic
- **Reputation:** Disciplined; respected/feared depending on which side you're on
- **Membership profile:** Battle mages, military officers
- **Ties:** [[Archontean Empire\|Archontean Empire]] military
- **Schools of magic favored:** Elemental, evocation, war magic
