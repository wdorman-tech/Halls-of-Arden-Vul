## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Cleric\|Cleric]] / [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 3rd (Cleric) / 4th (Magic-User)
- **School:** [[Divine Conjuration\|Divine Conjuration]] / [[Arcane Abjuration\|Arcane Abjuration]]
- **Range:** Touch
- **Duration:** Instantaneous (Cleric) / Permanent (Magic-User)
- **Area of Effect:** 1 creature
- **Components:** V, S
- **Casting Time:** 6 segments (Cleric) / 4 segments (Magic-User)
- **Saving Throw:** Yes (negates effect)

## Notes

This spell places a curse on the targeted creature.

## Details

A creature can only be affected by one instance of this spell at a time.

Some good-aligned religions ban their clerics from casting this spell. Others allow their clerics to cast it only at times of great need.

When this spell takes effect, roll 1d8 to see which curse is bestowed on the target creature.

| Roll | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 1-4 | One of the creature's ability scores is reduced to 3. |
| 5-6 | Affected creature must take a penalty of -4 on its attack rolls and saving throws. |
| 7-8 | Affected creature becomes clumsy. At the start of each round, there's a 50% chance it'll drop each object that it's holding. If it isn't holding each object, then at the start of each round, there's a 50% chance that it can't act that round. |

The curse remains until it is removed by [[Remove Curse\|Remove Curse]], dispelled, or revoked by the caster.

**Optional rule:** Clerics of certain religions might be able to learn alternative curses. These curses might be cult secrets, lost secrets, or long-lost secrets. If this rule is in play, then the GM decides what alternative curses can be learned and what their effects might be.

## Reversed Form

This is the reverse of [[Remove Curse\|Remove Curse]].
