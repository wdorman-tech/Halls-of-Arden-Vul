## Spell Stats

- **Class:** [[Magic-User\|Magic-User]]
- **Level:** 1st
- **School:** [[Arcane Conjuration\|Arcane Conjuration]] (Conjuration/Summoning)
- **Range:** Caster
- **Duration:** 6 turns + 1 turn per MU level
- **Area of Effect:** 30ft radius around caster
- **Components:** V, S, M
- **Casting Time:** Fast
- **Saving Throw:** None

## Notes

By means of this spell, the caster summons a mindless magical force to perform simple tasks. It can fetch and carry things, open doors, hold chairs, clean, mend, etc. The unseen servant continues at its assigned task until it is given another command.

## Details

An unseen servant cannot exert force (or carry weight) of more than twenty pounds.

Unseen servants cannot attack.

The unseen servant cannot move beyond the spell's area of effect, which moves with the caster.
