## Notes

Diktynna is among [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]], the chaotic neutral goddess of hunting, the chase in all its forms, and the moon. Her cult includes literal hunters and the metaphorical kind — pursuers of any quarry. Her symbols are a horn and a brown satchel; her weapons are bows and hunting spears.

- **Pantheon:** [[The Seventeen\|The Seventeen]]

- **Weapon(s):** Bows, hunting spears
