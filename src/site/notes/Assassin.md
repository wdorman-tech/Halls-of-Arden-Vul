## Notes

The [[Assassin\|Assassin]] is a sub-class of [[Thief\|Thief]] specialising in murder-for-hire and infiltration. Driven by [[Strength\|Strength]], [[Dexterity\|Dexterity]], and [[Intelligence\|Intelligence]], they combine thief skills with [[Disguise\|Disguise]], [[Assassinate\|Assassination]], and the eventual ability to brew [[Poison\|Poison]]. All assassins are evil and many operate within the structure of an [[Assassins' Guild\|Assassins' Guild]].

## Key Facts

- **Minimum Scores:** STR 12, DEX 12, CON 6, INT 11, WIS 6
- **Prime Requisite:** None (no XP bonus)
- **Hit Die:** d6 (max 15)
- **Alignment:** Any evil
- **Armour:** Leather or studded leather only; shields allowed
- **Weapons:** Any
- **Weapon Proficiencies:** 3 (additional at level 4, 8, and 12)
- **Non-Proficiency Penalty:** -3 to hit
- **Weapon Specialisation:** No
- **Initial Gold:** 2d6 x 10
- **Allowed Ancestries:** [[Human\|Human]], [[Half-Elf\|Half-Elf]], [[Half-Orc\|Half-Orc]] (per ancestry rules)
- **Multi-class options:** See [[Multi-Classing\|Multi-Classing]] (note campaign houserule)
- **Saving Throw Categories:** Levels 1-4, 5-8, 9-12, 13-15
- **Class Abilities:** [[Assassinate\|Assassinate]], [[Backstab\|Backstab]], [[Disguise\|Disguise]], thief skills (see [[Thief\|Thief]])
- **Higher-Level Abilities:** Lvl 4 — Hire Assassins; Lvl 8 — Hire Thieves; Lvl 9 — Special Languages, Brew Poison; Lvl 12 — Hire Any Henchmen, Read Scrolls; Lvl 14 — Guildmaster; Lvl 15 — Lord of Assassins
- **XP Milestones:** Level 2 at 1,500 XP; Level 4 at 6,000 XP (Hire Assassins); Level 8 at 100,000 XP (Master Assassin); Level 14 at 1,000,000 XP (Guildmaster); Level 15 at 1,500,000 XP (XP cap)
- **Stronghold/Followers:** [[Assassins' Guild\|Assassins' Guild]] at level 14, won by killing the existing guildmaster
