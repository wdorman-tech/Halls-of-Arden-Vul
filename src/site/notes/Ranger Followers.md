## Notes

At 9th level the [[Ranger\|Ranger]] may attract a band of followers — and the player chooses between a Band of Merry Folk (mortal companions) and a Band of Magical Creatures. Followers are optional; some rangers prefer to stay solitary. Followers are independent of whether the ranger builds a stronghold.

## Key Facts

- **Class:** [[Ranger\|Ranger]]
- **Level Required:** 9th
- **Choice (player's option):**
    - Band of Merry Folk
    - Band of Magical Creatures
- **Specifics:** As described in the OSRIC Game Master's Guide
- **Optional:** Rangers may decline followers and continue solo

### Earlier Restriction
- Rangers cannot employ hirelings or henchmen until they reach 8th level (one level before they may attract a band)

### Stronghold
- A ranger may also build a stronghold at level 9 if they have land, or may continue an outdoor lifestyle without a fixed base. Followers are independent of stronghold status.
